Title: Okenia elegans
Aspects: {'O. elegans'}

Okenia elegans, the yellow skirt slug, is a species of a sea slug, specifically a dorid nudibranch, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Goniodorididae. It is the type species of its genus.


== Distribution ==
This species was described from near Cette on the Mediterranean Sea coast of France. It is reported from the adjacent Atlantic Ocean as far north as Scotland.
