Title: Jomercocha (Camanti)
Aspects: {"Q'umir Qucha (disambiguation)"}

Jomercocha (possibly from Quechua q'umir green, qucha lake, "green lake") is a lake in Peru located in the Cusco Region, Quispicanchi Province, Camanti District. It is situated at a height of about 3,900 metres (12,795 ft). Jomercocha lies north of the mountain Vizcachani, near the Ajocunca.
