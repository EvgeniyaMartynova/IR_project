Title: Parachanna africana
Aspects: {'P. africana'}

Parachanna africana (African snakehead or Niger snakehead) is a species of fish from west-central Africa. Little is published on its biology. Limited primarily to coastal sections of rivers, it is thought to be a nest-building, thrust predator like other Channidae.Its native range is southern Benin to southern Nigeria, primarily the Oueme River and the basin of the Niger River.
