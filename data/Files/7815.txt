Title: Phoenicolacerta laevis
Aspects: {'L. laevis'}

Phoenicolacerta laevis, the Lebanon lizard, is a species of lizard in the Lacertidae family.
It is found in Cyprus, Israel, Jordan, Lebanon, Syria, and Turkey.
Its natural habitats are temperate forests, Mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation, rocky areas, arable land, pastureland, plantations, and rural gardens.
It is threatened by habitat loss.
