Title: Zabiele, Mława County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Strzegowo, within Mława County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Strzegowo, 28 km (17 mi) south of Mława, and 86 km (53 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
