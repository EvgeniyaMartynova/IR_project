Title: Oxymeris dillwynii
Aspects: {'T. concinna'}

Oxymeris dillwynii is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Terebridae, the auger snails.


== Description ==
The shell size between 15 mm and 85 mm.


== Distribution ==
This species is distributed in the Atlantic Ocean along West Africa, the Gulf of Guinea, Senegal and Cape Verdes.
