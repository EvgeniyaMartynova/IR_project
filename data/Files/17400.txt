Title: Zagacie, Lesser Poland Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagacie'}

Zagacie [zaˈɡat͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czernichów, within Kraków County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Czernichów and 19 km (12 mi) west of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 721.
