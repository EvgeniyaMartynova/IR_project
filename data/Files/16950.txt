Title: Zafaran, Kermanshah
Aspects: {"Za'faran", 'Zafaran'}

Zafaran (Persian: زعفران‎, also Romanized as Za‘farān) is a village in Howmeh-ye Sarpol Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,262, in 470 families.
