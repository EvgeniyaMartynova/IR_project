Title: Trevor Something
Aspects: {'D-Boy'}

Trevor Something is the alias of an anonymous American musician known for creating synthwave music. He creates both original music as well as remixed music through the sampling and mixing of music by other artists.


== Recording career ==
Trevor Something's first single, "All Night", was released on September 11, 2013. His first LP, entitled Synthetic Love, was released on January 28, 2014. It was followed by the mixtape Trevor Something Does Not Exist and two EPs, Distorted Reality and Lost Memories later in 2014, the Death Dream LP in 2015, and the Soulless Computer Boy and the Eternal Render LP in 2016. On May 19, 2017, his fourth LP, Die With You was released, featuring a 2005 illustration "The Last Embrace" by artist Laurie Lipton as its cover art. The latest EP from Trevor Something, Lost Love was released on June 21, 2018. It was followed by his fifth and LP, ULTRAPARANOIA on October 12, 2018. His 6th and most current LP, titled "Bots Don't Cry", was released February 15, 2019.
