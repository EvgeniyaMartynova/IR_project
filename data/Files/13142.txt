Title: Tillandsia gardneri
Aspects: {'T. gardneri'}

Tillandsia gardneri is a species in the genus Tillandsia. This species is native to Trinidad & Tobago, Colombia, eastern Brazil (as far south as Rio Grande do Sul) and Venezuela.


== Cultivars ==
