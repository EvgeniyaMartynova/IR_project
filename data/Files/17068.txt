Title: Żabka, Opole Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabka'}

Żabka [ˈʐapka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Namysłów, within Namysłów County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.


== History ==
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
