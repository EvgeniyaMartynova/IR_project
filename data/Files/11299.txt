Title: Qaidan, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari
Aspects: {'Qaidan'}

Qaidan (Persian: قائيدان‎, also Romanized as Qā’īdān; also known as Qā’īdān Shīāsī) is a village in Miankuh Rural District, Miankuh District, Ardal County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 276, in 58 families.
