Title: Ząbrowo, Świdwin County
Aspects: {'Ząbrowo'}

Ząbrowo [zɔmˈbrɔvɔ] (German: Semerow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świdwin, within Świdwin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) north-west of Świdwin and 82 km (51 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
