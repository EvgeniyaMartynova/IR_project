Title: Spotted gurnard
Aspects: {'P. picta'}

The spotted gurnard, Pterygotrigla picta, is a searobin of the family Triglidae, found in the Indo-Pacific oceans at depths between 200 and 500 m.  Its length is up to 35 cm.
