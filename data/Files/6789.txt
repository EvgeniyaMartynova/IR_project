Title: Jabalkandi, Urmia
Aspects: {'Jabal Kandi'}

Jabalkandi (Persian: جبل كندي‎, also Romanized as Jabalkandī) is a village in Torkaman Rural District, in the Central District of Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 404, in 119 families.
