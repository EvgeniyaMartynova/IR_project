Title: Wabash Township, Fountain County, Indiana
Aspects: {'Wabash Township, Indiana'}

Wabash Township is one of eleven townships in Fountain County, Indiana. As of the 2010 census, its population was 783 and it contained 336 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 33.21 square miles (86.0 km2), of which 32.96 square miles (85.4 km2) (or 99.25%) is land and 0.25 square miles (0.65 km2) (or 0.75%) is water.
