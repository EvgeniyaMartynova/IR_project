Title: Anahim Lake Airport
Aspects: {'YAA'}

Anahim Lake Airport, (IATA: YAA, TC LID: CAJ4), is located 1 nautical mile (1.9 km; 1.2 mi) south of Anahim Lake, British Columbia, Canada. It is a year-round airport serving the West Chilcotin area, operated by Cariboo Regional District.The airport has a 4,642 ft (1,415 m) paved strip and airport services for those who own or operate their own aircraft.
