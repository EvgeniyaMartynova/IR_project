Żabiniec [ʐaˈbiɲet͡s] (German: Fabianswalde) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kluczbork, within Kluczbork County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-west of Kluczbork and 35 km (22 mi) north-east of the regional capital Opole.
The village has a population of 265.
