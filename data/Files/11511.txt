Title: Qaleh-ye Mirzai, Kazerun
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Mirzai'}

Qaleh-ye Mirzai (Persian: قلعه ميرزايي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Mīrzā’ī; also known as Qal‘eh-ye Mīrzā) is a village in Famur Rural District, Jereh and Baladeh District, Kazerun County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 62, in 14 families.
