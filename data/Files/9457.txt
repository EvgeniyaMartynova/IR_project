Title: O Guaraní
Aspects: {'O Guarani'}

O Guaraní is a 1926 Brazilian drama film directed by Vittorio Capellaro based on a novel The Guarani by José de Alencar.
The film premiered in Rio de Janeiro on October 18, 1926.
