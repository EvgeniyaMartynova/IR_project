Title: Qalehgah-e Gudarz
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gah'}

Qalehgah-e Gudarz (Persian: قلعه گاه گودرز‎, also Romanized as Qal‘ehgāh-e Gūdarz; also known as Qal‘ehgāh) is a village in Sara Rural District, in the Central District of Saqqez County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 426, in 86 families.
