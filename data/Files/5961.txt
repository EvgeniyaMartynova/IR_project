Title: I Get Around (Adelitas Way song)
Aspects: {'I Get Around (disambiguation)'}

"I Get Around" is a song from the American hard rock band Adelitas Way, released on February 12, 2015. It is the first single from the band's EP Deserve This EP.


== Release ==
The song was first released exclusively on the band's Pledge Music Campaign for digital download along with the track "Deserve This"
