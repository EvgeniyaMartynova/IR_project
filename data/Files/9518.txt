Title: Sulawesian puddle frog
Aspects: {'O. celebensis'}

The Sulawesian puddle frog or Celebes Oriental frog (Occidozyga celebensis) is a species of frog in the Dicroglossidae family. It is endemic to Sulawesi, Indonesia.This abundant species lives in paddy fields and other disturbed habitats. It breeds in paddy fields and slow-moving streams. As a widespread, abundant and adaptable species, it is not threatened.
