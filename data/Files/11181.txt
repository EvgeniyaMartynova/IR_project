Title: Mohammed Qabel
Aspects: {'Qabil'}

Mohammed Qabel Mahdi (Arabic: محمد قابل‎) (born 9 January 1988 in Iraq) is an Iraqi football defender. He currently plays for the Karbalaa FC football club in Iraq.
