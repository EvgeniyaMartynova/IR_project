Title: Qaleh Joqeh, Divandarreh
Aspects: {'Qaleh Joqeh'}

Qaleh Joqeh (Persian: قلعه جقه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Joqeh; also known as Qal‘eh Jūqeh) is a village in Obatu Rural District, Karaftu District, Divandarreh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 497, in 108 families.
