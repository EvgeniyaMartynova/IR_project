Title: I Like It (Dino song)
Aspects: {'I Like It'}

"I Like It"  is a song recorded by the  singer Dino. It was released in 1989 as the third single from his album 24/7. It peaked at #7 on the Billboard Hot 100 and #3 on the Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart in the United States. In Canada, the song reached the top 30 of the 100 most played tracks, and reached No. 1 on the dance music chart.


== Music video ==
The music video was directed by Jim Hershleder and premiered on MTV & VH-1 in 1989.
