Title: Żabianka, Lublin Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabianka (disambiguation)'}

Żabianka [ʐaˈbjaŋka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ułęż, within Ryki County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Ułęż, 13 km (8 mi) south-east of Ryki, and 51 km (32 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.The village has a population of 134.
