Title: I Megaliteres Epitihies (Marinella album)
Aspects: {'I Megaliteres Epitihies'}

Marinella - I megaliteres epitihies tis (Greek: Μαρινέλλα - Οι μεγαλύτερες επιτυχίες της; English: Marinella - Her greatest hits) is a compilation of recordings by popular Greek singer Marinella, under the Minos EMI series I Megaliteres Epitihies (Greatest hits). This album is part of the compilation. It was released in November, 2001 in Greece and includes 16 studio recordings by Marinella from 1988 - 1995 for the Minos EMI.
