Title: A Letter from Home (film)
Aspects: {'A Letter from Home'}

A Letter from Home is a 1941 short documentary film directed by Carol Reed. It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Documentary Short.


== Cast ==
Joyce Grenfell as American Mother
Kathleen Harrison as The Maid
Celia Johnson as English Mother
Edie Martin as Bespectacled shopper
