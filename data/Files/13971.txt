U-23 may refer to one of the following German submarines:

SM U-23 (Germany), was the lead ship of the Type U 23 class of submarines; launched in 1913 and served in the First World War until sunk on 20 July 1915

SM UB-23, a Type UB II submarine launched in 1915 and interned in Spain 29 July 1917
SM UC-23, a Type UC II submarine launched in 1916 and surrendered on 25 November 1918
German submarine U-23 (1936), a Type IIB submarine that served in the Second World War until scuttled on 10 September 1944
