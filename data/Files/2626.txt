Title: Bottineau Municipal Airport
Aspects: {'D9'}

Bottineau Municipal Airport (FAA LID: D09) is a public airport located one mile (1.6 km) east of the central business district of Bottineau, in Bottineau County, North Dakota, United States. It is owned by the Bottineau Airport Authority.


== Facilities and aircraft ==
Bottineau Municipal Airport covers an area of 188 acres (76 ha) which contains two runways:
Runway 13/31: 3,700 x 60 ft (1,128 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt
Runway 3/21: 2,000 x 150 ft (610 x 46 m), Surface: TurfFor the 12-month period ending July 23, 2007, the airport had 4,720 aircraft operations: 93% general aviation, 5% air taxi, and 2% military.
