Title: Kabataş, Beyoğlu
Aspects: {'Kabataş'}

Kabataş is a quarter of Beyoğlu municipality (belediye) in Istanbul, Turkey. It is situated on the European shore of the Bosphorus, between Beşiktaş and Karaköy. Public transport connections include a funicular to Taksim Square, the T1 tram line and ferries.


== Historical and famous places ==
The historical Dolmabahçe Mosque and the İnönü Stadium of Beşiktaş J.K. are located at Kabataş.
