Title: Zabari-ye Yek
Aspects: {'Zabari, Iran'}

Zabari-ye Yek (Persian: زباري يك‎, also Romanized as Zabārī-ye Yek; also known as Shaikh Zibāri, Sheykh Zabārī, Sheykh Zebārī, Sheykh Zīārī, Zabārī, and Zobārī) is a village in Miyan Ab Rural District, in the Central District of Shushtar County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 174, in 30 families.
