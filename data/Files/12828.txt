Title: Sam Russell (footballer, born 1900)
Aspects: {'S. R. Russell'}

Samuel R. Russell (born 2 January 1900, date of death unknown) was an Irish footballer. He was born in Downpatrick, Ireland. During his career he had spells with Derry City, Newcastle United and Bradford City. He also represented the Ireland national team on three occasions between 1929 and 1931.


== Personal life ==
Russell served in the Royal Irish Fusiliers during the First World War.
