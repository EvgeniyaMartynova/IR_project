Title: Naticopsis
Aspects: {'N. laeta'}

Naticopsis is an extinct genus of small sea snails belonging to the family Neritopsidae.


== Fossil records ==
This genus is known in the fossil record from the Permian to the Triassic periods (from about 412.3 to 242.0 million years ago). Fossils of species within this genus have been found in China, United States, Europe, Cambodia, Iran, Japan, Malaysia and Pakistan.
