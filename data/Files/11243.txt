Title: Qaemiyeh, Rafsanjan
Aspects: {'Qaemiyeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaemiyeh (Persian: قائميه‎, also Romanized as Qā’emīyeh) is a village in Ferdows Rural District, Ferdows District, Rafsanjan County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 186, in 48 families.
