Title: Żabin, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabin'}

Żabin [ˈʐabin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Niechlów, within Góra County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Niechlów, 14 km (9 mi) west of Góra, and 77 km (48 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
