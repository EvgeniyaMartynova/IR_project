Title: Yadegar, Razavi Khorasan
Aspects: {'Yadegar'}

Yadegar (Persian: يادگار‎, also Romanized as Yādegār; also known as Kozma and Yadgah) is a village in Dowlatkhaneh Rural District, Bajgiran District, Quchan County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 440, in 125 families.
