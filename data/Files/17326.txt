Title: Zadonbeh-ye Bala
Aspects: {'Zadonbeh'}

Zadonbeh-ye Bala (Persian: زادنبه بالا‎, also Romanized as Zādonbeh-ye Bālā) is a village in Barakuh Rural District, Jolgeh-e Mazhan District, Khusf County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 30, in 14 families.
