Title: Safety (album)
Aspects: {"I Don't Mind"}

Safety is the third solo album by King's X guitarist Ty Tabor, released in 2002. All songs were written, recorded, and mastered by Ty Tabor at Alien Beans Studios in Katy, TX.

== Personnel ==
Ty Tabor - vocals, all guitars, bass, and loop programming.
Jerry Gaskill - drums on tracks 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 9, and 10.
Christian Nesmith - backing vocals on tracks 7 and 10.
Wally Farkas - "weird guitars" and solo on track 10
