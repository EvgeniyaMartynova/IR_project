Title: Qaleh Sefid-e Olya, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sefid-e Olya'}

Qaleh Sefid-e Olya (Persian: قلعه سفيدعليا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sefīd-e ‘Olyā; also known as Qal‘eh Sefīd, Qal‘eh-ye Safīd, and Qal‘eh-ye Sefīd) is a village in Margha Rural District, in the Central District of Izeh County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. In the 2006 census, its population was 68 persons, in 15 families.
