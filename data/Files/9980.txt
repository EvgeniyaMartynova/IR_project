Title: Oak Creek Township, Saunders County, Nebraska
Aspects: {'Oak Creek Township, Nebraska'}

Oak Creek Township is one of twenty-four townships in Saunders County, Nebraska, United States. The population was 949 at the 2000 census, 40.8% of whom were of Czech ancestry, the second highest percentage of Czech-Americans in the United States. A 2006 estimate placed the township's population at 988.The Village of Valparaiso lies within the township.
