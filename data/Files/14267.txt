Title: V. Gopalakrishnan (mayor)
Aspects: {'V. Gopalakrishnan'}

V. Gopalakrishnan of Tamil Maanila Congress is the first mayor of Coimbatore city, Tamil Nadu.


== Mayor of Coimbatore ==
He was elected as the mayor of the Coimbatore city in 1996. He was the mayor of the city from 1996-2001.
