Title: Qaleh Cheh, Golestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Cheh'}

Qaleh Cheh (Persian: قلعه چه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Cheh) is a village in Chehel Chay Rural District, in the Central District of Minudasht County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 78, in 23 families.
