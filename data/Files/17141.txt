Title: Zaborowo, Gmina Janowiec Kościelny
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Janowiec Kościelny, within Nidzica County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Janowiec Kościelny, 17 km (11 mi) south-east of Nidzica, and 60 km (37 mi) south of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 160.
