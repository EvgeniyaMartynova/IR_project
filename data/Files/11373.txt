Title: Qaleh Bin Yurdi
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bin'}

Qaleh Bin Yurdi (Persian: قلعه بين يوردي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Bīn Yūrdī) is a village in Lisar Rural District, Kargan Rud District, Talesh County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
