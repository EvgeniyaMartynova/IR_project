Title: Nanobagrus armatus
Aspects: {'N. armatus'}

Nanobagrus armatus is a species of bagrid catfish found in the Mahakam and Kapuas River basins of Borneo.This species grows to a length of 3.2 cm and has a brown body with numerous cream-colored spots and 2 relatively large and irregular cream-colored patches on the sides of the body.
