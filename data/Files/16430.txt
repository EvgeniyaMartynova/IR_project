Title: Yahmureh-ye Yek
Aspects: {'Yahmureh'}

Yahmureh-ye Yek (Persian: يحموره يك‎, also Romanized as Yaḩmūreh-ye Yek; also known as Yaḩmūreh and Yoḩmūreh) is a village in Shoaybiyeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Shadravan District, Shushtar County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 369, in 67 families.
