Title: Robbins's tateril
Aspects: {'T. arenarius'}

Robbins's tateril or the Sahel gerbil (Taterillus arenarius) is a species of gerbil found only in Mauritania. Its natural habitats are dry savanna and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.
