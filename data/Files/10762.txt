Title: Ivory-breasted pitta
Aspects: {'P. maxima'}

The ivory-breasted pitta (Pitta maxima) is a species of bird in the family Pittidae. It is endemic to North Maluku in Indonesia. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests.


== Subspecies ==
Two subspecies are recognized:

Pitta maxima morotaiensis, found on the island of Morotai
Pitta maxima maxima, found on the islands of Halmahera, Bacan, Kasiruta and Obi.
