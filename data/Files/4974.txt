Title: Grey sibia
Aspects: {'H. gracilis'}

The grey sibia (Heterophasia gracilis) is a species of bird in the Leiothrichidae family.
It is found in China, India, and Myanmar. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.
