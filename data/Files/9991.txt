Title: Oak Glen, New Jersey
Aspects: {'Oak Glen'}

Oak Glen is an unincorporated community located within Howell Township in Monmouth County, New Jersey, United States. The area consists of mostly forest with some single-family homes along the four roads that run through the area: Oak Glen Road, Old Tavern Road, Aldrich Road, and Windeler Road. The settlement is located between Interstate 195 and Manasquan Reservoir County Park (whose main entrance is on Windeler Road).
