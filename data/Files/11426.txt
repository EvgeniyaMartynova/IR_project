Title: Qaleh Madreseh, Behbahan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Madreseh'}

Qaleh Madreseh (Persian: قلعه مدرسه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Madreseh) is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Behbahan County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 201, in 45 families.
