Title: Ząbki, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Ząbki (disambiguation)'}

Ząbki [ˈzɔmpki] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Białogard, within Białogard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Białogard and 118 km (73 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
