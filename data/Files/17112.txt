Title: Żabno, Chojnice County
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brusy, within Chojnice County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Brusy, 21 km (13 mi) north-east of Chojnice, and 83 km (52 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 227.
