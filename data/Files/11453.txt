Title: Qaleh Sangi, Razavi Khorasan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sangi'}

Qaleh Sangi (Persian: قلعه سنگي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sangī) is a village in Balaband Rural District, in the Central District of Fariman County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 474, in 104 families.
