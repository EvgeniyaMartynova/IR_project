Title: Ddmasar
Aspects: {'Kabakhtapa'}

Ddmasar (Armenian: Դդմասար), formerly known as Ghabaghtapa and Huseyin Gheshlagh, is a village in the Aragatsotn Province of Armenia. The town is mostly populated by Yazidis.
