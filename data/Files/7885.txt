Title: Namibiana occidentalis
Aspects: {'L. occidentalis'}

For the western threadsnake from southwestern United States and northern Mexico, see Rena humilis
For the western worm snake from the United States, see Carphophis vermis

Namibiana occidentalis , also known as the western threadsnake or western worm snake, is a species of snakes in the family Leptotyphlopidae. It is found in Namibia and northwestern South Africa.
