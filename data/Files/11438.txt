Title: Qaleh Qazi-ye Sofla
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh Qazi-ye Sofla (Persian: قلعه قاضي سفلي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Qāẕī-ye Soflá) is a village in Gurani Rural District, Gahvareh District, Dalahu County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 116, in 22 families.
