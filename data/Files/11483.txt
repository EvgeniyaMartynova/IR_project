Title: Farahabad, Kashmar
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Bala'}

Farahabad (Persian: فرح اباد‎, also Romanized as Faraḩābād; also known as Qal‘eh-ye Bālā) is a village in Bala Velayat Rural District, in the Central District of Kashmar County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,376, in 620 families.
