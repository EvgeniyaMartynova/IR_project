Title: Xaçmaz, Oghuz
Aspects: {'Xaçmaz'}

Xaçmaz (also, Khachmas and Khachmaz) is a village and the most populous municipality, except for the capital Oğuz, in the Oghuz Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 6,045.  The municipality consists of the villages of Xaçmaz and Abdallı.


== Notable natives ==
Koroghlu Rahimov — National Hero of Azerbaijan.
