Title: Zabornia, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabornia'}

Zabornia [zaˈbɔrɲa] (German: Sabor) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Głogów, within Głogów County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 7 km (4 mi) east of Głogów, and 86 km (53 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has an approximate population of 80.
