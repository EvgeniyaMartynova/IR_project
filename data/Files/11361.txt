Title: Qaleh Abdollah, Isfahan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Abdollah'}

Qaleh Abdollah (Persian: قلعه عبداله‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh ‘Abdollāh and Qal‘eh-ye ‘Abdollāh; also known as Qal‘eh Abdullah) is a village in Emamzadeh Abdol Aziz Rural District, Jolgeh District, Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 188, in 47 families.
