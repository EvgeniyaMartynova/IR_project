Title: Qaleh Qazi, Hormozgan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh Qazi (Persian: قلعه قاضي‎, also Romanized as Qal’eh Qāẕī, Qal‘eh-i-Qāzi, Qal’eh Qāzī, and Qal‘eh-ye Qāzī; also known as Qal’eh Qāzī-ye Bālā) is a city in Qaleh Qazi Rural District, Qaleh Qazi District, Bandar Abbas County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,239, in 1,000 families.
