Title: G70 Fuzhou–Yinchuan Expressway
Aspects: {'G70'}

The Fuzhou–Yinchuan Expressway (Chinese: 福州—银川高速公路), commonly referred to as the Fuyin Expressway (Chinese: 福银高速公路) is an expressway that connects the cities of Fuzhou, Fujian, China, and Yinchuan, Ningxia. It is 2,397.55 km (1,489.77 mi) in length.
The expressway is complete except for the Jiujiang Yangtze River Expressway Bridge and associated works that under construction over the Yangtze River at Jiujiang, between Jiangxi and Hubei provinces. Currently, expressway traffic is rerouted over the Jiujiang Bridge that carries local traffic on China National Highway 105. There is also a small section in southern Ningxia, between the Gansu border and Shizilu Town, Yuanzhou District, in Guyuan, still under construction.
