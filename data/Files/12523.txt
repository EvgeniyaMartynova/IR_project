Title: Synaphea floribunda
Aspects: {'S. floribunda'}

Synaphea floribunda is a shrub endemic to Western Australia.The prostrate to ascending shrub typically grows to a height of 0.1 to 0.3 metres (0 to 1 ft) and usually blooms between September and November producing yellow flowers.
It is found in the South West and Great Southern regions of Western Australia between where it grows in sandy-loamy-gravelly soils.
