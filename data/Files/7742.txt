Title: Lenzites elegans
Aspects: {'L. elegans'}

Lenzites elegans – also known as Trametes elegans and Daedalea elegans – is a common polypore and wood-decay fungus with a pantropical distribution, found on hardwood hosts in regions including eastern North America, Australia, New Zealand, and Japan. Alluding to the pore surface which often exhibits a maze-like structure, L. elegans is also referred to as white maze polypore.
