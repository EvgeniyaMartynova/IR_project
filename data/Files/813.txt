Title: Berthelinia darwini
Aspects: {'B. darwini'}

Berthelinia darwini is a species of a sea snail with a shell comprising two separate hinged pieces or valves. It is a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Juliidae.


== Distribution ==
This species is found in Darwin, Northern Territory of Australia.
The type locality for this species is harbour in Darwin, Australia, Northern Australia.
