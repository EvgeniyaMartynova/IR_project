Title: Red Lion Airport
Aspects: {'N73'}

Red Lion Airport (FAA LID: N73) is a public-use airport located 2 nautical miles (3.7 km; 2.3 mi) south of the central business district of the Vincentown section of Southampton Township, in Burlington County, New Jersey, United States. The airport is privately owned.
