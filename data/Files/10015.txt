Title: Oak Grove School District 68 (Bartonville, Illinois)
Aspects: {'Oak Grove School District', 'Oak Grove School District 68'}

Oak Grove School District 68 is a primary school district in Peoria County, Illinois.  It receives students from portions of Bartonville and rural Peoria County.As of 2010, the district has around 410 students and 50 staff.  The district has two schools, both in Bartonville: Oak Grove East for kindergarten through 5th grade, and Oak Grove West for 6th through 8th grade.District 68 feeds into Limestone Community High School District 310.
