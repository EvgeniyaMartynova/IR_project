Title: Zaborów, Grójec County
Aspects: {'Zaborów'}

Zaborów [zaˈbɔruf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Belsk Duży, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-east of Belsk Duży, 10 km (6 mi) south of Grójec, and 49 km (30 mi) south of Warsaw. There is grocery and primary school in Zaborów.
