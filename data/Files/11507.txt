Title: Qaleh Kohneh, Kani Shirin
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Kohneh'}

Qaleh Kohneh (Persian: قلعه كهنه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kohneh) is a village in Kani Shirin Rural District, Karaftu District, Divandarreh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 550, in 97 families.
