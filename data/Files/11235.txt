Title: Qaemabad, Isfahan
Aspects: {'Qaemabad'}

Qaemabad (Persian: قائم اباد‎, also Romanized as Qā’emābād and Qa’emābād; also known as Qa’emābād Shāh Yūrdī, Shahivardi, Shāh Vardī, Shāhverdī, Shāhyowrdī, and Shāh Yūrdī) is a village in Yeylaq Rural District, in the Central District of Buin va Miandasht County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,186, in 226 families.
