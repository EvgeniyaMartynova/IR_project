Title: Strioterebrum japonicum
Aspects: {'S. japonica'}

Strioterebrum japonicum is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Terebridae, the auger snails.


== Description ==
The size of an adult shell varies between 20 mm and 47 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species is found along Japan.
