Title: Zabrodzie, Greater Poland Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] (German: Gramttenbrück)  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Szydłowo, within Piła County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) north-east of Szydłowo, 14 km (9 mi) north of Piła, and 98 km (61 mi) north of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 30.
