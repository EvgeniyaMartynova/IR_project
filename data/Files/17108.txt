Title: Żabno, Podkarpackie Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radomyśl nad Sanem, within Stalowa Wola County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Radomyśl nad Sanem, 11 km (7 mi) north-west of Stalowa Wola, and 71 km (44 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszów.
