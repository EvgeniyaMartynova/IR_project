Title: Zagajów, Pińczów County
Aspects: {'Zagajów'}

Zagajów [zaˈɡajuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Michałów, within Pińczów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Michałów, 11 km (7 mi) south-west of Pińczów, and 46 km (29 mi) south of the regional capital Kielce.
