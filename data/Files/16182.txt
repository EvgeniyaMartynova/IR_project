Title: Ya'ad, Israel
Aspects: {"Ya'ad"}

Ya'ad (Hebrew: יַעַד, lit. Destiny or Goal) is a small moshav in northern Israel. Located near the city of Karmiel, it falls under the jurisdiction of Misgav Regional Council. In 2018 it had a population of 682.


== History ==
It was founded in 1974 by computer science graduates from the Technion on the land where the Arab village of Mi'ar previously existed.
