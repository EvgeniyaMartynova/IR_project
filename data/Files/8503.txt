Title: Maerua juncea
Aspects: {'M. juncea'}

Maerua juncea, commonly known as the rough-skinned bush cherry, is a plant species in the  Capparaceae family. It is native to the Afrotropics.


== Subspecies ==
The following subspecies are accepted:
M. j. subsp. crustata Wild
M. j. subsp. juncea
