Title: Qadi Kola, Qaem Shahr
Aspects: {'Qadi Kola'}

Qadi Kola (Persian: قاديكلا‎, also Romanized as Qādī Kolā) is a village in Nowkand Kola Rural District, in the Central District of Qaem Shahr County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 241, in 66 families.
