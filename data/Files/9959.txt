Title: Oxford Area High School
Aspects: {'OAHS'}

Oxford Area High School (OAHS) is the only secondary school in the Oxford Area School District. It is in Oxford in Chester County, Pennsylvania, United States. The first high school in Oxford was established in 1869 and the current building was opened in 2005. The student body consists of about 1,200 students. The previous building, heavily renovated, is now the Penn's Grove Middle School.
