Title: Anropar försvunnen
Aspects: {'X22'}

Anropar försvunnen is a song written by Bobby Ljunggren, Robert Uhlmann and Anna-Lena Högdahl, and performed by Hanna Hedlund at Melodifestivalen 2000, ending up eighth.
The single peaked at 11th position in the Swedish singles chart and on 25 March 2000, the song received a Svensktoppen test., failing to enter chart.However the song was popular in Swedish radio, receiving a lot of airplay, and among school-aged children. The song lyrics are spaceflight-related.
