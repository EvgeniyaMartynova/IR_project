Title: Qaleh Sorkh, South Khorasan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sorkh'}

Qaleh Sorkh (Persian: قلعه سرخ‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sorkh, Ghal‘eh Sorkh, and Qal‘eh Surkh) is a village in Momenabad Rural District, in the Central District of Sarbisheh County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 44, in 10 families.
