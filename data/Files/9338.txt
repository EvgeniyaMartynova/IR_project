Title: Alexandria Airport (New Jersey)
Aspects: {'N85'}

Alexandria Airport (FAA LID: N85) is a public-use airport located in Alexandria Township, New Jersey, two nautical miles (3.704 km) west of Pittstown, in Hunterdon County, New Jersey, United States. The airport is privately owned.Alexandria Airport conducted an annual balloon festival from 1989 to 1998.
