Title: I Like That (Houston song)
Aspects: {'I Like That'}

"I Like That" is a hit song by American R&B singer Houston. It is the first single off his debut album It's Already Written (2004). It features Chingy, Nate Dogg, and I-20. Released in March 2004, the track peaked at number three on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart and number 11 on the Billboard Hot 100 and the UK Singles Chart. The song also charted in the top 40 in countries like Australia, France, Italy, New Zealand,  and Switzerland.


== Music video ==
The official music video for the song was directed by Jeremy Rall.
