Title: L'Isle-Jourdain, Vienne
Aspects: {"L'Isle-Jourdain"}

L'Isle-Jourdain is a commune in the Vienne department in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region in western France.


== Notable residents ==
Louis Fabien (1924-), pseudonym of a French painter who uses a modern form of pointillism
