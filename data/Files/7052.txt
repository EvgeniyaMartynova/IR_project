Title: Kraussia floribunda
Aspects: {'K. floribunda'}

Kraussia floribunda is a species of plant in the family Rubiaceae. It ranges from Mozambique and Swaziland to eastern South Africa, and is associated with the Tongoland-Pondoland regional mosaic. The type was described from a plant collected by Dr. F. Krauss near Durban.
