Title: Żabin, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabin'}

Żabin [ˈʐabin] (formerly German Groß Sabin) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wierzchowo, within Drawsko County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south of Wierzchowo, 26 km (16 mi) south-east of Drawsko Pomorskie, and 101 km (63 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 720.
