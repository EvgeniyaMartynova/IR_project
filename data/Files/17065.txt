Title: Prądnik Biały
Aspects: {'Żabiniec'}

Prądnik Biały is one of 18 districts of Kraków, located in the northwest part of the city. The name Prądnik Biały comes from a village of same name that is now a part of the district. 
According to the Central Statistical Office data, the district's area is 23.42 square kilometres (9.04 square miles) and 69 135 people inhabit Prądnik Biały.
