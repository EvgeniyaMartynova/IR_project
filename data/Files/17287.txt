Title: Zacisze, Gorzów County
Aspects: {'Zacisze'}

Zacisze [zaˈt͡ɕiʂɛ] (German: Emmashof) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubiszyn, within Gorzów County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north of Lubiszyn and 28 km (17 mi) north-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
