Title: A Life in the Theatre (1979 film)
Aspects: {'A Life in the Theatre (disambiguation)'}

A Life in the Theatre is a 1979 American television film based on the play of the same name by David Mamet.  It stars Peter Evans and Ellis Rabb, reprising their roles from the original 1977 off-Broadway production.
