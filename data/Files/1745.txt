Title: Caulerpa dichotoma
Aspects: {'C. dichotoma'}

Caulerpa dichotoma is a species of seaweed in the Caulerpaceae family.The seaweed is grass green at the base becoming a darker green. The thallus spreads outward to about 20 centimetres (7.9 in) and has stolons that are approximately 4 millimetres (0.16 in) in diameter.
It is found along the coast in a small area of the Kimberley region of Western Australia.
