Title: Zabłocie, Siedlce County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wiśniew, within Siedlce County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Wiśniew, 16 km (10 mi) south-east of Siedlce, and 96 km (60 mi) east of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 203.
