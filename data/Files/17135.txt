Title: Zaborowo, Kolno County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stawiski, within Kolno County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Stawiski, 16 km (10 mi) south-east of Kolno, and 75 km (47 mi) west of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 98.
