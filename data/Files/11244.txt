Title: Qaemiyeh-ye Do
Aspects: {'Qaemiyeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaemiyeh-ye Do (Persian: قايميه 2‎, also Romanized as Qā’emīyeh-ye Do; also known as Kāmiāb and Qā’emīyeh) is a village in Azadegan Rural District, in the Central District of Rafsanjan County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 188, in 43 families.
