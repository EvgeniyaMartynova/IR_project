Title: Qaleh Gah-e Khalifeh
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gah'}

Qaleh Gah-e Khalifeh (Persian: قلعه گاه خليفه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gāh-e Khalīfeh and Qal‘ehgāh-e Khalīfeh) is a village in Tilakuh Rural District, Ziviyeh District, Saqqez County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
