Title: Q'ara Qullu (Ancoraimes)
Aspects: {"Q'ara Qullu (disambiguation)"}

Q'ara Qullu (Aymara q'ara bare , bald, qullu mountain, "bare mountain", also spelled Khara Kkollu) is a 4,516 m (14,816 ft) mountain in the Bolivian Andes. It is located in the La Paz Department, Omasuyos Province, Ancoraimes Municipality. Q'ara Qullu lies northwest of the village of Surijaya.
