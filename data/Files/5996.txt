Title: I Got a Girl (Tripping Daisy song)
Aspects: {'I Got a Girl'}

"I Got a Girl" is the first single from alternative rock group Tripping Daisy's second studio album, I Am an Elastic Firecracker. The song reached number six on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart, and was the band's biggest hit of their career.
