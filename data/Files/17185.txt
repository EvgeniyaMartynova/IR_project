Title: Zabrodzie, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] (German: Zweibrodt) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kąty Wrocławskie, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) east of Kąty Wrocławskie and 11 km (7 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
