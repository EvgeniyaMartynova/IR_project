Title: Qaleh-ye Khvajeh, Fars
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Khvajeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaleh-ye Khvajeh (Persian: قلعه خواجه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Khvājeh; also known as Khvājeh and Qal‘eh Khvājeh’ī) is a village in Rostaq Rural District, in the Central District of Neyriz County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 238, in 58 families.
