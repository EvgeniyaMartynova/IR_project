Title: Qaleh-ye Sangi, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sangi'}

Qaleh-ye Sangi (Persian: قلعه سنگي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Sangī; also known as Qal‘eh Sang) is a village in Dasht-e Zarrin Rural District, in the Central District of Kuhrang County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 135, in 25 families.
