Title: Jabal Kandi, Anzal
Aspects: {'Jabal Kandi'}

Jabal Kandi (Persian: جبل كندي‎, also Romanized as Jabal Kandī and Jabalkandī) is a village in Anzal-e Jonubi Rural District, Anzal District, Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 388, in 84 families.
