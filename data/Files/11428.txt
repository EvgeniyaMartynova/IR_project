Title: Qaleh Malek, East Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Malek'}

Qaleh Malek (Persian: قلعه ملك‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Malek) is a village in Owch Hacha Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 49, in 12 families.
