Title: Qalehcheh, Razavi Khorasan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Cheh'}

Qalehcheh (Persian: قلعه چه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘ehcheh; also known as Qal‘echeh) is a village in Takab Rural District, in the Central District of Dargaz County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 97, in 30 families.
