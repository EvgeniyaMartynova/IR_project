Title: Qaleh-ye Mirzai, Kavar
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Mirzai'}

Qaleh-ye Mirzai (Persian: قلعه ميرزايي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Mīrzā’ī and Qal‘eh Mīrzā’ī; also known as Ghal‘ehé Mirzai) is a village in Kavar Rural District, in the Central District of Kavar County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 564, in 124 families.
