Title: Zaborów, Podkarpackie Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zaborów'}

Zaborów [zaˈbɔruf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czudec, within Strzyżów County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Czudec, 5 km (3 mi) north-east of Strzyżów, and 19 km (12 mi) south-west of the regional capital Rzeszów.
