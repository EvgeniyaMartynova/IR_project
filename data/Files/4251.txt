Title: G. Anderson (1861 cricketer)
Aspects: {'G. Anderson'}

G. Anderson (full name and dates of death unknown) was an English first-class cricketer who played his only first-class game in 1861 for the Gentlemen of the North cricket team against the Gentlemen of the South cricket team. He totalled 21 runs with a highest score of 13 and held two catches.
