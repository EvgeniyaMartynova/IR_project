Title: Zagrebačka Televizija
Aspects: {'Z1'}

Zagrebačka Televizija (Television of Zagreb), also known as Z1 Televizija, is a local commercial television station based in capital city of Croatia, Zagreb. Television started its broadcasting in August 2004 on one channel, Z1. A notable TV talk show produced and aired on Z1 was the Nightmare Stage by Željko Malnar.
