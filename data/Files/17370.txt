Title: Khvajeh Hoseyni
Aspects: {'Zafarabad'}

Khvajeh Hoseyni (Persian: خواجه حسيني‎, also Romanized as Khvājeh Ḩoseynī; also known as Z̧afarābād) is a village in Shaban Rural District, in the Central District of Nahavand County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 40, in 11 families.
