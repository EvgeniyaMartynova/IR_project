Title: D-Block Europe
Aspects: {'D block'}

D-Block Europe (often abbreviated to DBE) is a British hip hop duo, consisting of Young Adz and Dirtbike LB, from Lewisham, London. Their name is a reference to The Lox (also known as D-Block). The duo rose to fame in 2017 after releasing numerous singles including "Large Amounts".
Their debut mixtape Any Minute Now, a collaborative project with British rapper Yxng Bane, was released in 2018 and charted at number 14 on the UK Albums Chart. Their debut solo mixtape, Home Alone, was released on 15 February 2019 and peaked at number 6 on the UK Albums Chart followed by their second mixtape of 2019, PTSD, which was their most successful release debuting at number 4 on the UK Albums Chart. They also will be touring around Europe until 12 Dec 2019 on a Thursday.
Young Adz is a British rapper, singer and songwriter from Lewisham, London
Dirtbike LB is a British rapper, singer and songwriter from Lewisham, London
