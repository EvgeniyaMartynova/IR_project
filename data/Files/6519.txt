Title: J2K-Codec
Aspects: {'J2K'}

J2K-Codec is a commercial library to decode JPEG 2000 images. Version 2.0 was released on 12 April 2011.J2K-Codec supports decoding of different resolution levels and selective tile decoding. It also supports files, produced by ADV202/ADV212 hardware chips.The library is faster than JasPer or OpenJPEG libraries and has approximately the same decoding speed as Kakadu
.
