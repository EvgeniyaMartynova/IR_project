Title: Zabiele, Lubartów County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Niedźwiada, within Lubartów County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Niedźwiada, 15 km (9 mi) north-east of Lubartów, and 35 km (22 mi) north-east of the regional capital Lublin.
