Title: Zaborów, Siedlce County
Aspects: {'Zaborów'}

Zaborów [zaˈbɔruf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przesmyki, within Siedlce County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Przesmyki, 30 km (19 mi) north-east of Siedlce, and 114 km (71 mi) east of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 96.
