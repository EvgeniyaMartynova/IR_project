Title: Splendrillia intermedia
Aspects: {'S. intermedia'}

Splendrillia intermedia is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Drilliidae.


== Description ==


== Distribution ==
This marine species occurs in the Western Indian Ocean and off New Caledonia.
