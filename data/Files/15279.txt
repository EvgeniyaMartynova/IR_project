Title: HSwMS Wachtmeister
Aspects: {'Wachtmeister (disambiguation)'}

Several ships of the Swedish Navy have been named HSwMS Wachtmeister, named after the Swedish noble family of Wachtmeister:

Wachtmeister (1681) was a ship launched in 1681 and lost in battle in 1719
HSwMS Wachtmeister (10) was a Wrangel-class destroyer launched in 1917 and decommissioned in 1947
