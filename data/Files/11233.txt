Title: Qaedi-ye Seh
Aspects: {'Qaedi'}

Qaedi-ye Seh (Persian: قائدي3‎, also Romanized as Qā’edī-ye Seh; also known as Chāh Qā’edī, Maḩalleh-ye Qā’edī, Qā’edī, Qāyed, and Qāyedī) is a village in Sigar Rural District, in the Central District of Lamerd County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 89, in 20 families.
