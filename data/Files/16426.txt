Title: Yahel
Aspects: {'Yahel (disambiguation)'}

Kibbutz Yahel (Hebrew: יַהֵל) is a Reform kibbutz in southern Israel. Located in the Arabah region of the Negev desert, 60 kilometers  from the city of Eilat, it falls under the jurisdiction of Hevel Eilot Regional Council. In 2018 it had a population of 194.


== History ==
Established in 1977, it was the first kibbutz founded by the Reform movement. The name is symbolic and means "(he) will put up a tent". It comes from the Book of Job (Job 13:20).
