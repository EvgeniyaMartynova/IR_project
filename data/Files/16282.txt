Title: Yadabad-e Sofla
Aspects: {'Yadabad'}

Yadabad-e Sofla (Persian: يادابادسفلي‎, also Romanized as Yādābād-e Soflá; also known as Yādābād) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 86, in 20 families.
