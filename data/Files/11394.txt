Title: Qaleh Gah-e Sharif
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gah'}

Qaleh Gah-e Sharif (Persian: قلعه گاه شريف‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gāh-e Sharīf and Qal‘ehgāh-e Sharīf) is a village in Tilakuh Rural District, Ziviyeh District, Saqqez County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 219, in 44 families.
