Title: Ząbrowo, Kołobrzeg County
Aspects: {'Ząbrowo'}

Ząbrowo [zɔmˈbrɔvɔ] (German: Semmerow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gościno, within Kołobrzeg County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Kołobrzeg and 102 km (63 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
