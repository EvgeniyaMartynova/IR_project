Title: Zabłocie, Podlaskie Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nurzec-Stacja, within Siemiatycze County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Nurzec-Stacja, 16 km (10 mi) north-east of Siemiatycze, and 69 km (43 mi) south of the regional capital Białystok.
