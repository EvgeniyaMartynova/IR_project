Title: Puya maculata
Aspects: {'P. maculata'}

Puya maculata is a species of plant in the family Bromeliaceae. It is endemic to Ecuador.  Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical high-altitude grassland. It is threatened by habitat loss.
