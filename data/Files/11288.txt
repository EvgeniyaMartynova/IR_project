Title: Qahab-e Jonubi Rural District
Aspects: {'Qahab (disambiguation)'}

Qahab-e Jonubi Rural District (Persian: دهستان قهاب جنوبي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 15,169, in 3,862 families.  The rural district has 21 villages.
