Title: Zachełmie, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zachełmie'}

Zachełmie [zaˈxɛu̯mjɛ] (German: Saalberg) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Podgórzyn, within Jelenia Góra County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Podgórzyn, 11 km (7 mi) south-west of Jelenia Góra, and 103 km (64 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
