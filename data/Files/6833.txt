Title: Jabbarlu, West Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Jabbarlu'}

Jabbarlu (Persian: جبارلو‎, also Romanized as Jabbārlū) is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
