Title: Conus flavus
Aspects: {'C. flavus'}

Conus flavus is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Conidae, the cone snails and their allies.Like all species within the genus Conus, these snails are predatory and venomous. They are capable of "stinging" humans, therefore live ones should be handled carefully or not at all.


== Description ==
The size of the shell varies between 45 mm and 78 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species occurs off the Philippines, New Guinea; the Solomon Islands and Fiji.
