Title: Yaft Rural District
Aspects: {'Yaft'}

Yaft Rural District (Persian: دهستان يافت‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Moradlu District, Meshgin Shahr County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,493, in 737 families. The rural district has 13 villages.
