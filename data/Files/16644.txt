Title: Yalquz Aghaj, Marand
Aspects: {'Yalquz Aghaj'}

Yalquz Aghaj (Persian: يالقوزاغاج‎, also Romanized as Yālqūz Āghāj; also known as Yalghooz Aghaj, Yalgu Agāch, Yālgūz Āghāj, Yalkyz-agach, Yālqūz Āqāch, and Yālqūz Āqāj) is a village in Koshksaray Rural District, in the Central District of Marand County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,208, in 820 families.
