Title: Qaleh-ye Qazi, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh-ye Qazi (Persian: قلعه قاضي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Qāẕī and Qal‘eh Qāzi; also known as Ghal‘eh Ghazī) is a village in Shamsabad Rural District, in the Central District of Dezful County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 181, in 45 families.
