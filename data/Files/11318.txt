Title: Qaleh-ye Dokhtar, Kerman
Aspects: {"Qal'eh Dokhtar (disambiguation)"}

Qaleh-ye Dokhtar (Persian: قلعه دختر‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Dokhtar) is a village in Sorkh Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Qaleh Ganj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 119, in 40 families.
