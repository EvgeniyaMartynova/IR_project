Title: Bahman Jan-e Sofla
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bahman'}

Bahman Jan-e Sofla (Persian: بهمن جان سفلي‎, also Romanized as Bahman Jān-e Soflá; also known as Bahmanjān-e Pā’īn) is a village in Radkan Rural District, in the Central District of Chenaran County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 93, in 22 families.
