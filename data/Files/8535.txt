Title: Solariella lacunella
Aspects: {'M. maculata'}

Solariella lacunella, common name the channelled solarielle, is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Solariellidae.


== Distribution ==
This species occurs in the Caribbean Sea, the Gulf of Mexico and the Lesser Antilles; in the Atlantic Ocean off North Carolina and Florida at depths between 18 m and 1472 m.


== Description ==
The maximum recorded shell length is 8.7 mm.


== Habitat ==
Minimum recorded depth is 18 m. Maximum recorded depth is 1472 m.
