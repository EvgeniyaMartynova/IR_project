Title: Zaborowo, Rawicz County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] (German: Bismarckseich) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jutrosin, within Rawicz County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Jutrosin, 23 km (14 mi) east of Rawicz, and 89 km (55 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
