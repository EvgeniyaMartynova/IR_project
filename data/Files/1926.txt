Title: Shiny guinea pig
Aspects: {'C. fulgida'}

The shiny guinea pig (Cavia fulgida) is a guinea pig species of southeastern South America.
The rodent is endemic to Brazil and Argentina. It is native to the Atlantic Forest ecoregions.


== Taxonomy ==
Wagler in 1831 first called the species Cavia fulgida; P. W. Lund identified a Cavia rufescens in 1841, but since Oldfield Thomas (1901) these are considered to be identical species.
