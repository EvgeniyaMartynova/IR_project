Title: Jitender Mehra
Aspects: {'J. Mehra'}

Jitender Mehra was an Indian cricketer who played for Himachal Pradesh.
Mehra made a single first-class appearance for the team, during the 1996-97 season, against Jammu & Kashmir. He scored a duck in the only innings in which he batted, as Himachal Pradesh won the match by an innings margin.
Mehra took figures of 1-19 from 7 overs of bowling.
