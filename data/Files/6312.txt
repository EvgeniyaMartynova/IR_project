Title: J. M. Adams
Aspects: {'J. Adams'}

J. M. Adams of Oconto, Wisconsin was a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly.


== Biography ==
Adams was born on May 4, 1834, in Castleton, Vermont. He would graduate from Rush Medical College.


== Career ==
Adams represented Oconto County, Wisconsin and Shawano County, Wisconsin in the Assembly. He was a Democrat.
