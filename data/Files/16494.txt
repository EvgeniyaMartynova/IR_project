Title: Tombuiyeh
Aspects: {'Yakh Mur'}

Tombuiyeh (Persian: تمبوئيه‎, also Romanized as Tombū’īyeh; also known as Tomū, Tomū’īyeh, and Yakh Mūr) is a village in Bezenjan Rural District, in the Central District of Baft County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 87, in 26 families.
