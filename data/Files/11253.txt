Title: QAF FC
Aspects: {'Qaf'}

QAF FC, is a football (soccer) club from Bandar Seri Begawan, Brunei.  The club won the Brunei League in 2006 when the DPMM FC had to withdraw because they play in the Malaysian Premier League, thus the QAF FC won the title. Their captain was Riwandi Wahit


== Achievements ==
Brunei League: 32006, 2008, 2010Brunei League Cup: 22007–08, 2009Brunei Super Cup: 22007, 2008Asian Gold cup: 12011–12They recently Plays as a Brunei national football team side in the 2010 AFC Challenge Cup qualification in Sri Lanka.
