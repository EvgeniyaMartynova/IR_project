Title: Qadirabad, Iran
Aspects: {'Qadirabad, Iran (disambiguation)'}

Qadirabad (Persian: قديراباد‎, also Romanized as Qadīrābād; also known as Ghadīrābād and Qadīnābād) is a village in Radkan Rural District, in the Central District of Chenaran County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 54, in 12 families.
