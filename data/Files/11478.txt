Title: Qaleh-ye Ali, Kermanshah
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Ali'}

Qaleh-ye Ali (Persian: قلعه علي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye ‘Alī) is a village in Cham Chamal Rural District, Bisotun District, Harsin County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
