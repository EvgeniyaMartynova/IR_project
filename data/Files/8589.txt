Title: Mpanjaka montana
Aspects: {'M. montana'}

Mpanjaka montana is a moth of the family Erebidae that is found in Central Madagascar.
The male has a wingspan of 57 mm and the length of the frontwings is 28 mm. Abdomen, thorax and head are uniformely beige. Antennaes are bipectinated.
Front wings are white with a dense pattern of black scaled and 3 red lines of red spots crossing the wing. Rear wings are of yellowish-white colour.
