Title: Peridinium
Aspects: {'P. africanum'}

Peridinium is a genus of motile, marine and freshwater dinoflagellates. Their morphology is considered typical of the armoured dinoflagellates, and their form is commonly used in diagrams of a dinoflagellate's structure. Peridinium can range from 30-70 Î¼m in diameter, and has very thick thecal plates.
