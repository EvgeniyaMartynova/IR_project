Title: U.S. Music Corporation
Aspects: {'U.S. Music'}

U.S. Music Corporation is an American musical instrument company based in Buffalo Grove, Illinois, United States, a suburb of Chicago, that manufactures and distributes products worldwide. The company is currently a subsidiary of Canadian corporate group Jam Industries.


== History ==
