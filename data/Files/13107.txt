Title: Elegant worm snake
Aspects: {'T. elegans'}

The elegant worm snake (Afrotyphlops elegans) is a species of snake in the Typhlopidae family.  It was described by Wilhelm Peters in 1868. The species occurs on the island of Príncipe in São Tomé and Príncipe.
