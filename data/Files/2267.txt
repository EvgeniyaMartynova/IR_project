Title: Dolomedes elegans
Aspects: {'D. elegans'}

Dolomedes elegans Taczanowski, 1874 is a spider species in the family Pisauridae, found in French Guiana.(Note, Another species described two years after the original, initially namedDolomedes elegans L. Koch, 1876, and later named Nilus kochi is now considered as Mangromedes kochi, and is found in Queensland, Australia.)
