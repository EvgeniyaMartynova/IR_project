Title: Daphnellopsis fimbriata
Aspects: {'D. fimbriata'}

Daphnellopsis fimbriata is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Raphitomidae.


== Description ==
The shell grows to a length of 15 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species is distributed along the Philippines, New Guinea and the Loyalty Islands.
