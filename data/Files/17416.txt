Title: Zagaje, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagaje'}

Zagaje [zaˈɡajɛ] (German: Schonungshof) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Polanów, within Koszalin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
