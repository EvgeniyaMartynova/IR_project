Title: Qadamgah, East Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Qadamgah (disambiguation)'}

Qadamgah (Persian: قدمگاه‎, also Romanized as Qadamgāh; also known as Bādāmyār and Bādām Yār) is a village in Qebleh Daghi Rural District, Howmeh District, Azarshahr County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 589, in 141 families.
