Title: Q30 Television
Aspects: {'Q30'}

Q30 Television (sometimes referred to as Q30) is a student-run television station at Quinnipiac University in Hamden, Connecticut. The station provides coverage of news at Quinnipiac University, as well as Bobcat sports media coverage, and original entertainment shows.
