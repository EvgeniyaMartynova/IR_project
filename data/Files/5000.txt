Title: Heleioporus inornatus
Aspects: {'H. inornatus'}

Heleioporus inornatus, the plain frog, plains frog, or whooping frog, is a species of frog in the family Myobatrachidae. It is endemic to Australia. Its natural habitat is swamps.
