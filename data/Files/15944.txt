Title: Bearskin Lake Airport
Aspects: {'XBE'}

Bearskin Lake Airport, (IATA: XBE, TC LID: CNE3), is located 3 nautical miles (5.6 km; 3.5 mi) northwest of Bearskin Lake, Ontario, Canada.
