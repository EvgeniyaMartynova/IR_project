Title: I Don't Mind (Buzzcocks song)
Aspects: {"I Don't Mind"}

"I Don't Mind" is the third single by punk rock band Buzzcocks, released in 1978. It charted at number 55 in the UK Singles Chart. Both songs appear on Buzzcocks' debut album, Another Music in a Different Kitchen.
