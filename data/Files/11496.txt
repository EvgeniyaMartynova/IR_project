Title: Qaleh-ye Khan, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Khan'}

Qaleh-ye Khan (Persian: قلعه خان‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Khān and Qal’eh Khān; also known as Ghal‘eh Khan) is a village in Miyan Ab Rural District, in the Central District of Shushtar County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 268, in 53 families.
