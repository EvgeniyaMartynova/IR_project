Title: R37 expressway (Czech Republic)
Aspects: {'R37'}

Expressway R37 (Czech: rychlostní silnice R37) is an expressway in eastern Bohemia. Currently, it connects the most important cities in the eastern Bohemia: Hradec Králové and Pardubice.
None of these sections are signed as an expressway right now.
As of 2006, it has:

19.3 km between Hradec Králové and Pardubice (5 km 4 lanes, 14.3 km 2 lanes)
2.2 km in Pardubice
4.5 km under construction south of Pardubice
12.7 km in planning
44.2 km considered
