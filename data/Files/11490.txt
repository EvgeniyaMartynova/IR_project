Title: Qaleh Gol Ali Aqa
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Gol'}

Qaleh Gol Ali Aqa (Persian: قلعه گل علي اقا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gol ʿAlī Aqā) is a village in Posht Tang Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
