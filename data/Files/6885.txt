Title: Jabłonica, Masovian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Jabłonica'}

Jabłonica [jabwɔˈnit͡sa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wieniawa, within Przysucha County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) south of Wieniawa, 13 km (8 mi) east of Przysucha, and 97 km (60 mi) south of Warsaw.
