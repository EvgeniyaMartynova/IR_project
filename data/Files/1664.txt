Title: Cycloramphus carvalhoi
Aspects: {'C. carvalhoi'}

Cycloramphus carvalhoi is a species of frog in the Cycloramphidae family.
It is endemic to Brazil.
Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.
