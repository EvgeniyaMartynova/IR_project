Title: Waddington, California
Aspects: {'Waddington'}

Waddington is a locality in Humboldt County, California. It is located on the floodplain along the south side of the Eel River 3.25 miles (5.2 km) southwest of Fortuna, at an elevation of 13 feet (4.0 m).A post office operated at Waddington from 1891 to 1940. The name honors Alexander Waddington, local merchant.
