Title: Sabri, Iran
Aspects: {'Zabari, Iran'}

Sabri (Persian: صبري‎, also Romanized as Şabrī; also known as Ẕabarī) is a village in Kuh Hamayi Rural District, Rud Ab District, Sabzevar County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 48, in 15 families.
