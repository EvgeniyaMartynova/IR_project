Title: Żabieniec, Garwolin County
Aspects: {'Żabieniec'}

Żabieniec [ʐaˈbjɛɲɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Parysów, within Garwolin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Parysów, 6 km (4 mi) north of Garwolin, and 54 km (34 mi) south-east of Warsaw.
