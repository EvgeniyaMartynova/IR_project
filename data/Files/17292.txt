Title: Zacisze, Koszalin County
Aspects: {'Zacisze'}

Zacisze [zaˈt͡ɕiʂɛ] (German: Grünhof) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Manowo, within Koszalin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
