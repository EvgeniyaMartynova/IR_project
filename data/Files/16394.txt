Title: Yahad, Israel
Aspects: {'Yahad'}

Yahad (Hebrew: יחד‎, lit. Together) is a small kibbutz in northern Israel. Located in the Lower Galilee, it falls under the jurisdiction of Misgav Regional Council. It has a population of 35 families.
The kibbutz was founded in 1992 by a gar'in whose members were involved in Transcendental Meditation.
