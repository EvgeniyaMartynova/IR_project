Title: Zabłocie, Radomsko County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Kodrąb, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Kodrąb, 10 km (6 mi) north-east of Radomsko, and 76 km (47 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
