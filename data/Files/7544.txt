Title: Givar
Aspects: {'Kabar (disambiguation)'}

Givar (Persian: گيور‎, also Romanized as Gīvar; also known as Gabār, Gīār, and Kābār) is a village in Beyarjomand Rural District, Beyarjomand District, Shahrud County, Semnan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 120, in 50 families.
