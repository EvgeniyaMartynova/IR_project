Title: Loyens & Loeff
Aspects: {'L&L'}

Loyens & Loeff N.V. (L&L) is a prominent law firm headquartered in Rotterdam. It has offices in the Benelux, Switzerland (home market), as well as in major international financial centres. The firm has about 1.500 employees, including more than 900 tax and legal advisers.


== History ==
Loyens & Loeff was created in 2000 through a merger between the tax law firm Loyens & Volkmaars and part of the law firm Loeff Claeys Verbeke.
