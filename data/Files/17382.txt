Title: Zafarnama (Shami biography)
Aspects: {'Zafarnama'}

The Zafarnama (Persian: ظفرنامه‎, lit. Book of Victory) is a biography of Timur written by the historian Nizam ad-Din Shami. It served as the basis for a later and better-known Zafarnama by Sharaf ad-Din Ali Yazdi.
One translation by Felix Tauer was published in Prague in 1937.
