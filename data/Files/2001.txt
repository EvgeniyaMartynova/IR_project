Title: Copablepharon grandis
Aspects: {'C. grandis'}

The pale yellow dune moth (Copablepharon grandis) is a moth of the family Noctuidae. It is found from southern Alberta east to south-western Manitoba, the eastern parts of South Dakota, North Dakota and Iowa, west to California and south to southern Arizona, New Mexico and western Texas.
