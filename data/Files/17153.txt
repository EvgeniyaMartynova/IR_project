Title: Zaborze, Lublin Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zaborze'}

Zaborze [zaˈbɔʐɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Karczmiska, within Opole Lubelskie County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-east of Karczmiska, 16 km (10 mi) north-east of Opole Lubelskie, and 36 km (22 mi) west of the regional capital Lublin.
