Title: Zabłocie, Lubusz Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] (German: Sablath, 1937-1945: Raudenberg (Niederlausitz)) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jasień, within Żary County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-east of Jasień, 17 km (11 mi) north of Żary, and 32 km (20 mi) south-west of Zielona Góra.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
