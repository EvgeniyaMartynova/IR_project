Title: N67 road (Ireland)
Aspects: {'N67'}

The N67 road is a national secondary road in Ireland. It runs from Galway to Tarbert and passes though Oranmore, Clarinbridge, Kinvara, Ballyvaughan, Lisdoonvarna, Ennistymon, Lahinch, Milltown Malbay, Quilty, Doonbeg and Kilkee.
