Title: Qaleh Sorkh, Fereydunshahr
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sorkh'}

Qaleh Sorkh (Persian: قلعه سرخ‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sorkh; also known as Ghal‘eh Sorkh) is a village in Ashayer Rural District, in the Central District of Fereydunshahr County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,241 in 263 families.
