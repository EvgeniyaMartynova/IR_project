Title: Yal Rud
Aspects: {'Yalu (disambiguation)'}

Yal Rud (Persian: يالرود‎, also Romanized as Yāl Rūd; also known as Yālu) is a village in Sheykh Fazlolah-e Nuri Rural District, Baladeh District, Nur County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 153, in 47 families.Ásíyih Khánum, the wife of Bahá'u'lláh, the founder of Bahá'í Faith was born in Yal Rud. The oldest family in village is Behzadi.
