Title: Qaleh-ye Fereydun, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Fereydun'}

Qaleh-ye Fereydun (Persian: قلعه فريدون‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Fereydūn and Qal’eh Fereydūn; also known as Fereydūnābād, Qal‘eh Fereydon, and Qal‘eh-ye Fereydūnābād) is a village in Dasht-e Zarrin Rural District, in the Central District of Kuhrang County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 315, in 44 families.
