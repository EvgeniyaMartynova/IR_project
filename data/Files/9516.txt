Title: Satyrium carneum
Aspects: {'O. carnea'}

Satyrium carneum is a species of orchid endemic to southwestern Cape Province.It is a robust terrestrial up to from 370mm to 710mm tall. Leaves 2 to 4,thick and fleshy, lower 2 partly pressed onto the ground, 70mm to 230mm long, with gradual transition to the sheaths higher up.
