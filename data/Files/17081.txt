Title: Zabłocie, Kłodzko County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] (German: Krotenpfuhl) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bystrzyca Kłodzka, within Kłodzko County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north of Bystrzyca Kłodzka, 14 kilometres (9 mi) south of Kłodzko, and 94 kilometres (58 mi) south of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 180.
