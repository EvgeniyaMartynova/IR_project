Title: Triviella aperta
Aspects: {'C. carnea'}

Triviella aperta, common name the baby's toes, is a species of small sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Triviidae, the false cowries or trivias.


== Description ==


== Distribution ==
This species occurs off the south coast of South Africa.
