Title: I Dare You (song)
Aspects: {'I Dare You'}

"I Dare You" is the second single from Shinedown's second album, Us and Them. It reached number two on the US Billboard Mainstream Rock chart, has been sung on American Idol by Chris Daughtry, and was a minor hit on pop charts. The song was the secondary theme for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) Wrestlemania 22 and also used in a promo for WWE Friday Night SmackDown's move to myNetworkTV on October 3, 2008. This song was also Shinedown's first single to be played on pop stations.
