Title: KH Zagłębie Sosnowiec
Aspects: {'Zagłębie'}

KH Zagłębie Sosnowiec is an ice hockey team from Sosnowiec, Poland. They play in the Polish 1. Liga, the second level ice hockey league in Poland.
Sosnowiec was founded in 1937. They won five PLH titles in six years from 1980–1985, and one Polish 1. Liga title in 2005.


== Achievements ==
Polish champion : 1980, 1981, 1982, 1983, 1985.
Polish 1.Liga champion : 2005
