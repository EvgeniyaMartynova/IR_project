Title: Nectophrynoides minutus
Aspects: {'N. minutus'}

Nectophrynoides minutus is a species of toad in the family Bufonidae. It is endemic to Tanzania. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests. It is threatened by habitat loss.
