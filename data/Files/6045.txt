Title: 02 (Urban Zakapa album)
Aspects: {'I Hate You'}

02 is a studio album released by South Korean group Urban Zakapa. The lead single is "I Hate You" (Hangul: "니가 싫어"). "River" and "All The Same" ("똑같은 사랑 똑같은 이별") were pre-released. The album was nominated on 10th Korean Music Awards as Best R&B and Soul Album  and licensed by Avex Taiwan as Taiwan exclusive version, same as 02.
