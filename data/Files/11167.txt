Title: Qabaleh Kandi
Aspects: {'Qabaleh'}

Qabaleh Kandi (Persian: قباله كندي‎, also Romanized as Qabāleh Kandī; also known as Qabāleh) is a village in Angut-e Sharqi Rural District, Anguti District, Germi County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 149, in 36 families.
