Title: Euchromia folletii
Aspects: {'G. formosa'}

Euchromia folletii, the South African day-flying moth, is a moth of the subfamily Arctiinae. It was described by Félix Édouard Guérin-Méneville in 1832. It is found on the Comoros and Seychelles, as well as in Ethiopia, Kenya, Madagascar, Mozambique, Senegal, Sierra Leone, South Africa and Tanzania.
