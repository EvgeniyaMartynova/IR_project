Title: Zabiele, Mońki County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jaświły, within Mońki County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north of Jaświły, 22 km (14 mi) north-east of Mońki, and 52 km (32 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
