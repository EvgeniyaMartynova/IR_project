Title: Qaleh Gah, Dehgolan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gah'}

Qaleh Gah (Persian: قلعه گاه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gāh; also known as Kulāwa) is a village in Yeylan-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Dehgolan County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 114, in 20 families.
