Title: Qaleh Kohneh, Saqqez
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Kohneh'}

Qaleh Kohneh (Persian: قلعه كهنه‎, also Romanized as Qal’eh Kohneh and Qal‘eh-ye Kohneh) is a village in Saheb Rural District, Ziviyeh District, Saqqez County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 546, in 109 families.
