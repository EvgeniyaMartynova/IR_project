Title: Cattleya elongata
Aspects: {'C. elongata'}

Cattleya elongata, the Cattleya with the Elongated Stalk, is an orchid species in the genus Cattleya endemic to the campo rupestre vegetation in northeastern Brazil.Its ploidy is 2n = 80.C. elongata forms two natural hybrids :

Cattleya × tenuata (=  C. elongata × C. tenuis) (Brazil) .
Cattleya × undulata ( = C. elongata × C. schilleriana) (Brazil).
