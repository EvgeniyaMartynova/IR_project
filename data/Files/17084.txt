Title: Zabłocie, Gmina Biała Podlaska
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Biała Podlaska, within Biała Podlaska County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) west of Biała Podlaska and 94 km (58 mi) north of the regional capital Lublin.
