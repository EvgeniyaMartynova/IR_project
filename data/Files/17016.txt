Title: Żabia Wola, Białobrzegi County
Aspects: {'Żabia Wola'}

Żabia Wola [ˈʐabja ˈvɔla] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stara Błotnica, within Białobrzegi County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Stara Błotnica, 16 km (10 mi) south of Białobrzegi, and 78 km (48 mi) south of Warsaw.
