Title: Firebaugh Airport
Aspects: {'Q49'}

Firebaugh Airport (FAA LID: F34), formerly Q49, is a public airport located one mile (1.6 km) west of Firebaugh, serving Fresno County, California, USA. The airport is mostly used for general aviation.


== Facilities ==
Firebaugh Airport covers 37 acres and has one runway:

Runway 12/30: 3,102 x 60 ft (945 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt
