Title: Conus gilberti
Aspects: {'C. gilberti'}

Conus gilberti is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Conidae, the cone snails, cone shells or cones.These snails are predatory and venomous. They are capable of "stinging" humans.


== Description ==
The size of the shell attains 30 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species of cone snail occurs off Madagascar
