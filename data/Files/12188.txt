Title: R58 (South Africa)
Aspects: {'R58'}

The R58 is a provincial route in South Africa that connects Colesberg with Elliot via Aliwal North and Barkly East.


== Route ==
Towns on the R58-route include Colesberg, Norvalspont, Venterstad, Burgersdorp, Aliwal North, Lady Grey, Barkly East and Elliot.
