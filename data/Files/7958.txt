Title: French ship L'Adroit
Aspects: {"L'Adroit"}

At least three ships of the French Navy have been named L'Adroit:

French destroyer L'Adroit (1927), a L'Adroit-class destroyer launched in 1927 and sunk in 1940.
French destroyer L'Adroit (1941), a Le Hardi-class destroyer launched in 1938 as Épée, she was renamed in 1941 and scuttled in 1942
French patrol vessel L'Adroit, a Gowind-class corvette
