Title: Kafshgar Kola-ye Arateh
Aspects: {'Qadi Kola'}

Kafshgar Kola-ye Arateh (Persian: كفشگركلاارطه‎, also Romanized as Kafshgar Kolā-ye Araţeh and Kafshgar Kolā-ye Arţeh; also known as Kafshgar Kolā) is a village in Bisheh Sar Rural District, in the Central District of Qaem Shahr County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,852, in 476 families.
