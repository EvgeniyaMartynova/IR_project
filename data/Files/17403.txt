Title: Zagacie, Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagacie'}

Zagacie [zaˈɡat͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Koniecpol, within Częstochowa County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Koniecpol, 36 km (22 mi) east of Częstochowa, and 73 km (45 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 74.
