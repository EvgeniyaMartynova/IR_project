Title: Kaali (1980 film)
Aspects: {'Kaali'}

Kaali is a 1980 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by I. V. Sasi and produced by Hem-Nag. It stars Rajinikanth, Seema, Phataphat Jayalakshmi, Vijayakumar and Shubha in prominent roles. The film was simultaneously made in Telugu with Chiranjeevi replacing Vijayakumar's role. The cinematography was handled by Ashok Kumar.

== Soundtrack ==
The music was composed by Ilaiyaraaja. The film had Vairamuthu's first written song.
