Title: H. Thompson (rugby league)
Aspects: {'H. Thompson'}

H Thompson (born Sydney, Australia) was rugby league footballer in the New South Wales Rugby League (NSWRL)'s foundation season - 1908. Thompson played for the Eastern Suburbs club.
