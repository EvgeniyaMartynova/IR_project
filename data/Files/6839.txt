Title: Jabdi, Sarlahi
Aspects: {'Jabdi'}

Jabdi  is a town in Lalbandi Municipality in Sarlahi District in the Janakpur Zone of south-eastern Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 6507 people living in 1135 individual households.
