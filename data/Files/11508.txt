Title: Qaleh Kohneh, Obatu
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Kohneh'}

Qaleh Kohneh (Persian: قلعه كهنه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kohneh) is a village in Obatu Rural District, Karaftu District, Divandarreh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 545, in 114 families.
