Title: Yae (Cyrillic)
Aspects: {'Yae'}

Yae or Yæ (Ԙ ԙ; italics: Ԙ ԙ) is a letter of the Cyrillic script, a ligature of Я (Ya) and Е (E); я and е.
Yae was used in the old alphabet of the Mordvinic languages, where it  represented the sounds [jæ], like the pronunciation of ⟨ya⟩ in "yak".
