Title: Qaleh Kandi, Ahar
Aspects: {'Qaleh Kandi'}

Qaleh Kandi (Persian: قلعه كندي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kandī) is a village in Chahardangeh Rural District, Hurand District, Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 293, in 55 families.
