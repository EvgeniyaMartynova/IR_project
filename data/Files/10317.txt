Title: Pristimantis carvalhoi
Aspects: {'P. carvalhoi'}

Pristimantis carvalhoi is a species of frog in the family Craugastoridae.
It is found in  Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Ecuador, and Peru.
Its natural habitat is tropical moist lowland forests.
