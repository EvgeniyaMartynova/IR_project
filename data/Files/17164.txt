Title: Žabovřesky (České Budějovice District)
Aspects: {'Žabovřesky'}

Žabovřesky is a village and municipality (obec) in České Budějovice District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 11.84 square kilometres (4.57 sq mi), and has a population of 418 (as at 31 December 2007).
Žabovřesky lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) west of České Budějovice and 121 km (75 mi) south of Prague.
