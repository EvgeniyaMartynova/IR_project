Title: WABG (AM)
Aspects: {'WABG'}

WABG (960 AM) is an American radio station broadcasting a delta blues and classic rock format and licensed to serve the community of Greenwood, Mississippi, United States.  The station is currently owned by SPB LLC.


== History ==
The station was assigned call sign WJGJ on December 29, 2004.  On May 10, 2005, the station changed its call sign to the current WABG.  Previously the station broadcast a Talk/Personality format.
