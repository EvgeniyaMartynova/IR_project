Title: Qaleh Qazi-ye Olya
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh Qazi-ye Olya (Persian: قلعه قاضي عليا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Qāẕī-ye ‘Olyā) is a village in Gurani Rural District, Gahvareh District, Dalahu County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 33, in 7 families.
