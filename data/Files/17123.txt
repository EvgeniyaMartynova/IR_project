Title: Zaborów, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zaborów'}

Zaborów [zaˈbɔruf] (German: Lampersdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ścinawa, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south of Ścinawa, 17 km (11 mi) east of Lubin, and 51 km (32 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
