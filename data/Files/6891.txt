Title: Jabłoniec, Słupsk County
Aspects: {'Jabłoniec'}

Jabłoniec [jaˈbwɔɲet͡s] (German: Rosenhof) is a former settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Kępice, within Słupsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) east of Kępice, 26 km (16 mi) south of Słupsk, and 108 km (67 mi) west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
