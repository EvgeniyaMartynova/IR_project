Title: Yadahalli, Bilagi
Aspects: {'Yadahalli'}

Yadahalli  is a panchayat village in the southern state of Karnataka, India. Administratively, Yadahalli is under Bilagi Taluka of Bagalkot District in Karnataka.  The village of Yadahalli is 9 km by road east of Mantur and 22 km by road northwest of the town of Bilagi. Yadahalli is on the south shore of the Krishna River.


== Divisions ==
The Yadahalli gram panchayat oversees two villages: Yadahalli, and Amalazari.
