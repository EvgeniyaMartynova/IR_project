Title: Zabiele, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] (German: Sabel) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kotla, within Głogów County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Kotla, 7 km (4 mi) north-west of Głogów, and 96 km (60 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has an approximate population of 126.
