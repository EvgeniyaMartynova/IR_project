Title: Zagaje, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagaje'}

Zagaje [zaˈɡajɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jędrzejów, within Jędrzejów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south of Jędrzejów and 41 km (25 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kielce.
