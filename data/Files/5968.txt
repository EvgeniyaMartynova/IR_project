Title: Keep Your Wig On
Aspects: {'I Get High (disambiguation)'}

Keep Your Wig On is the fourth studio album released by the rock band Fastball. It was released by the indie label "Rykodisc" and the record was mixed by Bob Clearmountain.


== Track listing ==


== Personnel ==
Tony Scalzo  - vocals, bass guitar, keyboards, guitar
Miles Zuniga - vocals, guitar
Joey Shuffield  - drums, percussionThe European version of Keep Your Wig On has a bonus track, "High Low".
