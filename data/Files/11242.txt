Title: Qaemiyeh, Anbarabad
Aspects: {'Qaemiyeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaemiyeh (Persian: قائميه‎, also Romanized as Qāemīyeh) is a village in Hoseynabad Rural District, Esmaili District, Anbarabad County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 250, in 46 families.
