Title: Gratiola officinalis
Aspects: {'G. officinalis'}

Gratiola officinalis, the gratiole, common hedgehyssop or herb of grace, is an ornamental plant in the family Plantaginaceae. It is a rhizomatous perennial herb native to Europe.
