Title: Maoricolpus roseus
Aspects: {'M. roseus'}

Maoricolpus roseus is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Turritellidae, found only in New Zealand.
A subspecies of this species has been described: Maoricolpus roseus manukauensis Powell, 1931
