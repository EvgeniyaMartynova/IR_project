Title: Żabiniec, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabiniec'}

Żabiniec [ʐaˈbiɲet͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Topólka, within Radziejów County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-west of Topólka, 20 km (12 mi) south-east of Radziejów, and 62 km (39 mi) south of Toruń.
The village has a population of 50 as of 2006.
