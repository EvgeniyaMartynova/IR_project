Title: L'Autre (Et Sans album)
Aspects: {"L'Autre"}

l'Autre is the debut album of Et Sans, an experimental Canadian group. It was released in November 2001 by Locust Music.The entire album consists of one song, approximately 42 minutes and 17 seconds long, named after the album title.


== Track listing ==
