Title: Zacisze, Poddębice County
Aspects: {'Zacisze'}

Zacisze [zaˈt͡ɕiʂɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wartkowice, within Poddębice County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of Wartkowice, 11 km (7 mi) north-east of Poddębice, and 35 km (22 mi) north-west of the regional capital Łódź.
