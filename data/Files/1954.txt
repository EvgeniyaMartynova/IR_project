Title: Cirripectes gilberti
Aspects: {'C. gilberti'}

Cirripectes gilberti is a species of combtooth blenny found in coral reefs in the Indian ocean.  This species reaches a length of 9.3 centimetres (3.7 in) SL. The specific name honours the American ichthyologist Carter R. Gilbert of the Florida State Museum of Natural History.
