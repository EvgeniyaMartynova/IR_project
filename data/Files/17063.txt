Title: Żabiniec, Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabiniec'}

Żabiniec [ʐaˈbiɲet͡s] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Ustka, within Słupsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 17 kilometres (11 mi) east of Ustka, 17 km (11 mi) north of Słupsk, and 102 km (63 mi) west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
