Title: Wakonda Beach State Airport
Aspects: {'R33'}

Wakonda Beach State Airport (FAA LID: R33) is a public airport located three miles (4.8 km) southwest of Waldport in Lincoln County, Oregon, USA.  The airport is located a short walk from Beachside State Recreation Site.
