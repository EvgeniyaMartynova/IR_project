Title: Jabłoń-Dobki
Aspects: {'Jablon'}

Jabłoń-Dobki [ˈjabwɔɲ ˈdɔpki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowe Piekuty, within Wysokie Mazowieckie County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.There are only 11 houses there now. During the "pacification" of Jabłoń-Dobki in World War II, Germans killed 91 local people there on March 8, 1944, most of whom were burned alive.
