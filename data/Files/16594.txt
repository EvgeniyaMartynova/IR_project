Title: Yalabad, Markazi
Aspects: {'Yalabad'}

Yalabad (Persian: يل اباد‎, also Romanized as Yalābād and Yelābād; also known as Mīlābād and Valābād) is a village in Nur Ali Beyk Rural District, in the Central District of Saveh County, Markazi Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,837, in 937 families.
