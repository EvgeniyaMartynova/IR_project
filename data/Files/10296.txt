Poebrodon is an extinct genus of terrestrial herbivore in the family Camelidae, endemic to North America during the Lutetian (early Middle) Eocene 46.2â€”42.0 mya, existing for approximately 4.68 million years.


== Taxonomy ==
Poebrodon was named by Gazin (1955). Its type is Poebrodon kayi. It was assigned to Camelidae by Gazin (1955) and Carroll (1988).


== Fossil distribution ==
Fossil distribution ranged from Utah, Wyoming, and Southern California.
