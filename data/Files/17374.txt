Title: Zafarabad, Kakavand
Aspects: {'Zafarabad'}

Zafarabad (Persian: ظفراباد‎, also Romanized as Zafarābād; also known as Zafarābād-e Janqoli) is a village in Itivand-e Jonubi Rural District, Kakavand District, Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 144, in 23 families.
