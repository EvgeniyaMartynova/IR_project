Title: Ząbrsko Górne
Aspects: {'Ząbrsko'}

Ząbrsko Górne [ˈzɔmbrskɔ ˈɡurnɛ] (German: Ober Sommerkau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przywidz, within Gdańsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Przywidz, 19 km (12 mi) west of Pruszcz Gdański, and 24 km (15 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 103.
