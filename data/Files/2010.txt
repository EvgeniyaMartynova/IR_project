Title: Cobana
Aspects: {'C. guatemalensis'}

Cobana is a genus of herbaceous, perennial and bulbous plants in the Iris family (Iridaceae). A monotypic genus, it contains a single species, Cobana guatemalensis, distributed in Honduras and Guatemala.
