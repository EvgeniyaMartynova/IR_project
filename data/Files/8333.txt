Title: Vexillum taeniatum
Aspects: {'M. crocea'}

Vexillum (Vexillum) taeniatum, common name: the ribboned mitre,  is a species of small sea snail, marine gastropod mollusk in the family Costellariidae, the ribbed miters.


== Description ==
The shell size varies between 38 mm and 85 mm.


== Distribution ==
This species is distributed in the Indian Ocean along Madagascar and in the Pacific Ocean along the Philippines, Indonesia, Fiji, Papua New Guinea and in the Gulf of Thailand.
