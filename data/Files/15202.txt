Title: Waberi District
Aspects: {'Waberi'}

Waberi District (Somali: Degmada Waaberi) is a district in the southeastern Banaadir region of Somalia. A neighborhood in southwestern Mogadishu, it flanks the Somali Sea. The Aden Adde International Airport is also located in this district.
