Title: Qaemabad Rural District (Tehran Province)
Aspects: {'Qaemabad Rural District'}

Qaemabad Rural District (Persian: دهستان قائم آباد‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Shahriar County, Tehran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 30,682, in 7,571 families.  The rural district has 4 villages.
