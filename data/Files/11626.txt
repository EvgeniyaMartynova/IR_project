Title: Raphitoma concinna
Aspects: {'R. concinna'}

Raphitoma concinna is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Raphitomidae.


== Description ==
The shell grows to a length of 12 mm


== Distribution ==
This species is distributed in European waters and in the entire Mediterranean Sea and in the NE Atlantic Ocean along the Azores up Norway and South up to Canary Ial.
