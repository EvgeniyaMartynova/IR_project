Title: Hayfork Airport
Aspects: {'Q72'}

Hayfork Airport (FAA LID: F62), formerly Q72, is a public airport located one mile (1.6 km) south of Hayfork, serving Trinity County, California, USA. The airport is mostly used for general aviation.


== Facilities ==
Hayfork Airport covers 162 acres (66 ha) and has one runway:

Runway 7/25: 4,115 x 60 ft. (1,254 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt
