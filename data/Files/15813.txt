Title: Bandar Khamir
Aspects: {'Xamir'}

Bandar Khamir (Persian: بندرخمير‎, also Romanized as Bandar Khamīr, Bandar-e Khamīr; also, simply Khamīr or Xamir) is a city and capital of Khamir County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 11,307, in 2,441 families.Bandar Khamir includes five villages named Upper Lashtaghān, Lower Lashtaghān, Kandāl, BaghiAbād, and Chah Sahāri.
