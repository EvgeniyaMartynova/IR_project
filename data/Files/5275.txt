Title: Highway H09 (Ukraine)
Aspects: {'H9'}

H09 is a national road (H-Highway) in Lviv Oblast, Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast and Zakarpattia Oblast, Ukraine. It runs north-south and connects Mukacheve with Lviv.
