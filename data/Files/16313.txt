Title: Indonesia national under-17 football team
Aspects: {'Yadi'}

The Indonesia national under-17 football team  (also known as Merah Putih (Red and White) or Indonesia U-17) represents Indonesia in international football competitions such as AFC U-16 Championship, AFF U-16 Championship, FIFA U-17 World Cup and any other under-17 international football tournaments. It is controlled by the Football Association of Indonesia.
