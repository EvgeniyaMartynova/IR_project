Title: Qaleh Sefid-e Sofla, Kermanshah
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sefid-e Sofla'}

Qaleh Sefid-e Sofla (Persian: قلعه سفيد سفلي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sefīd-e Soflá; also known as Qal‘eh Sefīd-e Pā’īn) is a village in Qaleh Shahin Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 207, in 46 families.
