Title: Zacisze, Gryfice County
Aspects: {'Zacisze'}

Zacisze [zaˈt͡ɕiʂɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gryfice, within Gryfice County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north of Gryfice and 77 km (48 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 60.
