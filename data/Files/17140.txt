Title: Zaborowo, Ełk County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] (German: Saborowen, 1938-45: Reichenwalde in Ostpreußen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kalinowo, within Ełk County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Kalinowo, 16 km (10 mi) east of Ełk, and 138 km (86 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
