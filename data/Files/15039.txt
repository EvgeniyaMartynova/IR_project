Title: WAAC (FM)
Aspects: {'WAAC'}

WAAC (92.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Valdosta, Georgia, United States, the station is currently owned by the Rivers Radio Group.
It is the Heritage FM station, having been on air since 1966.


== History ==
The station went on the air as WGOV-FM in 1966. On February 20, 1984, the station changed its call sign to the current WAAC.The "GOV" referred to the fact that it was owned by "Dee" Rivers, son of former Georgia governor E.D. Rivers.
