Title: Synaphea grandis
Aspects: {'S. grandis'}

Synaphea grandis is a shrub endemic to Western Australia.The tufted shrub typically grows to a height of 0.3 metres (1 ft) and usually blooms between October and November producing yellow flowers.
It is found in the Wheatbelt region of Western Australia between Chittering and Calingiri where it grows in lateritic soils.
