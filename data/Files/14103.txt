Title: Universidade Autónoma de Lisboa
Aspects: {'UAL'}

Universidade Autónoma de Lisboa (UAL; Autonomous University of Lisbon) is a private university located in Lisbon, the capital of Portugal.  UAL was founded on December 13, 1985.Its seat is in the Palace of the Counts of Redondo.
