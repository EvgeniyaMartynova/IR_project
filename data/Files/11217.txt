Title: Qadi Kola, Sari
Aspects: {'Qadi Kola'}

Qadi Kola (Persian: قاديكلا‎, also Romanized as Qādī Kolā and Qādī Kalā) is a village in Miandorud-e Kuchak Rural District, in the Central District of Sari County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 972, in 256 families.
