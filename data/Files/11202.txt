Title: Hasanabad-e Qadamgah
Aspects: {'Qadamgah (disambiguation)'}

Hasanabad-e Qadamgah (Persian: حسن ابادقدمگاه‎, also Romanized as Ḩasanābād-e Qadamgāh; also known as Ḩasanābād, Ḩassanābād, and Qadamgāh) is a village in Shurab Rural District, in the Central District of Arsanjan County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 131, in 26 families.
