Title: Jablonec, Pezinok District
Aspects: {'Jablonec'}

Jablonec (Hungarian: Halmos) is a village and municipality in western Slovakia in Pezinok District in the Bratislava region.


== Genealogical resources ==
The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Bratislava, Slovakia"

Roman Catholic church records (births/marriages/deaths): 1774-1897 (parish B)
