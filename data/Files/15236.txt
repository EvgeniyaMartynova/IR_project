Title: WQQL
Aspects: {'WABZ'}

WQQL (93.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Sherman, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Springfield, Illinois, area. The station is currently owned by Saga Communications of Illinois, LLC.


== History ==
The station singed on as WLRX with an active rock format on May 3, 1981. On June 1, 1986, the callsign was changed to WESZ. On November 15, 1994, it was changed to WWTE, and on September 16, 1996 to WYXY. From February 19, 2001 to April 7, 2005, the station aired a hot adult contemporary format as WMHX, "Mix 93.9". On April 8, 2005, the station flipped to adult hits as "93.9 ABE FM" and apdopted the callsign WABZ. 
On October 2, 2013, the WQQL callsign was moved from 101.9 to 93.9, and the format was changed to classic hits as "Cool 93.9". On the same day, "ABE FM" was moved to 101.1 W266BZ (which is currently also aired on WDBR's HD2 subchannel as classic country "101.1 The Outlaw").
