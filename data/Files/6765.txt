Title: Jaaneman (2012 film)
Aspects: {'Jaaneman'}

Jaaneman is a 2012 romantic action film starring Soham Chakraborty and Koel Mallick in lead roles. It is a remake of Paiyaa.


== Cast ==
Soham Chakraborty as Deba
Koel Mallick as Rhea
Ashish Vidyarthi as Sridhar
Partho Sarathi Chakraborty as Joga
Rajat Ganguly as Rhea's Uncle
Saswati Guhathakurta as Rhea's Grandma


== Soundtrack ==

All music is composed by Jeet Gannguli.
