Title: Qadamgah, Yazd
Aspects: {'Qadamgah (disambiguation)'}

Qadamgah (Persian: قدمگاه‎, also Romanized as Qadamgāh) is a village in Fathabad Rural District, in the Central District of Khatam County, Yazd Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
