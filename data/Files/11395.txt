Title: Qaleh Gah, Sarvabad
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gah'}

Qaleh Gah (Persian: قلعه گاه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gāh; also known as Qal‘eh Qāh) is a village in Kusalan Rural District, in the Central District of Sarvabad County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 882, in 209 families.
