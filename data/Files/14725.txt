Title: Vacation from Marriage (1927 film)
Aspects: {'Vacation from Marriage'}

Vacation from Marriage (German: Eheferien) is a 1927 German silent comedy film directed by Victor Janson and starring Harry Halm, Lilian Harvey and Jutta Jol.The film's art direction was by Jacek Rotmil.
