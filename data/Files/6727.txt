Title: 4 Gewinnt
Aspects: {'Jaa'}

4 gewinnt is the second album of the German hip-hop group Die Fantastischen Vier. The name refers to the German version of the game Connect Four. The album peaked at position 3 in Austria, Germany and Switzerland.
