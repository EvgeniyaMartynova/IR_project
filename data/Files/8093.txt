Title: L'Isle-Jourdain, Gers
Aspects: {"L'Isle-Jourdain"}

L'Isle-Jourdain (Occitan: L'Isla de Baish [ˈlizlɔ ðe ˈβaʃ]) is a commune in the Gers department in southwestern France. The lexicographers Claude (1854–1924) and Paul Augé (1881–1951) were born in L'isle-Jourdain as was the writer Armand Praviel (1875–1944).


== Geography ==


== Population ==
Its inhabitants are called Lislois.
