Title: Qaland-e Olya
Aspects: {'Qaland'}

Qaland-e Olya (Persian: قالندعليا‎, also Romanized as Qāland-e ‘Olyā; also known as Kāland-e ‘Olyā, Kāland ‘Olyā, and Qāland) is a village in Dodangeh Rural District, in the Central District of Behbahan County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 795, in 145 families.
