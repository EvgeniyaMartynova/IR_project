Title: Yahmureh-ye Do
Aspects: {'Yahmureh'}

Yahmureh-ye Do (Persian: يحموره دو‎, also Romanized as Yaḩmūreh-ye Do) is a village in Shoaybiyeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Shadravan District, Shushtar County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 24 families.
