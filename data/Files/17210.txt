Title: Żaby, Łódź Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żaby'}

Żaby [ˈʐabɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dobryszyce, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north of Dobryszyce, 13 km (8 mi) north of Radomsko, and 68 km (42 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
