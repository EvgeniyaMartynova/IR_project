Title: T&D Holdings
Aspects: {'T&D'}

T&D Holdings is a Japanese insurance company. It is listed on the Nikkei 225. It is based in Tokyo, Japan. 
Subsidiaries include Taiyo Life, Daido Life, T&D Financial Life, and T&D Asset Management.
