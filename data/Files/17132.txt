Title: Zaborowo, Brodnica County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] (German: Waldzabern)  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Górzno, within Brodnica County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Górzno, 15 km (9 mi) east of Brodnica, and 70 km (43 mi) east of Toruń.
