Title: LC Perú
Aspects: {'W4'}

LC Perú was a Peruvian airline based in Lima, Peru. It operated scheduled domestic flights. Its main base was Jorge Chávez International Airport. On December 12, 2018 the airline failed to renew its aviation insurance and was forced to declare bankruptcy.
