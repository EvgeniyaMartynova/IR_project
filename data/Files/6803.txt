Title: Jaban, Tehran
Aspects: {'Jaban'}

Jaban (Persian: جابان‎, also Romanized as Jābān; also known as Jāhbun) is a village in Abarshiveh Rural District, in the Central District of Damavand County, Tehran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,540, in 757 families.
