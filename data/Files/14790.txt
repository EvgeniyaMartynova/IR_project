Title: Willis Fletcher Johnson
Aspects: {'W. F. Johnson'}

Willis Fletcher Johnson (1857 - March 29, 1931), was an author, journalist. lecturer and for twenty years foreign and diplomatic editorial writer for The New York Tribune.

Some of Johnson's works include:

Life of Wm. Tecumseh Sherman.
