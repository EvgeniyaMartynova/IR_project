Title: Ząbki, Łódź Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Ząbki (disambiguation)'}

Ząbki [ˈzɔmpki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dmosin, within Brzeziny County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of Dmosin, 15 km (9 mi) north of Brzeziny, and 29 km (18 mi) north-east of the regional capital Łódź.
