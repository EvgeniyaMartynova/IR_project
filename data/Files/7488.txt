Title: Kaba, Hungary
Aspects: {'Kaba'}

Kaba ( (listen)) is a town in eastern Hungary, in the county of Hajdú-Bihar. The town is located along the Hungarian Route 4, approximately 190 km from Budapest, the capital city and 32 km west from Debrecen, the county seat.
A 3 kg (7 lb) meteorite landed near Kaba in 1857.
