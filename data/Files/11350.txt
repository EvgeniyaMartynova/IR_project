Title: Qaleh Lar, West Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Qalatan'}

Qaleh Lar (Persian: قلعه لر‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Lar; also known as Qalātan) is a village in Solduz Rural District, in the Central District of Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 455, in 87 families.
