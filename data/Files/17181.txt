Title: Zabrody, Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabrody'}

Zabrody [zaˈbrɔdɨ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Kościerzyna, within Kościerzyna County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south of Kościerzyna and 62 km (39 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The settlement has a population of 10.
