A Cinderella Story (film series)

A Cinderella Story is an American series of films created by Leigh Dunlap, and based on Cinderella. The film series is owned by Warner Bros. The first film was theatrically released, while the following three films were released direct-to-video and the fourth film was first shown on Freeform. On October 15, 2019, the fifth film in the series, A Cinderella Story: Christmas Wish was digitally released.


== Films ==

== Reception ==
ComingSoon.net said A Cinderella Story "was widely hated by both fans and critics". It said Another Cinderella Story was "met with praise" while A Cinderella Story: Once Upon a Song "was also deemed a flop by critics and moviegoers".
