Give a Little Love (Judds album)

"Give a Little Love" is a song written by Paul Kennerley and recorded by American country music duo The Judds. It was released in June 1988 as the first new single from their Greatest Hits album.  The song reached number 2 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles chart.


== Chart performance ==
"Give a Little Love" debuted on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart the week of June 11, 1988.
