Just a Little Love

Just a Little Love is the seventh studio album by Reba McEntire, and the first on her second label, MCA Records, released on April 2, 1984. The album's singles were its title track and  "He Broke Your Memory Last Night." Both songs charted on Hot Country Songs, with the former reaching #5 and the latter reaching #15.
