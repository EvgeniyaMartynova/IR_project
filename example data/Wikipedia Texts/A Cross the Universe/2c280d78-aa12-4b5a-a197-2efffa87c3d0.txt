A Cross the Universe (film)

A Cross the Universe is a 2008 documentary film about the French electronic music duo Justice directed by Romain Gavras, So Me and Justice themselves.


== Background ==
The film follows Justice's March 2008 North American tour. This documentary is directed by Romain Gavras, So Me and the band themselves. The documentary is meant to cover fewer of the band's live shows, and more of their personal experience touring.The live portion of this release was recorded at a concert in San Francisco, California at the Concourse Exhibition Center, on March 27, 2008.
The film was released alongside a live album of the same name.
