Port of A Coruña

The Port of A Coruña is a port in A Coruña, Spain, on the Atlantic Ocean. The port complex occupies 1.15 km² of land and 9.94 km² of water along 7 km of waterfront.


== Docks ==
The Port of A Coruña has 8.458 metres for the different services:
4.230 metres in the commercial docks
2.954 metres in the fishing basins
1.274 in the Marina and Antedársena basins.
