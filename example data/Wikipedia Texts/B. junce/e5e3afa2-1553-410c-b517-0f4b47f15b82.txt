Bebbia juncea

Bebbia, common name sweetbush, is a genus of aromatic shrubs in the daisy family.It is native to the southwestern United States (California, Nevada, Arizona, southwestern Utah, southwestern New Mexico, and extreme western Texas (El Paso County)) and northern Mexico (Sonora, Baja California, Baja California Sur). It bears plentiful yellow discoid flowers.
SpeciesSome authors combine the entire genus into one species, B. juncea. The Global Compositae Checklist  and The Plant List accept two species, with B. juncea divided into two varieties:

Bebbia atriplicifolia (A.Gray) Greene - Baja California Sur
Bebbia juncea (Benth.) Greene
Bebbia juncea var. juncea
Bebbia juncea var. aspera (Benth.) Greene
