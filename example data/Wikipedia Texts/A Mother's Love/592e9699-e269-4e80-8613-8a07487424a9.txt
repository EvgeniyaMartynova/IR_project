A Mother's Love (1929 film)

A Mother's Love (German: Mutterliebe) is a 1929 German silent drama film directed by Georg Jacoby and starring Henny Porten, Gustav Diessl and Paul Henckels.The film's sets were designed by Gustav A. Knauer and Willy Schiller.
