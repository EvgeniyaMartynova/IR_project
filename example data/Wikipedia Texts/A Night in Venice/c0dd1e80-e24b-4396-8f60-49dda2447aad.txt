A Night in Venice (1934 Hungarian film)

A Night in Venice (Hungarian: Egy éj Velencében) is a 1934 Hungarian comedy film directed by Géza von Cziffra and starring Gyula Csortos, Zsuzsa Simon and, Lici Balla.The film's sets were designed by the art director Márton Vincze. It was shot at the Hunnia Studios in Budapest. A separate German version A Night in Venice was also produced.
