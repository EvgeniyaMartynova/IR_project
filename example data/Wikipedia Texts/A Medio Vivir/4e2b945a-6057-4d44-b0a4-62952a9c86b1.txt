A Medio Vivir (song)

"A Medio Vivir" (English: "Half Living") is the title track and the third single from Ricky Martin's album, A Medio Vivir (1995). It was released as a promotional single in the United States on February 13, 1996 and in May 1997 in Spain.
The song reached number thirty-six on the Hot Latin Songs in the United States.
