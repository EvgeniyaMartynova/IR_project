Buckhart Township, Christian County, Illinois

Buckhart Township is one of seventeen townships in Christian County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 1,792 and it contained 822 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 58.75 square miles (152.2 km2), of which 58.72 square miles (152.1 km2) (or 99.95%) is land and 0.03 square miles (0.078 km2) (or 0.05%) is water.
