Dushi, Afghanistan

Dushi (Dari: دوشی‎), also spelled Doshi or Dowshi, is a town in Dushi District, Baghlan Province, Afghanistan.  It is the administrative center of Dushi District and is located on the major  Kabul-Kunduz highway.
