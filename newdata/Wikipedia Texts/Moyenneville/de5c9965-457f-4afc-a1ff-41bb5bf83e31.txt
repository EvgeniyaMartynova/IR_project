Moyenneville, Somme

Moyenneville (Picard: Moéyénneville) is a commune in the Somme department in Hauts-de-France in northern France.


== Geography ==
Moyenneville is situated at the D22 and D73 crossroads, some 5 miles (8 km) southwest of Abbeville.
