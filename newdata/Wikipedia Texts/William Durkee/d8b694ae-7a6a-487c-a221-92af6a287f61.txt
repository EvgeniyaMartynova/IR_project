Will Durkee

William Durkee (born c. 1983) is a graduate student at the Northwestern University in Chicago, Illinois .  He graduated from the University of Virginia in 2005 with a degree in economics.  He graduated with "Honorable Mention All-American" honors in wrestling and "Academic All-American."
