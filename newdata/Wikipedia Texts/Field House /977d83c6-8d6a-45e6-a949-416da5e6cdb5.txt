Fields Heirs

Fields Heirs, also known as Fields House, was a historic home located at Middletown, New Castle County, Delaware.  It was built about 1820, and was a ​2 1⁄2-story, five-bay by two-bay center-hall plan residence with a ​1 1⁄2-story rear wing forming an L-shaped house. It sat on a stone foundation and had a gable roof.  Also on the property was a contributing drive-through granary.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985 and demolished between 1992 and 2002.
