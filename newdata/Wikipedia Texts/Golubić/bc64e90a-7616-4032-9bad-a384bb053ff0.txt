Golubić (surname)

Golubić (Serbian Cyrillic: Голубић, pronounced [ɡolǔbitɕ]) is a Serbo-Croatian surname, derived from the word golub, meaning "pigeon". Its literal meaning is "little pigeon". Notable people with the surname include:

Grgur Golubić (fl. 1347–1361), Serbian nobleman
Theodore Roy Golubic (1928–1998), American sculptor
Dijana Jovetić née Golubić (born 1984), Croatian handball player
Viktorija Golubic (born 1992), Swiss tennis player
