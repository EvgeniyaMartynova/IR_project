Vincent Brown (linebacker)

Vincent Bernard "The Undertaker" Brown (born  January 9, 1965) is an American football coach and former player.  He played professionally in the National Football League (NFL) linebacker  with the New England Patriots from 1988 to 1995. He is currently on the coaching staff at the College of William & Mary.
