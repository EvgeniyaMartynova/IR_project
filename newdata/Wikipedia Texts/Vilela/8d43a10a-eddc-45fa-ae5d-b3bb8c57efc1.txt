Vilela (Amares)

Amares (Portuguese pronunciation: [ɐˈmaɾɨʃ] (listen)) is a municipality in Braga District, Portugal. The population in 2011 was 18,889, in an area of 81.95 km².The present Mayor is José Gonçalves Barbosa, elected by the Socialist Party. The municipal holiday is June 13.
