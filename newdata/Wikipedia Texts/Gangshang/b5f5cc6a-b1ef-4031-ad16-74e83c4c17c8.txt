Gangshang, Hebei

Gangshang (simplified Chinese: 岗上; traditional Chinese: 崗上; pinyin: Gǎngshàng) is a town under the administration of Gaocheng City in southwestern Hebei province, China, located approximately halfway between Shijiazhuang and Gaocheng and just south of G1811 Huanghua–Shijiazhuang Expressway. As of 2011, it has 11 villages under its administration.
