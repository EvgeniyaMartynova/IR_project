Villeneuve-sous-Dammartin

Villeneuve-sous-Dammartin is a commune in the Seine-et-Marne department in the Île-de-France region in north-central France.


== Demographics ==
Inhabitants of Villeneuve-sous-Dammartin are called Villeneuvois.
