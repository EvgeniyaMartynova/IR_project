Villeneuve-sur-Bellot

Villeneuve-sur-Bellot is a commune in the Seine-et-Marne department in the Île-de-France region in north-central France.


== Demographics ==
Inhabitants of Villeneuve-sur-Bellot are called Villeneuvois.
