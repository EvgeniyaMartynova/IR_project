Big Sur (The Thrills song)

"Big Sur" is the third single released by Irish band the Thrills, off their debut album So Much for the City. It was released on 9 June 2003. The song reached number 17 on the UK Singles Chart, making it the most successful single release from the band. In their home country, the song reached number nine, giving them their second top-ten single after "One Horse Town". Elsewhere, "Big Sur" reached number 44 in Italy and number 55 in the Netherlands. The song contains elements from "(Theme from) The Monkees".
