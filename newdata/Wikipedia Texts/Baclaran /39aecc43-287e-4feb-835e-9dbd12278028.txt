Baclaran, Cabuyao City

Barangay Baclaran (PSGC: 043404001) is one of the eighteen (18) urbanized barangays comprising the city of Cabuyao in the province of Laguna, Philippines. It lies for about 3 kilometers away from the city proper of Cabuyao and is situated at the southeast portion of the city.  According to the 2010 Census, it has a population of 12,192 inhabitants (12,683 in Census 2007), making it ranked as 9th largest barangay in Cabuyao when it comes to population.
