Cormoș

The Cormoș (Hungarian: Kormos) is a right tributary of the river Olt in Romania. It discharges into the Olt in Augustin. Its length is 54 km (34 mi) and its basin size is 545 km2 (210 sq mi).


== Tributaries ==
The following rivers are tributaries to the river Cormoș (from source to mouth):

Left: Chișag, Fierarul, Gherend, Coșag, Șar, Agriș, Hotarul
Right: Bătătura Cailor, Sic, Vârghiș, Rica
