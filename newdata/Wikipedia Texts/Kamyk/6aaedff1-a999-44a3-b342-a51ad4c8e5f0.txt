Kamyk, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Kamyk [ˈkamɨk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łapanów, within Bochnia County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Łapanów, 13 km (8 mi) south-west of Bochnia, and 33 km (21 mi) south-east of the regional capital Kraków.
