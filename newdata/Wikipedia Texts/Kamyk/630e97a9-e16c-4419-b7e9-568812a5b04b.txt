Kamyk, Silesian Voivodeship

Kamyk [ˈkamɨk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kłobuck, within Kłobuck County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) east of Kłobuck and 73 km (45 mi) north of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 1,245.
