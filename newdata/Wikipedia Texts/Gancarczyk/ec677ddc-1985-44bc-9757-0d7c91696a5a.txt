Marek Gancarczyk

Marek Gancarczyk  (born February 19, 1983 in Grodków) is a Polish footballer. His position on the pitch is midfielder.


== Career ==


=== Club ===
In a past he was player of MKS Oława and Górnik Polkowice.


== Family ==
His brother is Janusz Gancarczyk, who plays for Zagłębie Lubin.
