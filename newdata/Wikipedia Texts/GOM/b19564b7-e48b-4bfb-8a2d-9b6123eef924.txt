Group & Organization Management

Group & Organization Management is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the fields of industrial and organizational psychology and management. The editor-in-chief is William L. Gardner (Texas Tech University). It was established in 1976 and is published by SAGE Publications.
