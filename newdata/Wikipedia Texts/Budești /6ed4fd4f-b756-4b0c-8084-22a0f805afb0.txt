Budești, Maramureș

Budești (Hungarian: Budfalva) is a commune in Maramureș County, northern Romania. It is composed of two villages, Budești and Sârbi (Szerfalva). The 17th century wooden church of Saint Nicholas, Budești Josani is one of eight Wooden Churches of Maramureș that UNESCO has listed as a World Heritage Site.[1]
