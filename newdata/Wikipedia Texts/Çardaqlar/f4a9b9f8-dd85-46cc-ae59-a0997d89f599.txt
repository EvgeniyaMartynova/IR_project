Yuxarı Çardaqlar

Yuxarı Çardaqlar (also, Yuxarı Çardaxlar, Chardakhlo, Yukhari Chardakhly, Yukhary Chardakhlar, and Yukhary-Chardakhly; Avar: Эхеди Чардахъ) is a village and municipality in the Zaqatala Rayon of Azerbaijan. It has a population of 2,083.
