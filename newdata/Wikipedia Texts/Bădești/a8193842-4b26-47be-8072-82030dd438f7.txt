Borșa River

The Borșa is a left tributary of the river Someșul Mic in Romania. It discharges into the Someșul Mic in Răscruci. Its length is 38 km (24 mi) and its basin size is 267 km2 (103 sq mi).The following rivers are tributaries to the river Borșa:
Left: Valea Rece, Fundături, Chidea, Bădești
Right: Cristorel, Șoimeni, Făureni, Giula
