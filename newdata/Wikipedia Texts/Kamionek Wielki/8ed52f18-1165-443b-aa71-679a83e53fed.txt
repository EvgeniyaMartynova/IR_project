Kamionek Wielki, Elbląg County

Kamionek Wielki (Polish pronunciation: [kaˈmjɔnɛk ˈfjɛlkʲi]; German: Groß Steinort) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Tolkmicko, within Elbląg County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Tolkmicko, 20 km (12 mi) north of Elbląg, and 86 km (53 mi) north-west of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 383.
