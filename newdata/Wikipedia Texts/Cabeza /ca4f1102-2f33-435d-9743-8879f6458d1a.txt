Richard Cabeza

Richard Cabeza (a.k.a. Richard Daemon) is a Swedish musician. He played the bass guitar in various death metal bands, including Unanimated, Dismember, Carbonized, Murder Squad. He was also the singer of the band General Surgery. In 2002 Cabeza was the touring bassist of Dark Funeral. He currently resides in Texas with his new band Rape, Pillage and Burn.
