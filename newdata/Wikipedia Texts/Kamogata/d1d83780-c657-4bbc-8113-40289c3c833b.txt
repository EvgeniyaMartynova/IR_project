Kamogata Station

Kamogata Station (鴨方駅, Kamogata-eki) is a railway station in Asakuchi, Okayama Prefecture, Japan, operated by West Japan Railway Company (JR West).


== Lines ==
Kamogata Station is served by the Sanyō Main Line.
