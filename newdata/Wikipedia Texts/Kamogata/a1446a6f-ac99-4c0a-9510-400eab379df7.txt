Kamogata, Okayama

Kamogata (鴨方町, Kamogata-chō) was a town located in Asakuchi District, Okayama Prefecture, Japan.
As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 18,446 and a density of 506.20 persons per km². The total area was 36.44 km².
On March 21, 2006, Kamogata, along with the towns of Konkō and Yorishima (all from Asakuchi District), was merged to create the city of Asakuchi.
