The Fiery Cross (newsletter)

The Fiery Cross was published by the United Klans of America mainly to attract the attention of "on-the-low" Whites, allowing them to be connected with the Klan without fear of others knowing they themselves are members, a  white-supremacist group headquartered in Tuscaloosa, Alabama. It was published in Swartz, Louisiana.
