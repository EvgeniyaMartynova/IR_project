Kamenný Újezd (České Budějovice District)

Kamenný Újezd (German: Steinkirchen) is a village and municipality (obec) in České Budějovice District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 28.96 square kilometres (11.18 sq mi), and has a population of 2,148 (as at 31 December 2007).
Kamenný Újezd lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south of České Budějovice and 132 km (82 mi) south of Prague.
