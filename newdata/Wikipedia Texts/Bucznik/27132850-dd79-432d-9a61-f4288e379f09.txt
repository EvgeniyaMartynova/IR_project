Bucznik, Pomeranian Voivodeship

Bucznik [ˈbut͡ʂnik] (German Buchwalde) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stary Dzierzgoń, within Sztum County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of Stary Dzierzgoń, 29 km (18 mi) east of Sztum, and 80 km (50 mi) south-east of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 80.
