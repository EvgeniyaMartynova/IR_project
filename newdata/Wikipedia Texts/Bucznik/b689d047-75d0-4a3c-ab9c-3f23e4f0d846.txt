Bucznik, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Bucznik [ˈbut͡ʂnik] (German: Buchheide) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Myślibórz, within Myślibórz County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
