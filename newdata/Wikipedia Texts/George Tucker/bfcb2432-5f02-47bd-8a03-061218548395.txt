George Tucker (American football)

George M. Tucker (September 5, 1929 – March 1, 2013) was an American football coach.  He served as the head football at Elon University from 1960 until 1964, compiling a record of 29–20–1.  Later working as a stockbroker after his retirement from coaching, Tucker was a member of the Elon Sports Hall of Fame.Tucker was the head football coach at Wingate University in the 1950s when the school was still a junior college.
