George R. C. Stuart

George Rogers Clark Stuart (August 31, 1924 â€“ August 23, 2008) was an American attorney and politician. He served as President of the Virginia Bar Association from 1968 to 1969, after which he was elected as a Democrat to the Virginia House of Delegates, where he served two terms. He was married to the former Mary Elizabeth Baker, the first wife of Congressman William C. Wampler.
