Vadu Moldovei

Vadu Moldovei is a commune located in Suceava County, Romania. It is composed of eight villages: Cămârzani, Ciumulești, Dumbrăvița, Ioneasa, Mesteceni, Movileni, Nigotești and Vadu Moldovei. It included four other villages until 2003, when these were split off to form Fântâna Mare Commune.
