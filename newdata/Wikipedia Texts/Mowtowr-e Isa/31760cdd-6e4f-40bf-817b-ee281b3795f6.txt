Mowtowr-e Isa, Dalgan

Mowtowr-e Isa (Persian: موتور عيسي‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e ʿĪsá) is a village in Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem Rural District, Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem District, Dalgan County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
