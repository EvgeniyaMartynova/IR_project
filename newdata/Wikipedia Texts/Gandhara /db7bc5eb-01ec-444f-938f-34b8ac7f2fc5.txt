Gandhara (moth)

Gandhara is a genus of moths in the subfamily Arctiinae. The genus was described by Moore in 1878.
Most species were previously placed in the genus Eilema.


== Species ==
Gandhara serva (Walker, 1854)
Gandhara vietnamica Dubatolov, 2012
