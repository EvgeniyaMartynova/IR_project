Parmar

Panwar redirects here. For people with the surname, see Panwar (disambiguation)The Parmar (or Panwar) is a Rajput  clan in India, who claim descent from the  Agnivansha dynasty. A Garasia clan also uses this title, claiming descent from the Rajputs.There are numerous variants of the name, including Pramar, Pavar, Powar, Pawar, Panwar and Ponwar.
