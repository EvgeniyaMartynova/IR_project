Vinary (Ústí nad Orlicí District)

See other places named Vinary (disambiguation).Vinary is very small village in the Ústí nad Orlicí District (before January 1, 2007 in the Chrudim District), Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. It has around 100 inhabitants, many of them yak farmers.
