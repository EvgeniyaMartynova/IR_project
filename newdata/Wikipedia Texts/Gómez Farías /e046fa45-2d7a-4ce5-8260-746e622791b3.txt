Metro Gómez Farías

Gómez Farías is a  station on Line 1 of the Mexico City Metro.  It is located in the Colonia Federal and Colonia Gómez Farías neighborhoods of Venustiano Carranza borough, to the northeast of downtown  Mexico City.


== General information ==
 The station logo represents the Mexican Constitution of 1857. Its name is in honour of Valentín Gómez Farías, one of the individuals who drafted that document.  The station was opened on 4 September 1969.


== Exits ==
North: Calzada Ignacio Zaragoza and Relaciones Exteriores street, Colonia Federal
South: Calzada Ignacio Zaragoza and Gómez Farías street, Colonia Valentín Gómez Farías
