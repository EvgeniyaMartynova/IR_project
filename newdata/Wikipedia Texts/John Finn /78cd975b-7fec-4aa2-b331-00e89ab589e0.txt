John Finn (American football)

John Thomas Finn (August 27, 1895 – December 25, 1970) was an American college and National Football League (NFL) player. He played running back for the Frankford Yellow Jackets for the 1924 season. From 1931–1932, he was the head coach for the Moravian College Greyhounds.
