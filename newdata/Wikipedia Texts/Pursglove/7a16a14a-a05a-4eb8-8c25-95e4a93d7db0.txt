Pursglove, West Virginia

Pursglove is an unincorporated community in Monongalia County, West Virginia, United States. Pursglove is located on U.S. Route 19 4.5 miles (7.2 km) northwest of downtown Morgantown. Pursglove has a post office with ZIP code 26546.
