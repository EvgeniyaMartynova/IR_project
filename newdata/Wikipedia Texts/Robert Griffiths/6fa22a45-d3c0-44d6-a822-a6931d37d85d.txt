Bob Griffiths (priest)

Robert Herbert (Bob) Griffiths(b 1953) is a Church in Wales priest: he has been Archdeacon of Wrexham since 2014.He was born in Rhyl. He studied for ordination at Chichester Theological College. After  a curacy in Holywell he was a Chaplain to the Forces from 1979 until 1987. After this he served at Llanfair Dyffryn Clwyd, St Asaph and Llanrhos.
