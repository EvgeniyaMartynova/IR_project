Vasilis Golias

Vasilis Golias (Greek: Βασίλης Γκόλιας; born 4 June 1985 in Ioannina, Greece) is a professional footballer who currently plays for Nafpaktiakos Asteras as a left back.


== Career ==
On 29 January 2019 it was announced, that Golias had joined Nafpaktiakos Asteras.
