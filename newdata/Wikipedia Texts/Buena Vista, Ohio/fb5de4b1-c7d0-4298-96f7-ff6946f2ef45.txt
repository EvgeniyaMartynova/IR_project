Buena Vista, Scioto County, Ohio

Buena Vista is an unincorporated community in Scioto County, in the U.S. state of Ohio.


== History ==
Buena Vista was platted in 1850. The community was named in commemoration of the Battle of Buena Vista (1847) in the Mexicanâ€“American War. A post office called Buena Vista was established in 1883, and remained in operation until 1960.
