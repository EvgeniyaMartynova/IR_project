John Good (Irish politician)

John Good (died 2 April 1941) was an Irish politician and company director. He was elected to Dáil Éireann as a Businessmen's Party Teachta Dála (TD) for the Dublin County constituency at the 1923 general election. He was re-elected at the June 1927, September 1927, 1932 and 1933 general elections as an independent TD. He did not contest the 1937 general election.
