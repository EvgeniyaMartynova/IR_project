Kanavis, Iran

Kenevist (Persian: كنويست‎, also Romanized as Kenevīst; also known as Kanvīst, Kanvīs, Kanavīs, Kanāwez, Kaneh Bīst, Kanvis, and Kenevīs) is a village in Kenevist Rural District, in the Central District of Mashhad County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,218, in 792 families.
