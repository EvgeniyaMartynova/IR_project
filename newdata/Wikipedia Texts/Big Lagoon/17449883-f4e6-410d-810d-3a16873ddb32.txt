Big Lagoon, California

Big Lagoon is a census-designated place in Humboldt County, California. It is located 7 miles (11 km) north of Trinidad, at an elevation of 56 feet (17 m). The population was 93 at the 2010 census.


== Education ==
Big Lagoon is the seat of the Big Lagoon Union Elementary School District, and home of the Big Lagoon School, a public K-8 school built in 1956 and renovated in 1996.  The school sits off of U.S. Route 101 on a 10 acres (4.0 ha) site, 26 miles (42 km) north of Eureka.
