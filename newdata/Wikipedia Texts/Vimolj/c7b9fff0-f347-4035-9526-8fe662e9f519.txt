Vimolj, Kostel

Vimolj (pronounced [ˈʋiːmɔl]) is a small settlement immediately north of Banja Loka in the Municipality of Kostel in southern Slovenia. The area is part of the traditional region of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region.
