Guli, Kermanshah

Qaleh Rashid Aqa (Persian: قلعه رشيداقا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Rashīd Āqā; also known as Gali, Guli, Qal’eh Pes Pes, and Rashīd ‘Abbāsī) is a village in Howmeh-ye Sarpol Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 238, in 54 families.
