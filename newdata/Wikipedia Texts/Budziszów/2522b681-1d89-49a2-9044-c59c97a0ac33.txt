Budziszów, Środa Śląska County

Budziszów [buˈd͡ʑiʂuf] (German: Baudis-Meesendorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kostomłoty, within Środa Śląska County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Środa Śląska, and 25 kilometres (16 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
