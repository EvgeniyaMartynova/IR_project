Budziszów, Wrocław County

Budziszów [buˈd͡ʑiʂuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kobierzyce, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 25 kilometres (16 mi) south of the regional capital Wrocław.
