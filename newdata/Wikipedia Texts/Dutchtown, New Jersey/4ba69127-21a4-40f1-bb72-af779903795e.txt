Dutchtown, Somerset County, New Jersey

Dutchtown is an unincorporated community located within Montgomery Township in Somerset County, New Jersey, United States. The area consists mainly of forested land with some houses grouped around the intersection of Dutchtown-Zion Road and Belle Meade-Blawenberg Road (County Route 601) in the northern portion of the township. Also nearby are the Unionville Cemetery and a rock quarry.
