Buddy Williams (jazz drummer)

Ira "Buddy" Williams (born December 17, 1952 in New York City) is an American jazz drummer.  He has played with  Grover Washington, Cedar Walton, David Sanborn, Kirk Whalum, Joe Sample, The Manhattan Transfer and others.
