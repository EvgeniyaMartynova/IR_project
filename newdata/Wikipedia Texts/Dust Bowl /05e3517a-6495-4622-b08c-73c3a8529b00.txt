Dustbowl (album)

Dustbowl is a studio album by the rock band Head of David. It was released in 1988 through Blast First.
Dog Day Sunrise has been covered by industrial metal band Fear Factory on their 1995 album Demanufacture.

All Music and Lyrics by Head of David.
