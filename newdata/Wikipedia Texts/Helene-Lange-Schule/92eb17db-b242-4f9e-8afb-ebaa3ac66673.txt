Helene-Lange-Schule (Frankfurt)

Helene-Lange-Schule is a modern language gymnasium in Frankfurt am Main, Hessen, Germany. The headmaster is Marc Peschke.In 2009 there were 567 students. In 2007, there were 52 teachers.
