George Webber (athlete)

George Joseph Webber (6 December 1895 â€“ 31 May 1950) was a British athlete who competed mainly in the 3000 metre team. He was born in Ipswich and affiliated with Highgate Harriers. He competed for Great Britain in the 1924 Summer Olympics held in Paris, France in the 3000 metre team where he won the silver medal with his team mates Bertram Macdonald and Herbert Johnston.
