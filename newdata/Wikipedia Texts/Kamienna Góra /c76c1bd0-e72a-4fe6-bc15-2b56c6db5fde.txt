Kamienna Góra, Opatów County

Kamienna Góra [kaˈmjɛnna ˈɡura] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Iwaniska, within Opatów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Iwaniska, 16 km (10 mi) south-west of Opatów, and 49 km (30 mi) east of the regional capital Kielce.
