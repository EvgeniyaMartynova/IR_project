Kamienna Góra, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamienna Góra [kaˈmjɛnna ˈɡura] (German: Steinberg) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czaplinek, within Drawsko County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Czaplinek, 35 km (22 mi) east of Drawsko Pomorskie, and 116 km (72 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
The village has a population of 40.
Between 1871 and 1945 the area was part of Germany.
