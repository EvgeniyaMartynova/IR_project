Kamienna Góra, Kielce County

Kamienna Góra [kaˈmjɛnna ˈɡura] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bodzentyn, within Kielce County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Bodzentyn and 25 km (16 mi) north-east of the regional capital Kielce.
