Kamieńczyk, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Kamieńczyk (Polish pronunciation: [kaˈmjɛj̃tʂɨk]; German: Steinbach) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Międzylesie, within Kłodzko County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland, near the border with the Czech Republic. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south of Międzylesie, 38 kilometres (24 mi) south of Kłodzko, and 118 kilometres (73 mi) south of the regional capital Wrocław.
