Lusagyugh, Armavir

Lusagyugh (Armenian: Լուսագյուղ, also Romanized as Lusagyukh, Lusagekh, and Lusaghyugh; until 1935, Nerk’in Turkmenlu and Turkmenlu) is a town in the Armavir Province of Armenia.
