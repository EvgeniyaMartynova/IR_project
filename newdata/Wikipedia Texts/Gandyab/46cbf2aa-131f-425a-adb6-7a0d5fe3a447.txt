Gandyab-e Bala

Gandyab-e Bala (Persian: گندياب بالا‎, also Romanized as Gandyāb-e Bālā; also known as Gandyāb) is a village in Natel Kenar-e Olya Rural District, in the Central District of Nur County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 347, in 86 families.
