Goldsmiths Prize

The Goldsmiths Prize is a British literary award founded in 2013. It is for fiction that "opens up new possibilities for the novel form". It is sponsored by Goldsmiths, University of London in association with the New Statesman and has a Â£10,000 remuneration. The award is limited to UK and Irish authors and books must be published by a UK-based publisher.
