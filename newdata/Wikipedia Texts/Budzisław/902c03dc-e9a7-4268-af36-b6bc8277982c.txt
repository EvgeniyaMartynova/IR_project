Budzisław, Radziejów County

Budzisław [buˈd͡ʑiswaf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bytoń, within Radziejów County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Bytoń, 14 km (9 mi) south-east of Radziejów, and 56 km (35 mi) south of Toruń.
