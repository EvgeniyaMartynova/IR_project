Budzisław, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship

Budzisław [buˈd͡ʑiswaf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Słupia Konecka, within Końskie County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Słupia, 32 km (20 mi) south-west of Końskie, and 38 km (24 mi) west of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of 230.
