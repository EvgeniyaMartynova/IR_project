Kamraniyeh-ye Pain

Kamraniyeh-ye Pain (Persian: كامرانيه پائين‎, also Romanized as Kāmrānīyeh-ye Pā’īn; also known as Kāmrānī and Kāmrānīyeh) is a village in Azizabad Rural District, in the Central District of Narmashir County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 248, in 69 families.
