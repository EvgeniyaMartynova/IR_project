Robert P. Higgins

Robert P. Higgins (born 1932) is an American systematic invertebrate zoologist and ecologist. 


== Career ==
In 1968, Higgins started his career as an oceanographer researcher at the Smithsonian Institution. Higgins served as Director of the Centre for Mediterranean Marine Classification 1969-1971, Program Director of Limnology and Oceanography, 1971-1974, Acting Director, International Programme on Environmental Sciences in 1974, Zoologist Superior from 1974 to 1978, and Curator, Department of Invertebrate Zoology, National Museum of Natural History in 1978. Higgins retired in 1996.
