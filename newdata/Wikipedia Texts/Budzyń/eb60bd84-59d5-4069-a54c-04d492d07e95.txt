Budzyń, Opole Lubelskie County

Budzyń [ˈbud͡zɨɲ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chodel, within Opole Lubelskie County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) west of Chodel, 11 km (7 mi) east of Opole Lubelskie, and 36 km (22 mi) south-west of the regional capital Lublin.
