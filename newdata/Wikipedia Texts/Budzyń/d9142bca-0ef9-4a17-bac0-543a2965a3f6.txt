Budzyń, Łódź Voivodeship

Budzyń [ˈbud͡zɨɲ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Żychlin, within Kutno County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Żychlin, 19 km (12 mi) east of Kutno, and 52 km (32 mi) north of the regional capital Łódź.
