Budzyń, Kraków County

Budzyń [ˈbud͡zɨɲ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Liszki, within Kraków County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Liszki and 12 km (7 mi) west of the regional capital Kraków.
The village has a population of 350.
