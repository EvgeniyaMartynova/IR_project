Budzyń, Biłgoraj County

Budzyń [ˈbud͡zɨɲ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Księżpol, within Biłgoraj County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) east of Księżpol, 18 km (11 mi) south-east of Biłgoraj, and 95 km (59 mi) south of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 109.
