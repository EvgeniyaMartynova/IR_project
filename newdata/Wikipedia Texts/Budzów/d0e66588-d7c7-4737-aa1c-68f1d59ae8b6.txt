Budzów, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Budzów [ˈbud͡zuf] is a village in Sucha County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Budzów. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-east of Sucha Beskidzka and 37 km (23 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kraków. 
The village has a population of 2,400.
