Budzów, Opole Voivodeship

Budzów [ˈbud͡zuf] (German: Busow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gorzów Śląski, within Olesno County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Gorzów Śląski, 19 km (12 mi) north of Olesno, and 51 km (32 mi) north-east of the regional capital Opole.
