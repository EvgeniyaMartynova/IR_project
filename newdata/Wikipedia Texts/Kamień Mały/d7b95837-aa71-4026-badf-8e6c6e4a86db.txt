Kamień Mały, Masovian Voivodeship

Kamień Mały (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃ ˈmawɨ]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wieniawa, within Przysucha County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Wieniawa, 10 km (6 mi) east of Przysucha, and 95 km (59 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 140.
