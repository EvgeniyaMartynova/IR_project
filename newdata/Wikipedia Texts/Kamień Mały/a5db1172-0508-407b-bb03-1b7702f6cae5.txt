Kamień Mały, Lubusz Voivodeship

Kamień Mały (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃ ˈmawɨ]; German: Stolberg (Neumark)) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Witnica, within Gorzów County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Witnica and 34 km (21 mi) west of Gorzów Wielkopolski.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
The village has a population of 364.
