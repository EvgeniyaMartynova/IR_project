Mowtowr-e Jahad, Bampur

Mowtowr-e Jahad (Persian: موتورجهاد‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Jahād) is a village in Bampur-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Bampur County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
