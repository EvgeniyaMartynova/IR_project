Kamenná Poruba (Vranov nad Topľou District)

Kamenná Poruba (Hungarian: Kővágó) is a village and municipality in Vranov nad Topľou District in the Prešov Region of eastern Slovakia.


== History ==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1402.


== Geography ==
The municipality lies at an altitude of 150 metres and covers an area of 7.402 km². It has a population of about 1622 people.
