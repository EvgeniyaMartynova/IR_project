Kamień, Chełm County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in Chełm County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Kamień. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Chełm and 73 km (45 mi) east of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 870.
