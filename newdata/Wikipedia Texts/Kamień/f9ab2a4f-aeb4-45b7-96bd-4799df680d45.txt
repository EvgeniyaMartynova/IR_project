Kamień, Opole Lubelskie County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łaziska, within Opole Lubelskie County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) west of Łaziska, 11 km (7 mi) west of Opole Lubelskie, and 55 km (34 mi) west of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 394.
