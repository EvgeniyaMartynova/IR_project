Kamień, Brzeziny County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dmosin, within Brzeziny County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Dmosin, 12 km (7 mi) north of Brzeziny, and 27 km (17 mi) north-east of the regional capital Łódź.
