Kamień, Augustów County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztabin, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north of Sztabin, 19 km (12 mi) south-east of Augustów, and 66 km (41 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
