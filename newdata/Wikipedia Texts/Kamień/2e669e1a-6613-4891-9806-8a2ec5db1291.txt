Kamień, Subcarpathian Voivodeship

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in Rzeszów County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Kamień. It lies approximately 34 km (21 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszów.
The village has a population of 2,700.
