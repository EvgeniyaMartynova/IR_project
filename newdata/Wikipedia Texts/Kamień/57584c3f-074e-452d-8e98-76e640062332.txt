Kamień, Kalisz County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ceków-Kolonia, within Kalisz County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-west of Ceków-Kolonia, 14 km (9 mi) north-east of Kalisz, and 110 km (68 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 1,100.
