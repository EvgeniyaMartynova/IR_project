Kamień, Wrocław County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Długołęka, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) south-east of Długołęka, and 14 kilometres (9 mi) east of the regional capital Wrocław.
