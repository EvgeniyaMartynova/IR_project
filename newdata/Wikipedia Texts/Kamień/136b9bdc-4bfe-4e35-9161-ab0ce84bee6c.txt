Kamień, Łuków County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wola Mysłowska, within Łuków County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Wola Mysłowska, 26 km (16 mi) west of Łuków, and 77 km (48 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.
