Kamień, Oleśnica County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]; German: Polnisch Steine, 1939–45: Schön Steine) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Międzybórz, within Oleśnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Międzybórz, 27 kilometres (17 mi) north-east of Oleśnica, and 49 kilometres (30 mi) north-east of the regional capital Wrocław.
