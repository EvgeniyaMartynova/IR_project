Kamień, Hajnówka County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]; Ukrainian: Камінь, romanized: Kamin’) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czyże, within Hajnówka County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-east of Czyże, 12 km (7 mi) north of Hajnówka, and 39 km (24 mi) south-east of the regional capital Białystok.
