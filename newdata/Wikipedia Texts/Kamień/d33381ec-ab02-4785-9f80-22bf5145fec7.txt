Kamień, Białobrzegi County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Białobrzegi, within Białobrzegi County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Białobrzegi and 68 km (42 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 290.
