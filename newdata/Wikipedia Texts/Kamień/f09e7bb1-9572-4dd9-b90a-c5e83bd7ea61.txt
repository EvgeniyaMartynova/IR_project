Kamień, Złotów County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Złotów, within Złotów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) north-west of Złotów and 115 km (71 mi) north of the regional capital Poznań.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For more on its history, see Złotów County.
The village has a population of 370.
