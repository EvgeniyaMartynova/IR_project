Kamień, Lubusz Voivodeship

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]; German: Kähmen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krosno Odrzańskie, within Krosno Odrzańskie County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
