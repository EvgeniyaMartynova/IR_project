Kamień, Włodawa County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stary Brus, within Włodawa County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north of Stary Brus, 20 km (12 mi) west of Włodawa, and 57 km (35 mi) north-east of the regional capital Lublin.
