Kamień, Koło County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Koło, within Koło County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north of Koło and 120 km (75 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 270.
