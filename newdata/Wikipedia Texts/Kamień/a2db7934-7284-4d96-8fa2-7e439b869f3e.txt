Kamień, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czernichów, within Kraków County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Czernichów and 26 km (16 mi) west of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 1,300.
