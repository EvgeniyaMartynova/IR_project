Kamień, Opoczno County

Kamień (Polish pronunciation: [ˈkamjɛj̃]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sławno, within Opoczno County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north of Sławno, 12 km (7 mi) north-west of Opoczno, and 60 km (37 mi) south-east of the regional capital Łódź.
