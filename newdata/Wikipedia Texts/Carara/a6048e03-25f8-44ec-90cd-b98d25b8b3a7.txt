Carara Kicks F.C.

Carara Kicks are a South African football (soccer) club based in Welkom, Free State that participates in the
National First Division.
The club is owned by Motebang Mokoena, the son of Free State Stars owner Mike Mokoena and serves as a feeder team to Stars who play in the Premier Soccer League.
