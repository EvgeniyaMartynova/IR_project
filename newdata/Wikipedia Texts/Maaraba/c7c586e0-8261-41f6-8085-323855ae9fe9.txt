Maaraba, Rif Dimashq

Maaraba (Arabic: معربا‎) is a Syrian village in the Al-Tall District of the Rif Dimashq Governorate. According to the Syria Central Bureau of Statistics (CBS), Maaraba had a population of 10,290 in the 2004 census. Its inhabitants are predominantly Sunni Muslims.
