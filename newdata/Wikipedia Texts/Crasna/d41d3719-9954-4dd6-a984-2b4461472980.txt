Albești, Vaslui

Albești is a commune in Vaslui County, Romania. It is composed of four villages: Albești, Corni-Albești, Crasna and Gura Albești.
Crasna village is a small railway hub on the Tecuci-Iași line, with trains branching off to Huși.
