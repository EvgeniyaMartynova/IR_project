Gamerki Małe

Gamerki Małe [ɡaˈmɛrki ˈmawɛ] (German: Klein Gemmern) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jonkowo, within Olsztyn County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) west of Jonkowo and 24 km (15 mi) west of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 20.
