Saint James Parish, New Brunswick

Saint James is a Canadian parish in Charlotte County, New Brunswick.


== History ==
Saint James Parish was created in 1823 from part of Saint Stephen Parish.  Saint James Parish was named after Saint James.


== Delineation ==
Saint James Parish is defined in the Territorial Division Act as being bounded:

South by Saint Stephen Parish and Saint David Parish; east by the northerly prolongation of the east line of Saint David Parish; north by the County line; and west by the St. Croix River.
