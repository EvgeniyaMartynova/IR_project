Bigamy (1922 film)

Bigamy (German: Bigamie) is a 1922 German silent drama film directed by Rudolf Walther-Fein and starring Alfred Abel, Margit Barnay and Reinhold Schünzel.The film's sets were designed by the art director Siegfried Wroblewsky.
