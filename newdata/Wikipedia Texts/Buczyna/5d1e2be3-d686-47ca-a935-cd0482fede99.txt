Buczyna, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Buczyna [buˈt͡ʂɨna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bochnia, within Bochnia County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) south-west of Bochnia and 33 km (21 mi) south-east of the regional capital Kraków.
