Buczyna, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Buczyna [buˈt͡ʂɨna] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Świdwin, within Świdwin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Świdwin and 87 km (54 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
