Bad Azz (album)

Bad Azz is the third studio album by American rapper Lil Boosie. It was released on October 24, 2006, by Trill Entertainment, Asylum Records and Warner Bros. Records. The album debuted at number 66 on the US Billboard 200, selling 12,000 copies in the first week.


== Singles ==
The album's lead single, titled "Zoom", was released on September 29, 2006. The song features guest vocals from American southern hip hop rapper Yung Joc, while its produced by Mouse.
