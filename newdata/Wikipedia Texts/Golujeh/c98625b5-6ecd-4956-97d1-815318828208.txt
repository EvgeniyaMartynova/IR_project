Gollujeh, Ahar

Gollujeh-ye Abellu (Persian: گلوجه ابللو‎, also Romanized as Gollūjeh-ye Abellū; also known as Goljeh, Gollūjeh, Golujeh, and Gyulyudzha) is a village in Azghan Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 77, in 23 families.
