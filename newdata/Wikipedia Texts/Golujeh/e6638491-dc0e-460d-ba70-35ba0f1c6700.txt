Golujeh, Khalkhal

Golujeh (Persian: گلوجه‎, also Romanized as Golūjeh and Gollūjeh; also known as Kollūjeh, Kuluja, and Kyulyudzha) is a village in Khanandabil-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Khalkhal County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 225, in 52 families.
