Golujeh-ye Molla Lar

Golujeh-ye Molla Lar (Persian: گلوجه مالالار‎, also Romanized as Golūjeh-ye Mollā Lār; also known as Kalījeh) is a village in Goyjah Bel Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
