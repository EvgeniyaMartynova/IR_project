Golujeh-ye Hasan Beyg

Golujeh-ye Hasan Beyg (Persian: گلوجه حسن بيگ‎, also Romanized as Golūjeh-ye Ḩasan Beyg) is a village in Almalu Rural District, Nazarkahrizi District, Hashtrud County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 277, in 40 families.
