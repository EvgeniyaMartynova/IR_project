Golujeh-ye Eslam

Golujeh-ye Eslam (Persian: گلوجه اسلام‎, also Romanized as Golūjeh-ye Eslām) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 113, in 24 families.
