Badasht, Semnan

Bedasht (Persian: بدشت‎; also Romanized as Badasht) is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Shahrud County, Semnan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 466, in 143 families.A conference of Bábís was held there in 1848; see Conference of Badasht.
