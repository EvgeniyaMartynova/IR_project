Kanakachilanga (1966 film)

Kanakachilanga is a Malayalam language film. It was released in 1966. It is an adaptation of the Bollywood movie Kismet, starring Ashok Kumar and Mumtaz Shanti. It is the first Malayalam film that included an additional scene after the release.
