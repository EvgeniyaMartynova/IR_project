Cassiane

Cassiane Santana Santos Macnhães Guimarães (born January 27, 1973) is a Brazilian gospel singer, songwriter, Christian minister and missionary known mononymouslsy as Cassiane. She is one of the most popular and best-selling Brazilian gospel singers. She has been singing for 37 years.
