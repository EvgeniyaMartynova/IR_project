Helenówka, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship

Helenówka [xɛlɛˈnufka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Imielno, within Jędrzejów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) west of Imielno, 11 km (7 mi) south-east of Jędrzejów, and 37 km (23 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kielce.
