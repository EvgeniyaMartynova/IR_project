Helenówka, Łódź Voivodeship

Helenówka [xɛlɛˈnufka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brzeziny, within Brzeziny County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Brzeziny and 25 km (16 mi) east of the regional capital Łódź.
The village has a population of 30.
