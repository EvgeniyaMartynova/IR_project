Field Township, Jefferson County, Illinois

Field Township is one of sixteen townships in Jefferson County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 1,468 and it contained 637 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 37.08 square miles (96.0 km2), of which 36.89 square miles (95.5 km2) (or 99.49%) is land and 0.19 square miles (0.49 km2) (or 0.51%) is water.  The townshop is centered at 38°26′N 88°52′W (38.433,-88.867).
