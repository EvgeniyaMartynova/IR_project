Finally Rollin 2

Finally Rollin 2 is a self-released mixtape by American hip-hop recording artist Chief Keef. It features production from D.Rich, Fatboi, Jose Palacios, K-Rab, Lex Luger, Metro Boomin, Mike WiLL Made-It, Shawty Redd, Sonny Digital, Southside, TM88, MP808, Young Chop & Zaytoven.
