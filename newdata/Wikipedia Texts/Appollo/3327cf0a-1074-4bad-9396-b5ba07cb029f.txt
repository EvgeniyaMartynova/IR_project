K.G.Koppal, Mysore

K.G.Koppal is a locality in Chamarajapuram, Mysore, Karnataka.  The locality is named after Kennaegowda.


== Important Landmarks ==
Ashokapuram Police Station
Appollo Hospital
K.G.Koppal Market
Akshay Bhandar Junction
Underbridge junction
Chamarajapuram railway station
Old district court complex
New district court complex
