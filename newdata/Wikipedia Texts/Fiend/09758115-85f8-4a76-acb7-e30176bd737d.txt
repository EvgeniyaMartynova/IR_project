Fiend (song)

"Fiend" is a song by Coal Chamber, from their third album, Dark Days. It is one of the band's most well known songs and is thought to be about how the band and the nu metal genre were getting heavily criticized at the time.


== Track listing ==
"Fiend" - 3:01


== Music video ==
Dez Fafara and Meegs Rascon drive to a suburban house, where two children are playing outside, their mother, horrified, runs out and ushers them back inside, Fafara and Rascon then walk to the house's garage where Mike Cox and Nadja Peulen are waiting, then they perform the song while an increasing crowd of teenagers listens outside.
