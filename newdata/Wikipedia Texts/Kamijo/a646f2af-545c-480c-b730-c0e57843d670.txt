Akimine Kamijo

Akimine Kamijyo (上条明峰, Kamijō Akimine, born September 13, 1975) is the pen name of a Japanese manga artist. Her works include Samurai Deeper Kyo, Shirogane no Karasu (also known as Silver Crow) which started on May 30, 2007 in Weekly Shōnen Magazine, and her most recent work Code:Breaker, which was completed in 2013.


== Works ==
Samurai Deeper Kyo (サムライ ディーパー キョウ) (1999–2006, serialized in Weekly Shōnen Magazine, Kodansha)
Shirogane no Karasu (しろがねの鴉) (2007–2008, serialized in Weekly Shōnen Magazine, Kodansha)
Code: Breaker (コード: ブレイカー) (2008-2013, serialized in Weekly Shōnen Magazine, Kodansha)
