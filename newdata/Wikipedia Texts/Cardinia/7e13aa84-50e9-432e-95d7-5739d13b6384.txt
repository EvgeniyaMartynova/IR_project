Cardinia Dam Power Station

Cardinia Dam Power Station is a small hydroelectric power station at Cardinia Reservoir near Melbourne, Australia. It is located at the outlet of the 12.5-kilometre (7.8 mi) pipeline transferring water from Silvan Reservoir.  It is capable of producing 3.5 megawatts (4,700 hp) of electricity and is owned by Pacific Energy.
