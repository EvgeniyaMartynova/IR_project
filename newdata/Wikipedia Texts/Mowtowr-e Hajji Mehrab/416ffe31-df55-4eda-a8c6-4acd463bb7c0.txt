Mowtowr-e Hajji Mehrab, Khash

Mowtowr-e Hajji Mehrab (Persian: موتورحاجي مهراب‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Ḩājjī Mehrāb) is a village in Gowhar Kuh Rural District, Nukabad District, Khash County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 8 families.
