Budy Michałowskie, Grójec County

Budy Michałowskie [ˈbudɨ mixaˈwɔfskʲɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Warka, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south-west of Warka, 23 km (14 mi) south-east of Grójec, and 58 km (36 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 40.
