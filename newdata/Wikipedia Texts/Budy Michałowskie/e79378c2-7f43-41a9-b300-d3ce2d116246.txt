Budy Michałowskie, Grodzisk Mazowiecki County

Budy Michałowskie [ˈbudɨ mixaˈwɔfskʲɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jaktorów, within Grodzisk Mazowiecki County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Jaktorów, 8 km (5 mi) south-west of Grodzisk Mazowiecki, and 35 km (22 mi) south-west of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 240.
