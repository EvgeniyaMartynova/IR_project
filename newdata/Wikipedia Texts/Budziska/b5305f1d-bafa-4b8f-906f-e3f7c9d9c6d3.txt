Budziska, Silesian Voivodeship

Budziska [buˈd͡ʑiska] (German: Bachweiler) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kuźnia Raciborska, within Racibórz County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Kuźnia Raciborska, 14 km (9 mi) north of Racibórz, and 53 km (33 mi) west of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 670.
