Budziska, Pomeranian Voivodeship

Budziska [buˈd͡ʑiska] (German: Budeziska) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Czersk, within Chojnice County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-east of Czersk, 34 km (21 mi) north-east of Chojnice, and 73 km (45 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The settlement has a population of 10.
