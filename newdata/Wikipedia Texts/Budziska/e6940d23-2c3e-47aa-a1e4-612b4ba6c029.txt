Budziska, Gołdap County

Budziska [buˈd͡ʑiska] (German: Budzisken, from 1938-45 Herbsthausen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Banie Mazurskie, within Gołdap County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland, close to the border with the Kaliningrad Oblast of Russia. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Banie Mazurskie, 22 km (14 mi) south-west of Gołdap, and 114 km (71 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945, the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
