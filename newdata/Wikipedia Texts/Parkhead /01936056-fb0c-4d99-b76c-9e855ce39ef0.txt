Parkhead, Edinburgh

Parkhead is a suburb of Edinburgh, the capital of Scotland. It is south-west of the Water of Leith. It is one of the smaller Wards in the Edinburgh City Council.  It is bounded to the north by the Calder Road (A71), to the west by Sighthill, southwest lies Wester Hailes and the south is bounded by the Kingsknowe Park.
