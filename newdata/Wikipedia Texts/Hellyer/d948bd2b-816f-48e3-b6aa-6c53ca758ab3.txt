Hellyer Park Velodrome

Hellyer Park Velodrome is a velodrome in Hellyer County Park in San Jose, California, United States. It is a 335 metres (1,099 ft) track with turns at a maximum banking of 23 degrees. It was built in 1963. It is located next to the Coyote Creek Trail and considered part of the county's Coyote Creek Parkway chain of parks along the creek.In 1972, the Hellyer Velodrome hosted the US Olympic Bicycling Trials.
