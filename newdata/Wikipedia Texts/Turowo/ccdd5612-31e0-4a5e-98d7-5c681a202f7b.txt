Turowo, Przasnysz County

Turowo [tuˈrɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czernice Borowe, within Przasnysz County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Czernice Borowe, 6 km (4 mi) south-west of Przasnysz, and 86 km (53 mi) north of Warsaw.
