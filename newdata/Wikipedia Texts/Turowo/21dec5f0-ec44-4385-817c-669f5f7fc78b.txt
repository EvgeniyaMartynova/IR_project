Turowo, Greater Poland Voivodeship

Turowo [tuˈrɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pniewy, within Szamotuły County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Pniewy, 24 km (15 mi) south-west of Szamotuły, and 42 km (26 mi) west of the regional capital Poznań.
