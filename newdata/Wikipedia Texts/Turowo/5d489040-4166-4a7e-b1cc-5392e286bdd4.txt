Turowo, Ełk County

Turowo [tuˈrɔvɔ] (German: Thurowen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kalinowo, within Ełk County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Kalinowo, 28 km (17 mi) east of Ełk, and 150 km (93 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
