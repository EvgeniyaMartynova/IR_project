List of Roswell episodes

The American science fiction television series Roswell ran between October 6, 1999, and May 14, 2002. The first two seasons aired on The WB, and the third season aired on UPN. The series follows the lives of teenage aliens, survivors of the 1947 UFO crash, hiding in plain sight as humans in Roswell, New Mexico. As of May 14, 2002, 61 episodes of Roswell have aired, concluding the third season.
