Kanan-e Sofla

Kanan-e Sofla (Persian: کنعان سفلی‎, also Romanized as Kan‘ān-e Soflá; also known as Kan‘ān-e Ra‘īyat) is a village in Dul Rural District, in the Central District of Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 111, in 26 families.
