Vilémov (Chomutov District)

Vilémov (German: Willomitz) is a village and municipality (obec) in Chomutov District in the Ústí nad Labem Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 18.83 square kilometres (7.27 sq mi), and has a population of 569 (as at 28 August 2006).
Vilémov lies approximately 20 kilometres (12 mi) south of Chomutov, 65 km (40 mi) south-west of Ústí nad Labem, and 82 km (51 mi) west of Prague.
