Vilémov (Havlíčkův Brod District)

Vilémov is a market town (městys) in Havlíčkův Brod District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The town covers an area of 24.56 square kilometres (9.48 sq mi), and has a population of 990 (as at 28 August 2006).
Vilémov lies approximately 23 kilometres (14 mi) north of Havlíčkův Brod, 46 km (29 mi) north of Jihlava, and 87 km (54 mi) east of Prague.
