John Gill (footballer, born 1903)

John J. Gill (born 1903) was an English professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper.


== Career ==
Born in Crook, Gill played for Bolton Wanderers, Bradford City and Clapton Orient. For Bradford City, he made 86 appearances in the Football League; he also made 3 FA Cup appearances.
