Biggie Tembo Jr.

Biggie Tembo Jr. (born 8 April 1988) is a Zimbabwean Jit musician who recorded for Gramma Records who released his debut album, Rwendo, in 2010. He is the son of Bhundu Boys singer Biggie Tembo.In 2012 he was engaged by Alick Macheso to open his shows.
