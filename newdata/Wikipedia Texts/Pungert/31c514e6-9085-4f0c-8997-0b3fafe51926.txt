Pungert, Loški Potok

Pungert (pronounced [ˈpuːŋɡɛɾt]; German: Baumgarten) is a small settlement in the Municipality of Loški Potok in southern Slovenia. The area is part of the traditional region of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region.
