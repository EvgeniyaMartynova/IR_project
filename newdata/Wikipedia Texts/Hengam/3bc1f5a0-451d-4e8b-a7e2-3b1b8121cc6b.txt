Hengam-e Jadid

Hengam-e Jadid (Persian: هنگام جديد‎, also Romanized as Hengām-e Jadīd, Hangām-e Jadīd, and Hengam Jadid; also known as Bandar-e Hengām, Bandar-e Jadīd, Hangām, Hengām, Hengām-e Now, Henjām, and Hīnām Māshī) is a village in Hengam Rural District, Shahab District, Qeshm County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 400, in 95 families.  The village is located on Hengam Island.
