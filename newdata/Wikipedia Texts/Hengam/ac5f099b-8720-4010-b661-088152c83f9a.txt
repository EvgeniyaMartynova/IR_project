Hengam-e Qadim

Hengam-e Qadim (Persian: هنگام قديم‎, also Romanized as Hengām-e Qadīm; also known as Hengām-e Kohneh, Hengām-e Qadīd, Hengam Kohneh, and Khammāsī) is a village in Hengam Rural District, Shahab District, Qeshm County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 48, in 12 families.  The village is located on Hengam Island.
