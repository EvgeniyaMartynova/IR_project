George C. Webb

George C. Webb was an American art director. He was nominated for four Academy Awards in the category Best Art Direction.


== Selected filmography ==
Webb was nominated for four Academy Awards for Best Art Direction:

Gambit (1966)
Thoroughly Modern Millie (1967)
Sweet Charity (1969)
Airport '77 (1977)
