Gonbadli, Razavi Khorasan

Gonbadli (Persian: گنبدلي‎, also Romanized as Gonbadlī; also known as Gombazlī, Gonbadlī-ye Now, Gondadalī, Gumbāzl, and Gumbazli) is a village in Khangiran Rural District, in the Central District of Sarakhs County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,556, in 362 families.
