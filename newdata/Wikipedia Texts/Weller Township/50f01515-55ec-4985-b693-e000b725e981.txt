Weller Township, Henry County, Illinois

Weller Township is one of twenty-four townships in Henry County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 422 and it contained 197 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 34.35 square miles (89.0 km2), of which 34.34 square miles (88.9 km2) (or 99.97%) is land and 0.01 square miles (0.026 km2) (or 0.03%) is water.
