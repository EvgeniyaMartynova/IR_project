Vils (Naab)

The Vils is a river in Bavaria, Germany.
Its source is near Freihung. It is approx. 87 km (54 mi) long. It flows generally south through the towns Vilseck, Amberg and Schmidmühlen. It is a right tributary of the Naab in Kallmünz.
