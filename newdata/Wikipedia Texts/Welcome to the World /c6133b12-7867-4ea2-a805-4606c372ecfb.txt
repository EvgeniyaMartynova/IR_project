Welcome to the World (Noiseworks song)

"Welcome to the World" is a song by Australian rock-pop band Noiseworks. It was released in November 1987 as the fourth single from their first studio album Noiseworks (1987) and peaked at number 43 on the Australian Kent Music Report.
