Gareth Parrington

Gareth Parrington is a former child actor who appeared in Boon, Soldier Soldier and Harry's Mad.
Parrington played the part of a young boy in Boon, then James Anderson in Soldier Soldier and the lead character Harry Holdsworth in the CITV comedy-drama Harry's Mad. In later years, he has worked as a snowboarding instructor.
