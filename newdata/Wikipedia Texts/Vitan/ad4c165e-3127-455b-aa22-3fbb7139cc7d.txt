Vitan, Slovenia

Vitan (pronounced [ˈʋiːtan]) is a settlement northeast of Ormož in northeastern Slovenia. It lies on the regional road from Središče ob Dravi to Miklavž pri Ormožu. The area traditionally belonged to the  region of Styria. It is now included in the Drava Statistical Region.
