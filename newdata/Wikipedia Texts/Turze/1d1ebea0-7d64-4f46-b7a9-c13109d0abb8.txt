Turze, Greater Poland Voivodeship

Turze [ˈtuʐɛ] (German: Turze, 1939-45: Tischendorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ostrzeszów, within Ostrzeszów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Ostrzeszów and 137 km (85 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 160.
