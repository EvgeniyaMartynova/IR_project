Turze, Masovian Voivodeship

Turze [ˈtuʐɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Poświętne, within Wołomin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) east of Poświętne, 16 km (10 mi) east of Wołomin, and 35 km (22 mi) east of Warsaw.
