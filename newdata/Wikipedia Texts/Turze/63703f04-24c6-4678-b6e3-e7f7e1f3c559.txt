Turze, Silesian Voivodeship

Turze [ˈtuʐɛ] (German: Wellendorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kuźnia Raciborska, within Racibórz County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Kuźnia Raciborska, 12 km (7 mi) north of Racibórz, and 53 km (33 mi) west of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 950. Ruda River enters the Odra River in the village.
