Turze, Myślibórz County

Turze [ˈtuʐɛ] (German: Thur) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Dębno, within Myślibórz County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) north-east of Dębno, 14 km (9 mi) south of Myślibórz, and 69 km (43 mi) south of the regional capital Szczecin.
The settlement has a population of 29.
