Kamo Station (Fukuoka)

Kamo Station (賀茂駅) is a subway station on the  Fukuoka City Subway Nanakuma Line in Sawara-ku, Fukuoka in Japan. Its station symbol is a catfish in violet, the sign of the Kamo Shrine.
