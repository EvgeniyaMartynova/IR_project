Puraz, Ardal

Yusefabad (Persian: يوسف اباد‎, also Romanized as Yūsefābād; also known as Dārābād and Pūrāz) is a village in Poshtkuh Rural District, in the Central District of Ardal County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 83, in 23 families.
