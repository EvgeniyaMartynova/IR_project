Gandabeh, Azna

Gandabeh (Persian: گندابه‎, also Romanized as Gandābeh and Gandāveh; also known as Gandābeh Ḩoseynābād and Hoseynābād) is a village in Azna Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 56, in 12 families.
