F. J. Turner High School

F. J. Turner High School is a high school located in the Town of Beloit, Rock County, Wisconsin, and is part of the School District of Beloit Turner. The school was named after frontiersman Frederick Jackson Turner. It has 388 students in grades 9 through 12. The school's athletic teams compete in the Wisconsin Interscholastic Athletic Association. The mascot is the Turner Trojan.
Their rivals include the Clinton Cougars, and the Jefferson Eagles.
