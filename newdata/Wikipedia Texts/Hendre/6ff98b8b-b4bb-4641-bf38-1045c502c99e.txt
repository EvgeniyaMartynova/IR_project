Hendre-Rhys

Hendre-Rhys is a small village in the  community of Trawsgoed, Ceredigion, Wales, which is 67.8 miles (109.2 km) from Cardiff and 173 miles (278.4 km) from London. Hendre-Rhys is represented in the  National Assembly for Wales by Elin Jones (Plaid Cymru) and the Member of Parliament is Mark Williams (Liberal Democrats).
