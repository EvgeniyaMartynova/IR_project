Golice, Lubusz Voivodeship

Golice [ɡɔˈlʲit͡sɛ] (German: Gohlitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Słubice, within Słubice County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland, close to the German border. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north-east of Słubice, 52 km (32 mi) south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and 80 km (50 mi) north-west of Zielona Góra.
The village has a population of 370.
