Helical plc

Helical plc (formerly Helical Bar plc) is a property investment and development company specialising in London and Manchester. It is based in London and a constituent of the FTSE SmallCap Index.


== History ==
The Helical Bar and Engineering Company Limited was incorporated as a limited company on 3 July 1919. The company sold its steel reinforcement business in 1986.
