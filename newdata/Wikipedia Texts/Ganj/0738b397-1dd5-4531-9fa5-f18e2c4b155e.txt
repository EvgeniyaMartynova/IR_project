Ganj, South Khorasan

Ganj-e Olya (Persian: گنج عليا‎, also Romanized as Ganj-e ‘Olyā; also known as Ganj-e Bālā, Ganj Bālā, and Ganj) is a village in Khusf Rural District, Central District, Khusf County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
