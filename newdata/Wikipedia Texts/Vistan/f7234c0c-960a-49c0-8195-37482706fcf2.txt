Vistan-e Pain

Vistan-e Pain (Persian: ويستان پائين‎, also Romanized as Vīstān-e Pā’īn, Veyestān-e Pā’īn, and Wistān Pāīn; also known as Wistān) is a village in Khorramdarreh Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramdarreh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 32, in 12 families.
