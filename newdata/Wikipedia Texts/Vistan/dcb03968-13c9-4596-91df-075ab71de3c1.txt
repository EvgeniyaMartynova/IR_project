Vistan-e Bala

Vistan-e Bala (Persian: ويستان بالا‎, also Romanized as Vīstān-e Bālā, Veyestān-e Bālā, Vistan-Bala, and Wistān Bāla; also known as Veyestān) is a village in Khorramdarreh Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramdarreh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 103, in 29 families.
