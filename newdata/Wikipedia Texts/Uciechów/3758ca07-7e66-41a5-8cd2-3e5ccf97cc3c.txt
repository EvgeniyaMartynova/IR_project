Uciechów, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Uciechów [uˈt͡ɕexuf] (German: Bertholdsdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dzierżoniów, within Dzierżoniów County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 10 km (6 mi) north-east of Dzierżoniów, and 50 km (31 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 1,000.
