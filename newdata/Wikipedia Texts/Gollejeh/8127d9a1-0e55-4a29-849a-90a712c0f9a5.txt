Goljeh, Ardabil

Golujeh (Persian: گلوجه‎, also Romanized as Golūjeh; also known as Goljeh, Koljeh and Kolūjeh) is a village in Dursun Khvajeh Rural District, in the Central District of Nir County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 152, in 33 families.
