Baczków, Lublin Voivodeship

Baczków [ˈbat͡ʂkuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wola Mysłowska, within Łuków County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Wola Mysłowska, 33 km (21 mi) west of Łuków, and 84 km (52 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.
