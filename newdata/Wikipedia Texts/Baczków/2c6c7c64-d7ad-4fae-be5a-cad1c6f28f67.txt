Baczków, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Baczków [ˈbat͡ʂkuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bochnia, within Bochnia County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Bochnia and 36 km (22 mi) east of the regional capital Kraków.
