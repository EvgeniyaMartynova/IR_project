Puran Tehsil

Puran (Urdu: پُوران‎) is a sub-division and tehsil of Shangla District in Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. The subdivision includes three tehsils viz: Puran itself, Martung and Makhuzai.  Puran Sub-division comprises nine Union Councils.
