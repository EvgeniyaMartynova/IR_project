The Applejacks (US band)

The Applejacks were a group of American studio musicians led by Dave Appell (March 24, 1922 â€“ November 18, 2014), a native of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. They released singles on Cameo Records. Their biggest hit was the instrumental "Mexican Hat Rock", a Top 20 hit in the U.S. in 1958.


== Singles ==
"Mexican Hat Rock" (1958) U.S. #16
"Rocka-Conga" (1959) U.S. #38
Early releases of "Rocka-Conga" were pressed as "Rocka-Tonga"
"Bunny Hop" (1959) U.S. #70
