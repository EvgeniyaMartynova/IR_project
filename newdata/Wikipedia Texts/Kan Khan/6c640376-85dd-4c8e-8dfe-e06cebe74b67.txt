Kan Khan-e Yaqub

Kan Khan-e Yaqub (Persian: كن خان يعقوب‎, also Romanized as Kan Khān-e Ya‘qūb; also known as Kankhvān) is a village in Mirbag-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 5 families.
