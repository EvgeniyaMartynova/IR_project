Kan Khan-e Masumeh

Kan Khan-e Masumeh (Persian: كن خان معصوم‎, also Romanized as Kan Khān-e Ma‘şūmeh; also known as Kankhvān-e Ma‘şūmābād) is a village in Mirbag-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 116, in 18 families.
