Vitalie Zlatan

Vitalie Zlatan (born 8 April 1993, Chişinău, Moldova) is a Moldavian football striker who plays for FC Iskra-Stal.


== Club statistics ==
Total matches played in Moldavian First League: 14 matches - 3 goals
