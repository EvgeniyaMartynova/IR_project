Emiliano Gómez (Uruguayan footballer)

Emiliano Gómez Duarte (born 18 September 2001) is a Uruguayan professional footballer who plays as a forward for Defensor Sporting. He was included in The Guardian's "Next Generation 2018", alongside compatriot and teammate Facundo Milán.
