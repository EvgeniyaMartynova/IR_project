Gömmece, Tarsus

Gömmece is a village in Tarsus district of Mersin Province, Turkey. It is situated in the southern part of Toros Mountains at 37°13′N 34°52′E. It is 45 kilometres (28 mi) to Tarsus and 75 kilometres (47 mi) to Mersin. The population of village was 171  as of 2012.
