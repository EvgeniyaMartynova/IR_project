Samir Duro

Samir Duro (born 18 October 1977) is a retired Bosnian-Herzegovinian professional footballer, who plays for Čelik Zenica in the Bosnian-Herzegovinian Premier League.


== Club career ==
Duro previously played for Sarajevo in the Bosnian Premier League and Međimurje in the Croatian Prva HNL.


== International career ==
Duro has made seven appearances for the Bosnia and Herzegovina national football team.
