Ibrahim Duro

Ibrahim Duro (born 26 April 1970) is a retired Bosnian-Herzegovinian footballer.


== Background ==
Duro played for NK Zagreb and NK Rijeka in the Croatian Prva HNL.Played in the Israeli premier league between 1997-2001, and was one of the key players in 1998-1999 season, when Maccabi Haifa achieved one of her best European achievements.
