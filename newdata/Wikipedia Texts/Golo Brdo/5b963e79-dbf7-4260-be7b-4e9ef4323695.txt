Golo Brdo, Brda

Golo Brdo (pronounced [ˈɡɔːlɔ ˈbəɾdɔ]) is a small village in the Municipality of Brda in the Littoral region of Slovenia, right on the border with Italy.The local church, built on a small hill outside the village, is dedicated to the Virgin Mary and belongs to the Parish of Kožbana.
