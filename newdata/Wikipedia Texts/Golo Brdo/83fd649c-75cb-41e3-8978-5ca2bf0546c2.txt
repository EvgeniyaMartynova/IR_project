Golo Brdo, Medvode

Golo Brdo (pronounced [ˈɡɔːlɔ ˈbəɾdɔ]; German: Golowerdu) is a settlement northwest of Ljubljana in Slovenia. It lies within the Municipality of Medvode in the Upper Carniola region.
