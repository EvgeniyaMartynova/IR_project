Golshanabad, Quchan

Golshanabad (Persian: گلشن اباد‎, also Romanized as Golshanābād; also known as Shādābeh) is a village in Dughayi Rural District, in the Central District of Quchan County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 89, in 28 families.
