Golshanabad, Aran va Bidgol

Chah-e Shomareh-ye Do (Persian: چاه شماره 2‎, also Romanized as Chāh-e Shomāreh-ye Do; also known as Golshanābād and Golshanābād-e Pā’īn) is a village in Kavirat Rural District, Kavirat District, Aran va Bidgol County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 22, in 8 families.
