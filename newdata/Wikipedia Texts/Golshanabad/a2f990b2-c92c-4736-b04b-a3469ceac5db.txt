Golshanabad, Tiran and Karvan

Kowhan (Persian: كوهان‎, also Romanized as Kowhān and Kūhān; also known as Golshanābād, Gūhān, and Kahān) is a village in Rezvaniyeh Rural District, in the Central District of Tiran and Karvan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 248, in 77 families.
