Víska (Havlíčkův Brod District)

Víska is a village and municipality (obec) in Havlíčkův Brod District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.85 square kilometres (1.49 sq mi), and has a population of 176 (as at 3 July 2006).
Víska lies approximately 20 kilometres (12 mi) north of Havlíčkův Brod, 42 km (26 mi) north of Jihlava, and 95 km (59 mi) east of Prague.
