Víska u Jevíčka

Víska u Jevíčka is a village and municipality (obec) in Svitavy District in the Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.66 square kilometres (1.41 sq mi), and has a population of 155 (as at 3 July 2006).
Víska u Jevíčka lies approximately 22 kilometres (14 mi) south-east of Svitavy, 80 km (50 mi) south-east of Pardubice, and 172 km (107 mi) east of Prague.
