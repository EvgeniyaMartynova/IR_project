Kamionna, Pułtusk County

Kamionna [kaˈmjɔnna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Winnica, within Pułtusk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south of Winnica, 16 km (10 mi) south-west of Pułtusk, and 43 km (27 mi) north of Warsaw.
