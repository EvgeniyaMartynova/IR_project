Kamionna, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Kamionna [kaˈmjɔnna] (German: Steindorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Trzciana, within Bochnia County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of Trzciana, 20 km (12 mi) south of Bochnia, and 42 km (26 mi) south-east of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 770.
In the course of the Josephine colonization Catholic and Lutheran Germans settled here in 1785.
