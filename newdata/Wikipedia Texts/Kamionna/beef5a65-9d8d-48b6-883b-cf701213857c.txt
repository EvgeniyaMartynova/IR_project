Kamionna, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Kamionna [kaˈmjɔnna] (German: Kammendorf bei Kanth) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kąty Wrocławskie, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south of Kąty Wrocławskie, and 26 kilometres (16 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
