Robert Hodgson (Archdeacon of Stafford)

The Ven. Robert Hodgson (b Hoxne 11 July 1844 - d Lichfield 29 January 1917) was Archdeacon of Stafford from 1898 to 1910.Hodgson was educated at Eton and Oriel College, Oxford and ordained in 1869.After a curacy in Stoke-upon-Trent he held incumbencies at West Bromwich, Walsall and Handsworth. In 1907 he became a residentiary canon at Lichfield Cathedral.
