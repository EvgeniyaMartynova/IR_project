Bigbee, Mississippi

Bigbee is an unincorporated community in Monroe County, Mississippi.Bigbee is located at 34°00′58″N 88°31′09″W northwest of Amory on Mississippi Highway 6 (close to its intersection with Mississippi Highway 371. According to the United States Geological Survey, a variant name is Johnsons Mill.
Bigbee derives its name from shortening and alteration of the nearby East Fork Tombigbee River.
