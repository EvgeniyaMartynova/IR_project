Bačkov (Havlíčkův Brod District)

Bačkov (Czech pronunciation: [ˈbatʃkof]) is a village and municipality (obec) in Havlíčkův Brod District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.12 square kilometres (1.20 sq mi), and has a population of 144 (as at 28 August 2006).
Bačkov lies approximately 17 kilometres (11 mi) north-west of Havlíčkův Brod, 39 km (24 mi) north of Jihlava, and 85 km (53 mi) south-east of Prague.
