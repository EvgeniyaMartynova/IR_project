Gołębiów, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship

Gołębiów [ɡɔˈwɛmbjuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lipnik, within Opatów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) east of Lipnik, 12 km (7 mi) south-east of Opatów, and 67 km (42 mi) east of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of 250.
