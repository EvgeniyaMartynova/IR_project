Gołębiów, Grójec County

Gołębiów [ɡɔˈwɛmbjuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jasieniec, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) east of Grójec and 44 km (27 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 60.
