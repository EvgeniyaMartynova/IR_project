Kamionka Mała, Masovian Voivodeship

Kamionka Mała [kaˈmjɔnka ˈmawa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wiskitki, within Żyrardów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) west of Wiskitki, 10 km (6 mi) west of Żyrardów, and 51 km (32 mi) west of Warsaw.
