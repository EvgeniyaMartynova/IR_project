Turkey Hill Sugarbush

Turkey Hill Sugarbush Ltd. is a Canadian producer of maple syrup and related products. It was founded in 1976 by Lloyd Herman and exports its products around the world.  Brothers Michael and Brian Herman continue to operate the company.The company also produces snacks and confections like maple butter, maple sugar, cookies, candies, maple popcorn, maple teas, maple coffee and maple chocolates.


== Awards ==
The company has earned awards, such as the:
International Maple Syrup Institute Award of Excellence;
Quebec Fleur de Lys Blue Ribbon of Excellence;
9 Time Winner Frontier Duty Free Association Supplier of the Year Award "1998, 2003, 2006, 2007, 2010, 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018";
Gutenberg International Award - Gold Medal for Packaging;
7 Time Winner Frontier Duty Free Association Best Specialized Product "2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018";
Quebec Fleur de Lys Blue Ribbon of Excellence;
International Syrup Golden Maple Leaf Award for Innovation in Marketing October 28, 2016;
Finalist DFNI Supplier of the Year 2018, 2019
