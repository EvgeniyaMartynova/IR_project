George Souza Sr.

George Allister Souza (senior) is a former Hong Kong international lawn bowler.


== Bowls career ==
Souza was born in 1919 in Hong Kong to Portuguese parents. He represented Hong Kong at cricket from 1953-1961 and won a gold medal in the fours at the 1970 Commonwealth Games in Edinburgh with Abdul Kitchell, Saco Delgado and Roberto da Silva.


== Personal life ==
He was an electric company employee by trade and his son George Souza Jr. became a world bowls champion.
