So Wonderful (Ladies' Code song)

"So Wonderful" is a song by South Korean girl group Ladies' Code. It was released as a digital single on February 13, 2014 by  Polaris Entertainment. It is one of the best charting songs they've released to date, with its peak position being 18 on the Billboard Hot 100 K-pop songs, the other one of their higher charting songs  was "Hate You". The song  is performed in Korean, apart from the English title.


== Track listing ==
All the songs in this album are written, composed and arranged by Super Changddai.
