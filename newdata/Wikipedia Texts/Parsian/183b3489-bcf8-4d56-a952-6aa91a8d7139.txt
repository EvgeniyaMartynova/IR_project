Parsian, Bandar Abbas

Tazian-e Pain (Persian: تازيان پايين‎, also Romanized as Tāzīān-e Pā’īn; also known as Pārsīān, Tāzeyān, Tazeyān-e Zīr, Tāzīān, Tāzīān-e Zīr, and Tazīyan) is a city in Tazian Rural District, in the Central District of Bandar Abbas County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 5,695, in 1,446 families.
