MacDonald Taylor, Sr.

MacDonald Taylor Sr. (born 27 August 1957) is a United States Virgin Islands former international soccer player.


== Career ==
Taylor appeared in four FIFA World Cup qualifying matches for United States Virgin Islands between 2000 and 2004. After playing against Saint Kitts and Nevis in March 2004, Taylor became the oldest player to appear in a FIFA World Cup qualifying match.


== Personal life ==
His son is MacDonald Taylor Jr.
