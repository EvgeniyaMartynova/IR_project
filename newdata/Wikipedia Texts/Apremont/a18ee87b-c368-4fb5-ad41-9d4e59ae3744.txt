Apremont-sur-Allier

Apremont-sur-Allier is a commune in the Cher department in the Centre-Val de Loire region of France.


== Geography ==
An area of forestry and farming comprising a small village and two hamlets situated by the west bank of the river Allier, some 35 miles (56 km) southeast of Bourges at the junction of the D100 with the D76 and D45 roads.
