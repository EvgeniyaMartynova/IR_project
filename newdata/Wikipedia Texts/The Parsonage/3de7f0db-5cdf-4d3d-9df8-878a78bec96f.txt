The Parsonage (Oak Hill, New York)

The Parsonage is a historic home located at Oak Hill in Greene County, New York.  The house was built about 1815 and is a two-story, heavy timber framed, five bay gable roofed dwelling modified about 1840 and about 1870.  Also on the property is a carriage barn with board and batten siding.  From 1868 to 1973 it served as the parsonage for the nearly Methodist Episcopal church.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2005.
