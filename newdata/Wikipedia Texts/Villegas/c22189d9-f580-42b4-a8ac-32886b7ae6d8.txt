Constant Villegas

Constant Villegas is a professional rugby league footballer who currently plays for Villeneuve XIII in the Elite One Championship. He previously played for Toulouse Olympique. He plays as a five-eighth or halfback. He is a French international.
He was named in the France training squad for the 2008 Rugby League World Cup.Villegas represented France in the 2009 Four Nations tournament.
