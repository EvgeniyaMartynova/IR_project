Harry "Pombo" Villegas

Harry "Pombo" Villegas (born 1940 in Yara, Cuba) is a Cuban Communist guerrilla, who fought alongside Che Guevara in battles from the Sierra Maestra to the Bolivian insurgency. Villegas is also a published writer.


== Books in English ==
Pombo: A Man of Che's guerrilla. With Che Guevara in Bolivia, 1966-68. (1997)
At the Side of Che Guevara: Interviews With Harry Villegas. (1997).
