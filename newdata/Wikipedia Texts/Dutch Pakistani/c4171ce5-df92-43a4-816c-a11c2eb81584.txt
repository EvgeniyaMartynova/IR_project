Pakistanis in the Netherlands

Dutch Pakistanis formed a population of 23,855 individuals (persons born in Pakistan or with at least one parent born there) according to the latest official statistics published by the Netherlands Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek on the 1st January 2019.
