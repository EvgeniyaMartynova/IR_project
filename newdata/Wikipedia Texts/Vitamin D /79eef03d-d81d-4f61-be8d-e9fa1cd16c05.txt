22-Dihydroergocalciferol

22-Dihydroergocalciferol is a form of vitamin D, also known as vitamin D4.  It has the systematic name (5Z,7E)-(3S)-9,10-seco-5,7,10(19)-ergostatrien-3-ol.Vitamin D4 is found in certain mushrooms.
