Macey, Aube

Macey  is a commune in the Aube department in north-central France.


== Population ==


== Demographic ==
In 2012, the municipality had 918 inhabitants . The evolution of the number of inhabitants is known throughout the population censuses conducted in the town since 1793. From the twenty-first century, actual census towns with less than 10,000 inhabitants are held every five years, unlike other cities that have a sample survey every year
