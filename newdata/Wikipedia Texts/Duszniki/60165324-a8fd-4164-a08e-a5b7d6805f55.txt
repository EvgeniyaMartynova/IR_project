Duszniki, Greater Poland Voivodeship

Duszniki [duʂˈniki] is a village in Szamotuły County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Duszniki. It lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) south-west of Szamotuły and 36 km (22 mi) west of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 2,100.
