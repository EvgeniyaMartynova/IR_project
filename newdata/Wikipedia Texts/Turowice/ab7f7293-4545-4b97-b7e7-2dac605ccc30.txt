Turowice, Piaseczno County

Turowice [turɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Konstancin-Jeziorna, within Piaseczno County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Konstancin-Jeziorna, 11 km (7 mi) east of Piaseczno, and 22 km (14 mi) south-east of Warsaw.
