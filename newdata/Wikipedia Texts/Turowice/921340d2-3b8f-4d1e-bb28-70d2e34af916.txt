Turowice, Grójec County

Turowice [turɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jasieniec, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Jasieniec, 7 km (4 mi) south-east of Grójec, and 45 km (28 mi) south of Warsaw.
