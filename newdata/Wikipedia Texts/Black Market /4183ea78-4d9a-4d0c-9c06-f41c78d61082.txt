Black Market (film)

Black Market (Kannada: ಬ್ಲಾಕ್ ಮಾರ್ಕೆಟ್) is a 1967 Indian Kannada film, directed and produced by S. N. Singh. The film stars Dikki Madhavarao, Narasimharaju, Sampath and Meese Muniyappa in the lead roles. The film has musical score by Chellapilla Satyam.
