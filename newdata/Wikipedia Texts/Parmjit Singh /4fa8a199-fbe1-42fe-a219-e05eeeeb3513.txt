Sandhu is a Jat clan in  the Punjab region of India and Pakistan.
Sandhu is the oldest and first caste of people who accepted Sikhism. Sandhu refers to Sindhu Ghati Sabhayata that is indus valley civilisation ( from c. 3300 BCE to till now). Sandhu's are divided by different religions like sikh, muslim and hindu in Pakistan and india.It is believed that it is the uppermost cast of sikhs.
