Ganga Gowri (1973 film)

Ganga Gowri is a 1973 Indian mythological Tamil film, directed and produced by B. R. Panthulu. The film stars Gemini Ganesan, Jayalalithaa, S. A. Asokan and Cho in lead roles. The film had musical score by M. S. Viswanathan. The movie is a remake of director's own 1967 Kannada movie Gange Gowri starring Rajkumar.
