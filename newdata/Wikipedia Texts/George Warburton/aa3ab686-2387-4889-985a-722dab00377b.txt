George Warburton (footballer, born 1934)

George Warburton (born 13 September 1934) is a Welsh former professional footballer who played as a full back.


== Career ==
Born in Wrexham, Warburton made 36 appearances in the Football League for Wrexham and Barrow between 1958 and 1961, before later playing non-League football with Morecambe.
