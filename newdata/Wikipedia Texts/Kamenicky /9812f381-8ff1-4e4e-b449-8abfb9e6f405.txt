Kamenický Šenov

Kamenický Šenov (German: Steinschönau) is a town in the Liberec region, Czech Republic. The town has a long history of glass works.


== History ==
Until 1918, the city was part of the Austrian monarchy (Austria side after the compromise of 1867), in the Tetschen  Děčín district, one of the 94 Bezirkshauptmannschaften in Bohemia.
