List of Total Drama Island episodes

This is a list of the episodes for Total Drama Island, a Canadian animated television series which premiered in Canada on Teletoon on July 8, 2007 and on June 5, 2008 in the United States on Cartoon Network. This season has 27 episodes, each 22 minutes in duration and a special 44-minute episode at the end of the season.
