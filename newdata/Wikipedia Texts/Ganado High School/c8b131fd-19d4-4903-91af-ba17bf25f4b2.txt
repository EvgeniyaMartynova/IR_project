Ganado High School (Texas)

Ganado High School is a public high school located in Ganado, Texas (USA) and classified as a 3A school by the UIL.   It is part of the Ganado Independent School District located in east central Jackson County.  In 2015, the school was rated "Met Standard" by the Texas Education Agency.


== Athletics ==
The Ganado Indians compete in these sports Cross Country, Volleyball, Football, Basketball, Powerlifting, Golf, Track, Softball & Baseball


=== State Titles ===
Girls Golf 1979(1A), 1981(2A), 1982(2A)
Boys Track 1980(1A)
