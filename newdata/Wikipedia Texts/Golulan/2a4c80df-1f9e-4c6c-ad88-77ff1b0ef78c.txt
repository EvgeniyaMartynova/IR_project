Golulan-e Sofla

Golulan-e Sofla (Persian: گلولان سفلي‎, also Romanized as Golūlān-e Soflá; also known as Galūlān and Golūlān) is a village in Il Gavark Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 464, in 76 families.
