Mowtowr-e Chengiz, Iranshahr

Mowtowr-e Chengiz (Persian: موتورچنگيز‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Chengīz) is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Iranshahr County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 26, in 5 families.
