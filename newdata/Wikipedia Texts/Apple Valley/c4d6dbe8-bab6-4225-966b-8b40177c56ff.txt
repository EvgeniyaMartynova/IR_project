Apple Valley, Ohio

Apple Valley is a census-designated place (CDP) in Knox County, in the U.S. state of Ohio. It consists of a planned community surrounding a 511-acre (2.07 km2) reservoir named Apple Valley Lake. As of the 2010 census the CDP had a population of 5,058.Primarily a resort community, Apple Valley contains a golf course.
