Bad Decisions (The Last Vegas album)

Bad Decisions is the fifth studio album by American hard rock band The Last Vegas, released on August 28, 2012, on FrostByte Media label.


== Singles ==
The first single from the album was "Other Side" in November 3, 2011.
The second single from the album was "Evil Eyes" in July 11, 2012.
The third single from the album was "She's My Confusion" in May 28, 2013.


== Track listing ==
All tracks are written by Chad Cherry.
