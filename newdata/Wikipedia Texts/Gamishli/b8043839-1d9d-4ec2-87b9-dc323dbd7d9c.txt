Gamishli Yelqi

Gamishli Yelqi (Persian: گاميشلي يلقي‎, also Romanized as Gāmīshlī Yelqī and Gomīshlī Yelqī; also known as Vakīlī) is a village in Gorganbuy Rural District, in the Central District of Aqqala County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,466, in 324 families.
