Gamishli Nazar

Gamishli Nazar (Persian: گاميشلي نزار‎, also Romanized as Gāmīshlī Nazār; also known as Gāmīshlī and Gomīshlī) is a village in Jafarbay-ye Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Torkaman County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,002, with 215 families.
