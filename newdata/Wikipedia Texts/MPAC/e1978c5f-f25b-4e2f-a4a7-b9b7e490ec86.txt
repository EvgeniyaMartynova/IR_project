Malaria Policy Advisory Committee

The Malaria Policy Advisory Committee is an international organization that works to prevent malaria.
The World Health Organization presently works with international MPAC members, led by Doctor Salim Abdulla, for initiatives such as the fight against malaria. Members include Kevin Marsh, Rose Leke, Kamini Nirmala Mendis, Brian Greenwood, and Fred Binka, and are from countries which include; Tanzania, Spain, Ghana, Australia, England, Cameroon, Sudan, Kenya, Sri Lanka, The Philippines, Switzerland, India, and Thailand.
