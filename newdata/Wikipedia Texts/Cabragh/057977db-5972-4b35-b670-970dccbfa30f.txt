Cabragh (Killeeshil)

Cabragh (from Irish: An Chabrach, meaning "the poor land") is a townland in County Tyrone, Northern Ireland. It is situated in the historic barony of Dungannon Lower and the civil parish of Killeeshil and covers an area of 347 acres. Cabragh is located approximately 12 km
west of Dungannon, close to Aghaginduff.
