Dušan Petković (footballer, born 1974)

Dušan Petković (Serbian Cyrillic: Душан Петковић; born 13 June 1974) is a Serbian former professional footballer who played as a defender. He is the son of Ilija Petković.
Petković earned seven caps for Serbia and Montenegro between 2000 and 2004. He was called up to the 2006 FIFA World Cup squad, as a replacement for the injured Mirko Vučinić, but withdrew from the roster following media criticism of the fact that it was his father who selected him.
