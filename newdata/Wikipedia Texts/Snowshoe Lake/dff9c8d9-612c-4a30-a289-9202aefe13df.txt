Snowshoe Lake (Thunder Bay District)

Snowshoe Lake is a lake in Thunder Bay District, Ontario, Canada, and part of the Lake Superior drainage basin. It is about 2.2 kilometres (1.4 mi) long and 1.9 kilometres (1.2 mi) wide, and lies at an elevation of 293 metres (961 ft). The primary inflow is an unnamed creek from Camel Read Lake, and primary outflow is an unnamed creek to Wabinosh Bay on Lake Nipigon (just 1.5 kilometres (0.9 mi) from the mouth of the Wabinosh River) which flows via the Nipigon River into Lake Superior.
