Snowshoe Lake (Temagami)

Snowshoe Lake is a "T"-shaped lake  in the Great Lakes Basin in Temagami, Nipissing District, Ontario, Canada. It is about 1,400 metres (4,593 ft) long and 1,000 metres (3,281 ft) wide, and lies at an elevation of 308 metres (1,010 ft). The primary outflow is an unnamed creek to Wasaksina Lake, which flows via intermediate lakes into Shiningwood Bay on Lake Temagami.
