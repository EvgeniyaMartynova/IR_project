Maba, Indonesia

Maba is a town and subdistrict in East Halmahera Regency, North Maluku, Indonesia. It is the capital of the East Halmahera Regency.
Buli, formerly Boeli is a village in Maba subdistrict. Most of the people in Buli are Christian and about a third of the population is Muslim.
