Mozu Station

Mozu Station (百舌鳥駅, Mozu-eki) is a railway station on the Hanwa Line in Sakai-ku, Sakai, Osaka Prefecture, Japan, operated by West Japan Railway Company (JR West).


== Lines ==
Mozu Station is served by the Hanwa Line.


== Adjacent stations ==


== History ==
The station opened on 18 July 1929. With the privatization of JNR on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR West.
