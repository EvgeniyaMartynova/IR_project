Ulaanbaatar Broadcasting System

Ulaanbaatar Broadcasting System (Mongolian: Улаанбаатар телевиз), or UBS, is a television broadcaster in Mongolia. It operates three channels, UBS which is the main channel, UBS Global and UBS Music Channel.

Sumo tournaments
