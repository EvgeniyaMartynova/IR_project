Islands of Turku

The Islands of Turku consist of islands belonging to the Finnish city of Turku. There are dozens of islands and skerries, of which four have significant amounts of permanent inhabitants:

Ruissalo/Runsala
Hirvensalo
Kakskerta
Satava
