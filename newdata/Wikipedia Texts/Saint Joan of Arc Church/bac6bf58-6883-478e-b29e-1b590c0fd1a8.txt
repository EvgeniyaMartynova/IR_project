Sainte Jeanne d'Arc Church (Besançon)

Sainte-Jeanne-d'Arc is a church located in the French city of Besançon, in the departement of Doubs.


== History ==
The church was built in 1930, in the area of Bregille. The choir was completed in 1933 but the work for termined the construction of the church was stopped in this time. In 1948 the construction started again and M. Dumas drew a new plan of the steeple (31 meters high), and the building took a Gothic Revival architecture style. In 2002, the church was renovated and in 2006, many rooms are amenaged for the orthodoxy chretiens.
