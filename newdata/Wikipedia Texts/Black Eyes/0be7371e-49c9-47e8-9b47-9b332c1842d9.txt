Black Eyes (Bradley Cooper song)

"Black Eyes" is a song from the 2018 film A Star Is Born and soundtrack of the same name, performed by Bradley Cooper. It was written by Cooper and Lukas Nelson, and produced by Cooper, Nelson and Lady Gaga.


== Background and composition ==
Musically, "Black Eyes" is a rock and roll, southern rock song, written by Bradley Cooper and Lukas Nelson. It was produced by Cooper and Nelson with additional production by Lady Gaga.
