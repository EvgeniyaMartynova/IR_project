Hemmatabad

Hemmatabad (Persian: همت اباد‎, also Romanized as  also Romanized as Hemmatābād and Himmatābād; also known as Hemmatābād-e Shahr Kohneh, Hemmatābād-e Zamānābād, and Unafābād) is a city in Taghenkoh District, Firuzeh County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,264, in 345 families.
