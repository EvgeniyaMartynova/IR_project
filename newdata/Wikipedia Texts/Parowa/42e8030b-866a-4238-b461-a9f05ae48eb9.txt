Parowa, Węgorzewo County

Parowa [paˈrɔva] (German: Birkental) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Węgorzewo, within Węgorzewo County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland, close to the border with the Kaliningrad Oblast of Russia.Before 1945, the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
