Robert Garcia (squash player)

Robert Andrew Garcia (born 15 April 1986) is a Filipino male professional squash player.He reached his highest career singles rankings of 216 in February 2018 during the 2018 PSA World Tour.  He has represented Philippines at several international competitive events including the Southeast Asian Games, Asian Games, PSA World Tour and Asian Individual Squash Championships. He is the current leading male squash player to represent Philippines at international arena.At the 2018 Asian Games, he reached round of 16 in the men's singles.
