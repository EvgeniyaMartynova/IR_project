Vincent Lee

Vincent Lee (born September 8, 1988) is a retired Malaysian defender who last played for Woodlands Wellington FC in the S-League. He was usually deployed play either as a centre back or wing back. 
He made his full debut in the S.League for Woodlands Wellington FC in place of defender Fabien Lewis in the match against Geylang United FCIn June 2012, Lee decided to leave football during the S-League mid-season break to pursue a normal career outside of the pitch. He played 4 games for the Rams, coming on as a substitute in 3 of those matches.
