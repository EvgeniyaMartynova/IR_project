Parlin, Świecie County

Parlin [ˈparlin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pruszcz, within Świecie County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Pruszcz, 12 km (7 mi) south-west of Świecie, 34 km (21 mi) north-east of Bydgoszcz, and 44 km (27 mi) north-west of Toruń.
