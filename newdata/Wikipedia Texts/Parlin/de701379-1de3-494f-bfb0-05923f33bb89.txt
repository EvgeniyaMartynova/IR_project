Parlin, Mogilno County

Parlin [ˈparlin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dąbrowa, within Mogilno County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) west of Dąbrowa, 10 km (6 mi) north of Mogilno, and 44 km (27 mi) south of Bydgoszcz.
