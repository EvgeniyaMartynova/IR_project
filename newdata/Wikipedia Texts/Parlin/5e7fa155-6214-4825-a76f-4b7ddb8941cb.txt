Parlin, Płońsk County

Parlin [ˈparlin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czerwińsk nad Wisłą, within Płońsk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Czerwińsk nad Wisłą, 25 km (16 mi) south of Płońsk, and 52 km (32 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
