John Garcia (album)

John Garcia is the eponymously titled debut studio album of John Garcia, released on July 25, 2014 by Napalm Records. Music videos were filmed for the songs "My Mind" and ""Her Bullets Energy".


== Track listing ==
All tracks are written by John Garcia, Dandy Brown, Dave Angstrom, except "5000 Miles" by Danko Jones.
