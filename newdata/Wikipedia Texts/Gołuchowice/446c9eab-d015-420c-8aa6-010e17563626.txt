Gołuchowice, Będzin County

Gołuchowice [ɡɔwuxɔˈvʲit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Siewierz, within Będzin County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Siewierz, 16 km (10 mi) north-east of Będzin, and 28 km (17 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 426.
