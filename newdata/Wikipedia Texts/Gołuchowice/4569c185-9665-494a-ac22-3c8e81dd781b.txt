Gołuchowice, Zawiercie County

Gołuchowice [ɡɔwuxɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kroczyce, within Zawiercie County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Kroczyce, 17 km (11 mi) north-east of Zawiercie, and 57 km (35 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
