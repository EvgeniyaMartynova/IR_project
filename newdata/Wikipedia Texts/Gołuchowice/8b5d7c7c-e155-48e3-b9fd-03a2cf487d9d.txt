Gołuchowice, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Gołuchowice [ɡɔwuxɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Skawina, within Kraków County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Skawina and 17 km (11 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
