Kamienna Wola, Masovian Voivodeship

Kamienna Wola [kaˈmjɛnna ˈvɔla] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Odrzywół, within Przysucha County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Odrzywół, 20 km (12 mi) north of Przysucha, and 83 km (52 mi) south-west of Warsaw.
