Kamienna Wola, Gmina Gowarczów

Kamienna Wola [kaˈmjɛnna ˈvɔla] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gowarczów, within Końskie County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Gowarczów, 15 km (9 mi) north of Końskie, and 52 km (32 mi) north of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of 330.
