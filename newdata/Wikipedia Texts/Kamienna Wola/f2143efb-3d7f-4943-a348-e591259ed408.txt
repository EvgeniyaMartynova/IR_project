Kamienna Wola, Gmina Stąporków

Kamienna Wola [kaˈmjɛnna ˈvɔla] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stąporków, within Końskie County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Stąporków, 15 km (9 mi) south of Końskie, and 24 km (15 mi) north of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of 220.
