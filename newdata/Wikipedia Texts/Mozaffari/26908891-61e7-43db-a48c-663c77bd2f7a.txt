Mozaffari-ye Shomali

Mozaffari-ye Shomali (Persian: مظفري شمالي‎, also Romanized as Moz̧affarī-ye Shomālī; also known as Moz̧affarī) is a village in Liravi-ye Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Deylam County, Bushehr Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 246, in 46 families.
