Badalpur, Gautam Buddh Nagar

Badalpur is a village located in the Gautam Budh Nagar district of Uttar Pradesh. It is part of Bisrakh Block and Dadri Tehsil. The village is native place of former Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister, Mayawati.


== Geography ==
Badalpur village is located in Dadri Tehsil of Gautam Buddha Nagar district in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated 5km away from sub-district headquarter Dadri and 16km away from district headquarter Gautam Buddha Nagar. Badalpur is the gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 313.61 hectares.
