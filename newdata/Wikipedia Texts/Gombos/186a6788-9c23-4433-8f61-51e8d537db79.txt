Norbert Gomboš

Norbert Gombos (Slovak pronunciation: [ˈnɔɾbɛɾd ˈɡɔmbɔʃ]; born 13 August 1990) is a Slovak tennis player playing on the ATP Challenger Tour and a member of the Slovakia Davis Cup team. He achieved a career-high ATP singles ranking of World No. 80 on 2 October 2017.
