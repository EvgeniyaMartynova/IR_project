Durian Tunggal

Durian Tunggal is a mukim in Alor Gajah District, Melaka, Malaysia. It is situated within the parliamentary constituency of Hang Tuah Jaya and the district of southern Alor Gajah. It is also located 4 Kilometre from Lebuh Ayer Keroh tol Plaza.
