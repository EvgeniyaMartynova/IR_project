Ceica

Ceica (Hungarian: Magyarcséke) is a commune in Bihor County, northwestern Romania with a population of 3,591 people. It is composed of seven villages: Bucium (Tőkefalva), Ceica, Ceișoara (Cseszvára), Corbești (Hollószeg), Cotiglet (Kótliget), Dușești (Dusafalva) and Incești (Jancsófalva).
