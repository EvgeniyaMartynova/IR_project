Ubiquity Global Services

Ubiquity Global Services is a multinational services organization offering customer management solutions, risk management operations and interactive voice response (IVR) solutions. Founded in 2012, Ubiquity provides outsourcing services including call centers, digital correspondence services, knowledge process outsourcing and information technology development. The company is privately held. Ubiquity operates in the United States, Portugal, El Salvador and the Philippines.
