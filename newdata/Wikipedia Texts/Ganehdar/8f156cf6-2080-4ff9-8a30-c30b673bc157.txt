Ganeh Dar, Piranshahr

Ganeh Dar (Persian: گنه دار‎, also Romanized as Ganeh Dār and Gonah Dār; also known as Genehdā, Genehdar, Gonah Dār-e Mangūr, and Gonodar) is a village in Lahijan Rural District, in the Central District of Piranshahr County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 133, in 23 families.
