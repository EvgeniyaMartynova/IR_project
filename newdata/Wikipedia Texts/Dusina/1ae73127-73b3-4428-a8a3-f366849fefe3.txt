Dusina, Poland

Dusina [duˈɕina] (German: Dusin) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gostyń, within Gostyń County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Gostyń and 55 km (34 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 310.
