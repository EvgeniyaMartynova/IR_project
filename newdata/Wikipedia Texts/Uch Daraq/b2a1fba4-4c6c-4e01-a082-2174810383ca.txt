Owch Darreh-ye Moghanli Ogham Ali

Owch Darreh-ye Moghanlu Ogham Ali (Persian: اوچ دره مغانلواغامعلي‎, also Romanized as Owch Darreh-ye Moghānlū Oghām ʿAlī; also known as Owch Darreh, Qeshlāq-e Pākdel, and Ūch Daraq-e ‘Olyā) is a village in Qeshlaq-e Sharqi Rural District, Qeshlaq Dasht District, Bileh Savar County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 144, in 26 families.
