Uch Daraq, East Azerbaijan

Uch Daraq (Persian: اوچ درق‎, also Romanized as Ūch Daraq and Ūchdaraq) is a village in Charuymaq-e Markazi Rural District, in the Central District of Charuymaq County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 376, in 75 families.
