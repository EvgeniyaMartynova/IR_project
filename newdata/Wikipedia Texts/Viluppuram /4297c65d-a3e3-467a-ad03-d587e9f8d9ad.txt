Viluppuram taluk

Viluppuram taluk is a taluk of Viluppuram district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. The headquarters of the taluk is the town of Viluppuram.


== Demographics ==
According to the 2011 census, the taluk of Viluppuram had a population of 715,411 with 358,721  males and 356,690 females. There were 994 women for every 1000 men. The taluk had a literacy rate of 69.6. Child population in the age group below 6 was 38,979 Males and 37,052 Females.
