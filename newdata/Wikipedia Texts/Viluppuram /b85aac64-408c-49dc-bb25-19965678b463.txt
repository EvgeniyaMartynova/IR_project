Villupuram (State Assembly Constituency)

Villupuram is a state legislative assembly constituency in Viluppuram district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It comprises a portion of Viluppuram taluk and is a part of the Viluppuram constituency for national elections to the Parliament of India.Villupuram  was one of 17 assembly constituencies to have Voter-verified paper audit trail (VVPAT) facilities with EVMs in the state elections of 2016.
