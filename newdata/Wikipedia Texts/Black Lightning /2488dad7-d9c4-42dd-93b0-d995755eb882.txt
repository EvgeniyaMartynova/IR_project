Black Lightning (1924 film)

Black Lightning is an extant 1924 American silent drama film directed by James P. Hogan, and starring Clara Bow and a dog named Thunder. It was made by independent Gotham Pictures. Prints of the film are held at the Russian state film archive Gosfilmofond and the UCLA Film and Television Archive.
