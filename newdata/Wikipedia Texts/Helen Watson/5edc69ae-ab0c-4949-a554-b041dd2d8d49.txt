Helen Watson (cricketer)

Helen Maree Watson (born 17 February 1972) is a former cricketer who made 66 women's One Day International appearances for New Zealand between 1999 and 2008.
His major teams are Auckland Women, Canterbury'B' Women, Canterbury Women, Pub Charities XI Women, and New Zealand Women. His batting style and bowling style are Right-hand bat and Right-arm medium.
