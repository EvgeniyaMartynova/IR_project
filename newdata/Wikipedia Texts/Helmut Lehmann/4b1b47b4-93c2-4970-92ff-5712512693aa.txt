Helmut Lehmann (rugby union)

Helmut Lehmann is a South African rugby union player, currently playing with  Western Province in the Currie Cup and Vodacom Cup. His usual position is flanker.
He also played for  Maties in the 2011, 2012 and 2013 Varsity Cup competitions and was named in a South African Universities team that played against Namibia in 2013.
