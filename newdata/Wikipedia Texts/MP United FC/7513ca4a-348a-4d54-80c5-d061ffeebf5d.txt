MP United FC (Northern Mariana Islands)

MP United is an association football club from the Northern Mariana Islands and is since the beginning in 2008 the most successful club in Northern Mariana Islands. The club has a senior and a women's team. MP United worked has the following youth teams: 
U6 (non competitive)
U8 (non competitive)
U10 (non competitive)
U12 (non competitive)
U15 (competitive)
U18 (non competitive)
