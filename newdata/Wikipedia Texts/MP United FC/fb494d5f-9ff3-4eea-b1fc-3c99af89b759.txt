MP United FC (India)

MP United FC is an Indian football 2nd Division I-League club from Madhya Pradesh, India. Their full name is Madhya Pradesh United Football Club. The club is the first football club in Madhya Pradesh.
The team was established in 2013 for improvement of football in the Central India. Over 9000 players play from over 1500 clubs across the state. As Madhya Pradesh was lacking a Professional Football team, MP United was established in Indore.
