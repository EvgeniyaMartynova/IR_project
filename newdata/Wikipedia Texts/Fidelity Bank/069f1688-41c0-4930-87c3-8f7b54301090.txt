Fidelity Bank Ghana

Fidelity Bank  is a commercial bank in Ghana which was issued with its Universal Banking License on June 28, 2006, making it the 22nd bank to be licensed by the Bank of Ghana. It is one of the twenty-seven licensed commercial banks in the country.Fidelity Bank is headquartered in Accra, at Ridge Towers. As of January 2013, the bank operates 75 networked branches and 120 VISA enabled ATMs at various locations.
