Vilyuysk Airport

Vilyuisk Airport (Russian: Аэропорт Вилюйск) (IATA: VYI, ICAO: UENW) is an airport serving, and located 3 kilometres (1.9 mi) east of, the urban locality of Vilyuysk, Vilyuysky District, in the Sakha Republic of Russia. It accommodates small transport aircraft.
