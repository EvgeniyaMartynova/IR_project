Gandarvakottai (State Assembly Constituency)

Gandarvakottai was a state assembly constituency in Tamil Nadu. It was in existence from 1957 to 1971 state elections.


== Madras State ==


== Tamil Nadu ==


== Post Delimitation ==
Gandarvakottai (SC) constituency is formed again after constituency delimitations 2008. It is included in the Tiruchirappalli parliamentary constituency.
