Gonaveh, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad

Gonaveh (Persian: گناوه‎, also Romanized as Gonāveh, Ganāveh, Ganāweh, and Genāveh) is a village in Emamzadeh Jafar Rural District, in the Central District of Gachsaran County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 171, in 43 families.
