Pianu

Pianu (German: Pien; Hungarian: Felsőpián) is a commune located in Alba County, Romania. It has a population of 3,390 and is composed of five villages: Pianu de Jos (Alsópián), Pianu de Sus (the commune center), Plaiuri (Plaintelep), Purcăreți (Sebespurkerec) and Strungari (Sztrugár).
