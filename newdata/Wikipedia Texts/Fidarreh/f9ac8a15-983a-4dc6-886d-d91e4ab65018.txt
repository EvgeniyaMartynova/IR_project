Bala Fidarreh

Bala Fidarreh (Persian: بالافيدره‎, also Romanized as Bālā Fīdarreh; also known as Bālā Fedreh and Bālā Fūdarreh) is a village in Layl Rural District, in the Central District of Lahijan County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 257, in 67 families.
