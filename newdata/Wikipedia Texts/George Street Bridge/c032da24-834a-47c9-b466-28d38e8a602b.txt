George Street Bridge (Aurora, Indiana)

George Street Bridge, also known as County Bridge No. 159, is a historic Whipple truss bridge located at Aurora, Dearborn County, Indiana. It was built by the Lomas Forge & Bridge Works in 1887.  It traverses Hogan Creek and measures 199 feet, 4 inches, long.  It is one of the oldest iron bridges in Indiana.It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.  It is located in the Downtown Aurora Historic District.
