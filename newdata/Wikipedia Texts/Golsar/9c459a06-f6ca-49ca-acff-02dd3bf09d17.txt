Golsar, Savojbolagh

Golsar (Persian: گلسار‎; formerly, Seyfabad (Persian: سيف اباد), also Romanized as Seyfābād; also known as Seyfābād-e Pol-e Kordān) is a city in the Central District of Savojbolagh County, Alborz Province, Iran.  At the 2006 census, its population was 11,013, in 2,775 families.Golsar was created a city out of two villages: Seyfabad-e Bozorg (Persian: سيف ابادخالصه, meaning Big Seyfabad) and Seyfabad-e Khaleseh (Persian: سيف ابادبزرگ, meaning Little Seyfabad).
