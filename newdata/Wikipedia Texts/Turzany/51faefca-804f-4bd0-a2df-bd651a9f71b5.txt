Turzany, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship

Turzany [tuˈʐanɨ] (German: Rieden) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Inowrocław, within Inowrocław County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) east of Inowrocław, 34 km (21 mi) south-west of Toruń, and 44 km (27 mi) south-east of Bydgoszcz.
