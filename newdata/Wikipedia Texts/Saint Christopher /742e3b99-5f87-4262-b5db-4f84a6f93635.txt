St. Christopher's School, Bahrain

St Christopher's School is a British private school located in Isa Town, Bahrain (senior campus) and Sar, Bahrain (infant and junior campuses), offering British curriculum education. It offers primary to secondary education ranging from reception to Sixth form. Students sit GCSE and A-level examinations.
In 2006, the Guardian newspaper listed St. Christopher's among the top eight best international schools offering a British curriculum around the world.In December 2013, Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II recognised the school's long term principal, Ed Goodwin, with an OBE for his contribution to British education in the Middle East.
