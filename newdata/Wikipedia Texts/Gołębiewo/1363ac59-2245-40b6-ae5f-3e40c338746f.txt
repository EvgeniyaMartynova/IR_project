Gołębiewo, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Gołębiewo [ɡɔwɛmˈbjɛvɔ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Szczecinek, within Szczecinek County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
