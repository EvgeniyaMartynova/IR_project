Canaã dos Carajás

Canaã dos Carajás is a municipality in the state of Pará in the Northern region of Brazil.The world's largest iron ore mining project, S11D, is being built nearby.
The municipality contains 30% of the Carajás National Forest, a 411,949 hectares (1,017,950 acres) sustainable use conservation unit created in 1998 that includes mining operations in a huge deposit of high-grade iron ore.
