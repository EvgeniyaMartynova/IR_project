Gomila pri Kogu

Gomila pri Kogu (pronounced [ɡɔˈmiːla pɾi ˈkoːɡu]) is a settlement in the hills northeast of Ormož in northeastern Slovenia, right on the border with Croatia. The area traditionally belonged to the Styria region and is now included in the Drava Statistical Region.


== Name ==
The name of the settlement was changed from Gomila to Gomila pri Kogu in 1955.
