Cabin Creek (Appomattox River)

Cabin Creek is a 4.8-mile-long (7.7 km) stream in the U.S. state of Virginia.  It is a tributary of the Appomattox River.  It rises in Fort Lee and flows north into the western side of the city of Hopewell, reaching the Appomattox River 3 miles (5 km) upstream of that river's confluence with the James River.
