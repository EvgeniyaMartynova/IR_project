Wellington Silva (footballer, born 1988)

Wellington Nascimento Silva (born 6 March 1988), known as Wellington Silva, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a right back for Boavista Sport Club.
After mild prominence in the Flamengo, the player notified and left the team, unilaterally.
