Wellington da Silva de Souza

Wellington da Silva de Souza, or simply Souza (born 27 May 1987), is a Brazilian football striker who currently plays for Oeste Futebol Clube. He played for Gyeongnam FC in the South Korea and J2 League side Tokushima Vortis.
