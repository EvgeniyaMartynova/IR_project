Turkey Creek Township, Barber County, Kansas

Turkey Creek Township is a township in Barber County, Kansas, USA.  As of the 2000 census, its population was 37.


== Geography ==
Turkey Creek Township covers an area of 47.49 square miles (123.0 km2) and contains no incorporated settlements.  According to the USGS, it contains one cemetery, Chinn.
