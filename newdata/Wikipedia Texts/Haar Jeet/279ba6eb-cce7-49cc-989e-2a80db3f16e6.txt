Haar Jeet (2016 film)

Haar Jeet is a 2016 Bangladeshi romance film directed by Bodiul Alam and produced by Sadat Mahmood under the banner of New Gen Entertainment, in association with The Abhi Pictures. The film stars Shajal Noor and Mahiya Mahi in lead roles.
