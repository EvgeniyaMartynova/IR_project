Haar Jeet (1972 film)

Haar Jeet (lit. Losing, Winning) is a 1972 Bollywood drama film directed by C.P. Dixit. The film stars Rehana Sultan and Anil Dhawan. The film was a remake of Tamil film Thamarai Nenjam.
