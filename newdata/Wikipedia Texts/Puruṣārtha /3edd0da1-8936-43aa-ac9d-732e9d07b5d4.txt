Purushartham

Purushartham is a 1986 Indian Malayalam film, directed by K. R. Mohan and produced by P. T. Kunjumuhammed. The film stars Adoor Bhasi, Sujatha Mehta, Jain George and Madambu Kunjukuttan in the lead roles.
