Apradhi (1992 film)

Apradhi is a 1992 Indian Bollywood film directed by K. Ravi Shankar and produced by N.R. Pachisia. It stars Anil Kapoor and Vijayashanti in pivotal roles.

== Soundtrack ==
Music was composed by Laxmikant-Pyarelal and lyrics were written by Anand Bakshi.
Music was launched on Tips Records and Cassettes.
