Roman Catholic Diocese of Carabayllo

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Carabayllo (Latin: Carabaillen(sis)) is a diocese located in the city of Carabayllo in the Ecclesiastical province of Lima in Peru.


== History ==
14 December 1996: Established as Diocese of Carabayllo from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Lima
