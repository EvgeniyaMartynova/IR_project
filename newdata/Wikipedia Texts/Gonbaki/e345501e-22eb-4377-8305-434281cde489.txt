Gonbaki District

Gonbaki District (Persian: بخش گنبکی‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Rigan County, Kerman Province, Iran.  At the 2006 census, its population was 11,926, in 2,554 families.  The District has one city: Mohammadabad-e Gonbaki. The District has one rural district (dehestan): Gonbaki Rural District.
