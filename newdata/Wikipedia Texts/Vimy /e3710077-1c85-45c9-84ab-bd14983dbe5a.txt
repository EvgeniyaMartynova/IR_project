Vimy, Alberta

Vimy is a hamlet in Westlock County in Central Alberta, Canada. It is located 1.4 kilometres (0.87 mi) east of Highway 2, approximately 59 kilometres (37 mi) north of Edmonton.


== Demographics ==
As a designated place in the 2016 Census of Population conducted by Statistics Canada, Vimy recorded a population of 198 living in 91 of its 106 total private dwellings, a change of -3.4% from its 2011 population of 205. With a land area of 0.53 km2 (0.20 sq mi), it had a population density of 373.6/km2 (967.6/sq mi) in 2016.As a designated place in the 2011 Census, Vimy had a population of 205 living in 91 of its 103 total dwellings, a 9.6% change from its 2006 population of 187. With a land area of 0.51 km2 (0.20 sq mi), it had a population density of 402/km2 (1,041/sq mi) in 2011.
