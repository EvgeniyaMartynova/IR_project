Kamienny Most, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamienny Most [kaˈmjɛnnɨ ˈmɔst] (German Steinhöfel) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chociwel, within Stargard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) east of Chociwel, 27 km (17 mi) north-east of Stargard, and 54 km (34 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
The village has a population of 232.
Between 1871 and 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
