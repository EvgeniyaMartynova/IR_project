Robert Harte (American actor)

Robert Harte is an American actor. 
Harte temporarily took over the role of Dr. Michael McBain for Nathaniel Marston on the ABC soap One Life to Live in 2007.Harte's other television credits include episodes of Third Watch, Law & Order and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.Harte co-founded Revolution Rail Co., a recreational rail-biking company, in 2017 in North Creek, NY.
