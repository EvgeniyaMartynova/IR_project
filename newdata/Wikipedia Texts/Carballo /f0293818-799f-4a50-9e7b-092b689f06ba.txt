Ezequiel Carballo

Ezequiel Damián Carballo (born November 10, 1989 in Morteros)  is an Argentine footballer currently playing as a striker.


== Teams ==
 9 de Julio de Morteros 2006–2010
 Ferro Carril Oeste 2010–2011
 Unión San Felipe 2011–2012
 Central Español 2012–2013
