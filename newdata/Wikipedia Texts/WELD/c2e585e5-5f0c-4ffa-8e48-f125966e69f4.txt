WELD-FM

WELD-FM is a Country, Bluegrass, and Southern Gospel formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Moorefield, West Virginia, serving the Potomac Highlands of West Virginia.  WELD-FM is owned and operated by Thunder Associates, LLC.


== Translator ==
In addition to the main station, WELD-FM is relayed by an FM translator to widen its broadcast area.
