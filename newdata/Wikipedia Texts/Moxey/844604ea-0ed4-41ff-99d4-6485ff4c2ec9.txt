Jim Moxey

James George Moxey (born May 28, 1953) is a Canadian retired professional ice hockey forward. Born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, Moxey played 127 games in the National Hockey League for the Cleveland Barons, Los Angeles Kings, and California Golden Seals. As a youth, he played in the 1965 Quebec International Pee-Wee Hockey Tournament with the Toronto George Bell minor ice hockey team.
