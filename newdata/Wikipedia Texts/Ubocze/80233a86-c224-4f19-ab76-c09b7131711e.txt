Ubocze, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Ubocze [uˈbɔt͡ʂɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gryfów Śląski, within Lwówek Śląski County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Gryfów Śląski, 14 kilometres (9 mi) south-west of Lwówek Śląski, and 114 kilometres (71 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 1,500.
Home of the Ubocze Sea Urchins, the local baseball team.
