Ubocze, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Ubocze [uˈbɔt͡ʂɛ] (German Augustenhof) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Grzmiąca, within Szczecinek County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 28 kilometres (17 mi) north-west of Szczecinek and 132 km (82 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 30.
