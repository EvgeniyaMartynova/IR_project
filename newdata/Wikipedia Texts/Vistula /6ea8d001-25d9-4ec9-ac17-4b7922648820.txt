Vistula, Indiana

Vistula is an unincorporated community in York Township, Elkhart County, Indiana.


== History ==
Vistula was laid out in 1865. It was likely named after the Vistula River.


== Geography ==
Vistula is located at 41°44′57″N 85°43′41″W.
