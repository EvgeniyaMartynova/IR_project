Henabad-e Olya

Henabad-e Olya (Persian: هناابادعليا‎, also Romanized as Henābād-e ‘Olyā and Hanābād-e ‘Olyā; also known as Haneh Abad Olyā, Hīnābād Bālā, and Hīnābād-e Bālā) is a village in Peyghan Chayi Rural District, in the Central District of Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 102, in 23 families.
