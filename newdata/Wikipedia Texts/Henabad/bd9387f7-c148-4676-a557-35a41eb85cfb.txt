Henabad-e Sofla

Henabad-e Sofla (Persian: هناابادسفلي‎, also Romanized as Henābād-e Soflá and Hanābād-e Soflá; also known as Haneh Abad Sofla, Hīnābād-e Pā’īn, Hīnābād Pā’īn, and Hīnābād Soflá) is a village in Peyghan Chayi Rural District, in the Central District of Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 139, in 30 families.
