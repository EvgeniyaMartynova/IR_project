Buenavista, Bohol

Buenavista, officially the Municipality of Buenavista, (Cebuano: Lungsod sa Buenavista; Tagalog: Bayan ng Buenavista),  is a 4th class municipality in the province of Bohol, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 27,261 people.The town of Buenavista, Bohol celebrates its fiesta on October 10, to honor the town patron Santo Rosario.
