Buenavista, Salamanca

Buenavista is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca,  western Spain, part of the autonomous community of Castile-Leon. It is 23 kilometres (14 mi) from the provincial capital city of Salamanca and has a population of 270 people (2016).
It lies 947 metres (3,107 ft) above sea level.
The post code is 37789.
