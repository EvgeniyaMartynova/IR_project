Mabini, Dinagat Islands

Mabini is one of 9 Barangays of the municipality of Tubajon, Dinagat Islands, Philippines. The barangay is 4 km east of the town proper of Tubajon, in the central northern portion of the province.


== Education ==
The barangay hosts the Mabini Elementary School which was established, January 01, 1942 and is part of the  Tubajon District.
