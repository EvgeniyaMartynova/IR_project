Mabini-Borja, Dinagat Islands

Mabini (Borja) is one of 14 Barangays of the municipality of Cagdianao, Dinagat Islands, Philippines, situated in the southwest portion of the province.


== Education ==
The barangay hosts the Boja Elementary School which was established, January 01, 1984 and is part of the Dinagat Islands District.
