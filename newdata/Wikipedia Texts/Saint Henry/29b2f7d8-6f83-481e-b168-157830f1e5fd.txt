Saint Henry, Indiana

Saint Henry is an unincorporated community in Cass Township, Dubois County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.


== History ==
Saint Henry was platted about 1874. The town was originally called Henryville, but was changed to Saint Henry because there was already a Henryville in Indiana.A post office was established at Saint Henry in 1870, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1933.


== Geography ==
Saint Henry is located at 38°13′03″N 86°55′43″W.
