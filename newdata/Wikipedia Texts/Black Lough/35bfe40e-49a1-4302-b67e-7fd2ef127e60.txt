Ballysaggart Lough

Ballysaggart Lough or Black Lough is a lough in Dungannon, County Tyrone, Northern Ireland.  It is part of the waterway created to service mills in nearby Moygashel.In 2002 a man drowned after getting into difficulties swimming across it.The  Ballysaggart Environmental Group was formed in 2004 to protect the unique habitat in and around the Lough.
