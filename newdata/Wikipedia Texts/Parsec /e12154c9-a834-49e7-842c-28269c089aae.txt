Parsec (magazine)

Parsec was an Argentine science fiction magazine, published in 1984.It presented authors as Mario Levrero, Robert Sheckley, George R. R. Martin, Fritz Leiber, Theodore Sturgeon, as well as the Argentine authors Angélica Gorodischer, Eduardo J. Carletti, Tarik Carson, Eduardo Abel Giménez, and Sergio Gaut vel Hartman.
