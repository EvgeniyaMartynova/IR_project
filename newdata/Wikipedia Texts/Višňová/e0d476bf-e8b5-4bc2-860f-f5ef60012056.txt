Višňová (Liberec District)

Višňová (German: Böhmisch Weigsdorf) is a village in the Liberec Region of the Czech Republic, near Frýdlant v Čechach. It has approximately 1,300 inhabitants.
The hamlets Andělka, Předlánce, Víska, Poustka, Saň, Loučná, Minkovice and Filipovka are administrative parts of Višňová.
There are several timber framed houses.
