Hababeh-ye Vosta

Hababeh-ye Vosta (Persian: حبابه وسطي‎, also Romanized as Ḩabābeh-ye Vostá; also known as Ḩabābeh and Hobbābeh) is a village in Chah Salem Rural District, in the Central District of Omidiyeh County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 127, in 22 families.
