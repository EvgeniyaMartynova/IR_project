Hababeh-ye Olya

Hababeh-ye Olya (Persian: حبايه عليا‎, also Romanized as Ḩabābeh-ye ‘Olyā; also known as Ḩabābeh-ye Bālā) is a village in Chah Salem Rural District, in the Central District of Omidiyeh County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 641, in 116 families.
