George Trimble House (Colonie, New York)

The George Trimble House in Colonie in Albany County, New York.  It was built in 1909 and is a ​1 1⁄2-story stucco house in the Arts and Crafts style.  It has a gable roof and a large shed dormer across the rear elevation.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.
