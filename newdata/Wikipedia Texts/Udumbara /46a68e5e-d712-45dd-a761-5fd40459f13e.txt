Whirling Udumbara II (piano)

Whirling Udumbara II ( 优昙波罗旋转舞 II ) is a work for piano,
composed by He Xuntian in 2012.


== Summary ==
He Xuntian adopted RD Composition and the SS Composition in his work Whirling Udumbara II.


== Inspiration ==
Whirling Udumbara II was inspired from Xuntian He’s poem Fragrant Nirvana Tree (1999).
