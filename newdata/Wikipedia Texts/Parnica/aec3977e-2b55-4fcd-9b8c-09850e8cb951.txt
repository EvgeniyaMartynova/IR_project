Parnica, Gryfino County

Parnica [parˈnit͡sa] (formerly German Rohrsdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Banie, within Gryfino County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Banie, 20 km (12 mi) south-east of Gryfino, and 34 km (21 mi) south of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
