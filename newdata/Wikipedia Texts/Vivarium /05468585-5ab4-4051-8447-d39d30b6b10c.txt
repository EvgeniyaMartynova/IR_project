Vivarium (film)

Vivarium is a 2019 internationally co-produced science fiction film, directed by Lorcan Finnegan, from a story by Finnegan and Garret Shanley. It stars Jesse Eisenberg and Imogen Poots.
It had its world premiere at the Cannes Film Festival on May 18, 2019.


== Cast ==
Jesse Eisenberg as Tom
Imogen Poots as Gemma
Jonathan Aris as Martin
Danielle Ryan as Mom
Senan Jennings as The Boy
Eanna Hardwicke as The Boy


== Production ==
In May 2018, it was announced Jesse Eisenberg and Imogen Poots had joined the cast of the film, with Lorcan Finnegan directing from a screenplay he wrote.


== Release ==
The film had its world premiere at the Cannes Film Festival on May 18, 2019. Shortly after, Saban Films acquired distribution rights to the film.


== Critical reception ==
Vivarium holds a 89% approval rating on review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, based on 9 reviews, with a weighted average of 8/10.
