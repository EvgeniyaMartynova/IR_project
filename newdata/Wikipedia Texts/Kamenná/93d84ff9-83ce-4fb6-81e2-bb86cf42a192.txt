Kamenná (Třebíč District)

Kamenná is a village and municipality (obec) in Třebíč District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 6.1 square kilometres (2.4 sq mi), and has a population of 225 (as at 3 July 2006).
Kamenná lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) north-east of Třebíč, 37 km (23 mi) east of Jihlava, and 149 km (93 mi) south-east of Prague.
