Kamenná (České Budějovice District)

Kamenná (German Sacherles) is a village and municipality (obec) in České Budějovice District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 12.61 square kilometres (4.87 sq mi), and has a population of 284 (as at 31 December 2007).
Kamenná lies approximately 26 kilometres (16 mi) south-east of České Budějovice and 147 km (91 mi) south of Prague.
