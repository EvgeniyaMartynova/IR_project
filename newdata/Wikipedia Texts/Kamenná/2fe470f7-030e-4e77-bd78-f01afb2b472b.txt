Kamenná Poruba, Žilina District

Kamenná Poruba is a village and municipality in Žilina District in the Žilina Region of northern Slovakia.


== History ==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1368.


== Geography ==
The municipality lies at an altitude of 484 metres and covers an area of 14.182 km². It has a population of about 1815 people.


== Genealogical resources ==
The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Bytca, Slovakia"
