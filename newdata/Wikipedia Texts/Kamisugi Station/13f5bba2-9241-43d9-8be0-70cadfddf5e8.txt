Kamisugi Station (Akita)

Kamisugi Station (上杉駅, Kamisugi-eki) is a railway station located in Kitaakita, Akita Prefecture, Japan.


== Lines ==
Kamisugi Station is served by the Akita Nairiku Jūkan Railway Akita Nairiku Line, and is located 12.1 km from the terminus of the line at Takanosu Station. 


== Station layout ==
Kamisugi Station consists of a single side platform serving bi-directional traffic. The station is unattended.

== History ==
Kamisugi Station opened on November 21, 1965 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR) serving the town of Aikawa, Akita. The line was privatized on November 1, 1986, becoming the Akita Nairiku Jūkan Railway.
