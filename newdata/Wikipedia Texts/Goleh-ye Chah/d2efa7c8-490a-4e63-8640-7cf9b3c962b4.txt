Galah Chah, Sistan and Baluchestan

Galah Chah (Persian: گله چاه‎, also Romanized as Galah Chāh) is a village in Dust Mohammad Rural District, in the Central District of Hirmand County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 590, in 124 families.
