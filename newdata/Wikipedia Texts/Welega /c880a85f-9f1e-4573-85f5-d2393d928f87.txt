Welega Oromo

Welega (also spelled Wellega, Wallega, Wollega, etc.) is a branch of the Oromo people who live in Ethiopia in the former Welega Province; a few live across the border in Sudan. They speak West Central Oromo, a dialect of the Oromo language. 
The Wellega population is approximately 8 million, many of them being Christians.
