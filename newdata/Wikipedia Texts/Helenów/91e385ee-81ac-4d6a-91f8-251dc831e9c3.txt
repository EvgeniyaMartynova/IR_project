Helenów, Gmina Głowno

Helenów [xɛˈlɛnuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Głowno, within Zgierz County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north-west of Głowno, 24 km (15 mi) north-east of Zgierz, and 29 km (18 mi) north of the regional capital Łódź.
