Helenów, Gmina Przyłęk

Helenów [xɛˈlɛnuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przyłęk, within Zwoleń County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 18 kilometres (11 mi) east of Zwoleń and 111 km (69 mi) south-east of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 110.
