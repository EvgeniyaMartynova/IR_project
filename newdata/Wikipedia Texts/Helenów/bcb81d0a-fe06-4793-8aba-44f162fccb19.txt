Helenów, Gmina Budziszewice

Helenów [xɛˈlɛnuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Budziszewice, within Tomaszów Mazowiecki County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Budziszewice, 19 km (12 mi) north of Tomaszów Mazowiecki, and 36 km (22 mi) east of the regional capital Łódź.The village has a population of 20.
