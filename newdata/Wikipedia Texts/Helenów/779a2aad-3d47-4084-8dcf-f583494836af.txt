Helenów, Gmina Adamów

Helenów [xɛˈlɛnuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Adamów, within Łuków County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Adamów, 29 km (18 mi) south-west of Łuków, and 56 km (35 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.The village has a population of 100.
