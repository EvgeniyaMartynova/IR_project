Helenów, Gmina Zwoleń

Helenów [xɛˈlɛnuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zwoleń, within Zwoleń County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) east of Zwoleń and 105 km (65 mi) south-east of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 50.
