University of Delaware Police

The University of Delaware Police (UDPD) are responsible for law enforcement on UD's three campuses.  The police represent one branch of the University of Delaware Department of Public Safety, whose employees also include security officers, as well as dispatchers, EMTs, and Police Cadets.
