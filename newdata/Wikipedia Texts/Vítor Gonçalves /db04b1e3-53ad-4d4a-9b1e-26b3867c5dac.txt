Vítor Bruno Rodrigues Gonçalves

Vítor Bruno Rodrigues Gonçalves (born 29 March 1992 in São Bartolomeu de Messines, Silves, Algarve) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays for C.D. Nacional as a midfielder.
