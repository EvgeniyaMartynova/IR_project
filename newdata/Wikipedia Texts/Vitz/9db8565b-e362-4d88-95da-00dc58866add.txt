Vitz-sur-Authie

Vitz-sur-Authie is a commune in the Somme department in Hauts-de-France in northern France.


== Geography ==
The commune is situated 16 miles (26 km) northeast of Abbeville, on the D121 road and on the border with the Pas-de-Calais department.
