Ha Jae-Hoon (footballer born 1984)

Ha Jae-Hoon (Korean: 하재훈; Hanja: 河在勳, born October 3, 1984) is a South Korean football player who, as of 2011 is playing for Changwon City FC.
He was played at Korea National League side Changwon City FC since 2007 to 2008. He was played 44 games and 4 assists for Changwon.
