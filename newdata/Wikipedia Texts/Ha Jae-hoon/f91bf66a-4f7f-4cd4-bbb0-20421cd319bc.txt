Ha Jae-Hoon (footballer born 1965)

Ha Jae-Hoon (Korean: 하재훈, born on February 10, 1965) is a former South Korea football player.
At the club side, he won the K-League 1989 with Yukong Elephants.
He was manager of Bucheon SK and Cheonan City FC.
