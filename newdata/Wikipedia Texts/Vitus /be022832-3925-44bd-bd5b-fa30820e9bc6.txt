Maksim Vitus

Maksim Yuryevich Vitus (Belarusian: Максім Юр'евіч Вітус; Russian: Максим Юрьевич Витус; born 11 February 1989) is a Belarusian professional footballer. As of 2018, he plays for Dinamo Brest.


== Career ==
Born in Vawkavysk, Vitus made his Belarusian Premier League debut with MTZ-RIPO in 2008.


== Honours ==
Dinamo Brest

Belarusian Cup winner: 2016–17, 2017–18
Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2018, 2019
