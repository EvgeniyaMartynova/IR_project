Punta de Rieles - Bella Italia

Punta de Rieles – Bella Italia (Italian: [ˈbɛlla iˈtaːlja]) is a barrio (neighbourhood or district) of Montevideo, Uruguay.


== Places of worship ==
Parish Church of St John Bosco, Camino Maldonado Km 16 Nº 7777 (Roman Catholic, Salesians of Don Bosco)
