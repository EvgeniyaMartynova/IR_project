Budki, Pomeranian Voivodeship

Budki [ˈbutki] (German: Bottke) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Główczyce, within Słupsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) east of Główczyce, 40 km (25 mi) north-east of Słupsk, and 77 km (48 mi) north-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
