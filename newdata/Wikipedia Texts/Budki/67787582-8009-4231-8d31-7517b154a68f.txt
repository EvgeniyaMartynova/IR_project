Budki, Przasnysz County

Budki [Ëˆbutki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chorzele, within Przasnysz County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Chorzele, 27 km (17 mi) north of Przasnysz, and 115 km (71 mi) north of Warsaw.
