Budki, Lubartów County

Budki [ˈbutki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Michów, within Lubartów County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 26 kilometres (16 mi) west of Lubartów and 40 km (25 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 100.
