Budki, Łuków County

Budki [ˈbutki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krzywda, within Łuków County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Krzywda, 23 km (14 mi) south-west of Łuków, and 69 km (43 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.
