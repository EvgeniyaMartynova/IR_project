Budziszyn, Masovian Voivodeship

Budziszyn [buˈd͡ʑiʂɨn] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chynów, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Chynów, 14 km (9 mi) east of Grójec, and 38 km (24 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 100.
