Gonbad-e Vila

Gond-e Vila (Persian: گندويلا‎, also Romanized as Gond-e Vīlā; also known as Gonbad-e Vīlā) is a village in Oshnavieh-ye Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Oshnavieh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 681, in 150 families.
