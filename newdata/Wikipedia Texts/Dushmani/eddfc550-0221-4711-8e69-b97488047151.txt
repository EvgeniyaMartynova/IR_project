Dushmani: A Violent Love Story

Dushmani: A Violent Love Story is a 1996 Indian Bollywood film directed by Bunty Soorma. It stars Sunny Deol, Jackie Shroff, Manisha Koirala and Deepti Naval in pivotal roles. The rights of this film are now owned by Shah Rukh Khan's Red Chillies Entertainment.
