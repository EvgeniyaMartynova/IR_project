Dushmani (film)

Dushmani is a 2002 Hindi-language Indian film directed by Imran Khalid, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Faisal Khan, Pooja Gandhi, Armaan Kohli and Puru Raaj Kumar. The rights for this movie are owned by Red Chillies Entertainment.


== Plot ==
A revenge tale, and Mithun heading the cast with his Juniors, Armaan Kohli, Faisal Khan and Puru Rajkumar where Roshni Jaffery plays Mithun's wife. This film was shelved then delay released.
