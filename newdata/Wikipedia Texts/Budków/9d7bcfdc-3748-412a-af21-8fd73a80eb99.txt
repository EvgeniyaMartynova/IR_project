Budków, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Budków [ˈbutkuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wińsko, within Wołów County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) west of Wińsko, 20 kilometres (12 mi) north-west of Wołów, and 56 kilometres (35 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 154.
