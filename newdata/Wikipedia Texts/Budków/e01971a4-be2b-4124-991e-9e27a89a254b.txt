Budków, Piotrków County

Budków [ˈbutkuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wola Krzysztoporska, within Piotrków County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Wola Krzysztoporska, 14 km (9 mi) west of Piotrków Trybunalski, and 41 km (25 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.The village has a population of 70.
