Gandu, Sistan and Baluchestan

Gondow Bazar (Persian: گندوبازار‎, also Romanized as Gondow Bāzār and Gandū Bāzār; also known as Gandū and Gondow) is a village in Polan Rural District, Polan District, Chabahar County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 217, in 30 families.
