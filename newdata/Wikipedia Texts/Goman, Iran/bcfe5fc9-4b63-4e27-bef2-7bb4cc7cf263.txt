Gaman, Tarom

Goman Dasht (Persian: گماندشت‎, also Romanized as Gomān Dasht and Gamān Dasht; also known as Gamān, Gumbadasht, and Konbadasht) is a village in Darram Rural District, in the Central District of Tarom County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 128, in 36 families.
