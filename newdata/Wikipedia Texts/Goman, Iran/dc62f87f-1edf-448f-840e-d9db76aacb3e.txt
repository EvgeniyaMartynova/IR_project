Gaman, Tabriz

Gomanj-e Sofla (Persian: گمانج سفلي‎, also Romanized as Gomānj-e Soflá and Gamanj Sofla; also known as Gamān, Gomānāb Pā’īn, Gomāneh Āshāqī, Gomānj-e Pā’īn, Gomānj Pā’īn, Gomyānāb Ashāqī, Gumianāb Ashāghi, Gyumyanab-Ashagy, Kamānj, Kamānj-e Pā’īn, Kamānj-e Soflá, Komanch Pā’īn, and Komānj Pā’īn) is a village in Esperan Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 377, in 96 families.
