Vilkij-e Shomali Rural District

Vilkij-e Shomali Rural District (Persian: دهستان ويلكيج شمالي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Namin County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 5,966, in 1,423 families. The rural district has 20 villages.
