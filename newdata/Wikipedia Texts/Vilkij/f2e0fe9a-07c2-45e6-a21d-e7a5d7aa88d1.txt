Vilkij-e Markazi Rural District

Vilkij-e Markazi Rural District (Persian: دهستان ويلكيج مركزئ‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Vilkij District, Namin County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 14,469, in 2,952 families. The rural district has 20 villages.
