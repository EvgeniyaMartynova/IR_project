Vilkij District

Vilkij District (Persian: بخش ویلکیج‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Namin County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23,940, in 4,930 families.  The District has one city: Abi Beyglu. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Vilkij-e Jonubi Rural District and Vilkij-e Markazi Rural District.
