Vilkij-e Jonubi Rural District

Vilkij-e Jonubi Rural District (Persian: دهستان ويلكيج جنوبي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Vilkij District, Namin County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,229, in 888 families. The rural district has 4 villages.
