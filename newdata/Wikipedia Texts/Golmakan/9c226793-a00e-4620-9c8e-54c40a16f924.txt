Gol Makan, Zanjan

Gol Makan (Persian: گلمكان‎, also Romanized as Gol Makān and Golmakān; also known as Golmeh Gān, Kalmakān, Kol Makān, and Kul-Makan) is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Khodabandeh County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 149, in 32 families.
