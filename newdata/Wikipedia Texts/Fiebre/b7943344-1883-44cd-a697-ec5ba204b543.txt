Fiebre (Ricky Martin song)

"Fiebre" (transl. "Fever") is a song by Puerto Rican singer Ricky Martin featuring Wisin & Yandel. It was released on February 23, 2018, as well as its music video which was filmed in Puerto Vallarta.


== Background ==
This is the second collaboration between Martin and the Puerto Rican duo Wisin & Yandel, following "Frío", released in 2011. Martin also worked with Wisin on "Adrenalina" in 2014 and "Que Se Sienta El Deseo" in 2015.


== Commercial performance ==
"Fiebre" became Martin's 47th entry on Hot Latin Songs, debuting at number 27 and peaking at number 17 in May 2018. The song entered the Latin Airplay chart at number 28 with 6 million in audience, number 3 on Latin Digital Songs (4,000 sold), and collected 911,000 streams. "Fiebre" also entered the Latin Pop Airplay chart at number 38 and peaked number three so far. On Latin Airplay, it moved to number one.
