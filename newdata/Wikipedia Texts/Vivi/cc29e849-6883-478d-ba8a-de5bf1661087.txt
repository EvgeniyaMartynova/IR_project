Río Viví

The Viví River (Spanish: Río Viví) is a river in the U.S. commonwealth of Puerto Rico that runs through the town of Utuado and Adjuntas, Puerto Rico. It is about 11 miles long.The town was to begin work on a bridge that goes over the Viví river in 2019, since its destruction on September 19, 2017 by Hurricane Maria.
