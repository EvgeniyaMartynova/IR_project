Unmatched Brutality Records

Unmatched Brutality is a record label based out of Powell, Tennessee. It specialises in brutal death metal. It started as a small mail order distribution and is growing steadily in the underground death metal scene. Some of its best known bands are Brodequin, Guttural Secrete, Devourment, Retch, Insidious Decrepancy, and Liturgy (New York death metal band not to be confused with the black metal band from Tennessee).
