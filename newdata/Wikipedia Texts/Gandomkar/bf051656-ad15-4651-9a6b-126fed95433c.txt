Gandomkar-e Vosta

Deh-e Azizi Gandomkar-e Vosta (Persian: ده عزيزي گندمكاروسطي‎, also Romanized as Deh-e ʿAzīzī Gandomkār-e Vosţá; also known as Gandomkār-e Vosţá) is a village in Miankuh Rural District, Miankuh District, Ardal County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 152, in 32 families.
