Black River Township, Butler County, Missouri

Black River Township is one of ten townships in Butler County, Missouri, USA.  As of the 2000 census, its population was 1,488.


== Geography ==
Black River Township covers an area of 51.35 square miles (133.0 km2) and contains no incorporated settlements.  It contains eight cemeteries: Black River, Davidson, Harwell, Keele, Magill, Military Crossing, Nunley and Three Springs.
The streams of Aldridge Creek, Hartman Creek, Powers Creek, Swift Creek and Widow Creek run through this township.
