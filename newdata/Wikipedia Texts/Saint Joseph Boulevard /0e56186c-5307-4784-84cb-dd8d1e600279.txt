Saint Joseph Boulevard

Saint Joseph Boulevard (official in French: boulevard Saint-Joseph) is a major boulevard located east of Mount Royal in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. It is mainly residential, but is nevertheless a major east–west artery. It is located in the Plateau Mont-Royal and the Rosemont–La Petite-Patrie boroughs. Its intersection with Iberville Street is known as the infamous Tunnel de la mort (Death Tunnel).
