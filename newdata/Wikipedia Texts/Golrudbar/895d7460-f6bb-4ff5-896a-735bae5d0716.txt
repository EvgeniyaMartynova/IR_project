Pain Mahalleh-ye Golrudbar

Pain Mahalleh-ye Golrudbar (Persian: پائين محله گلرودبار‎, also Romanized as Pā’īn Maḩalleh-ye Golrūdbār and Pā’īn Maḩalleh-ye Gol-e Rūdbār; also known as Galrūdbār) is a village in Baz Kia Gurab Rural District, in the Central District of Lahijan County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 188, in 58 families.
