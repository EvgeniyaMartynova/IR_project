Bala Mahalleh-ye Golrudbar

Bala Mahalleh-ye Golrudbar (Persian: بالامحله گلرودبار‎, also Romanized as Bālā Maḩalleh-ye Golrūdbār and Bālā Maḩalleh-ye Gol-e Rūdbār) is a village in Baz Kia Gurab Rural District, in the Central District of Lahijan County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 209, in 63 families.
