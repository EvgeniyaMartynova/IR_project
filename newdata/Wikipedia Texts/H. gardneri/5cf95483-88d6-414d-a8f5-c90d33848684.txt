Hydrothrix gardneri

Hydrothrix is a monotypic flowering plant genus in the family Pontederiaceae.
The highly modified Hydrothrix gardneri is a submerged aquatic with a two-flowered pseudanthium found in Eastern Brazil. It is used as an aquarium plant.
