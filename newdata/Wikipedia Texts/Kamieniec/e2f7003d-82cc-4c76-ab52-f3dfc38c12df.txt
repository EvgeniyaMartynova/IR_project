Kamieniec Wrocławski

Kamieniec Wrocławski [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s vrɔt͡sˈwafski] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czernica, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Czernica, and 12 kilometres (7 mi) south-east of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has an approximate population of 2,000.
