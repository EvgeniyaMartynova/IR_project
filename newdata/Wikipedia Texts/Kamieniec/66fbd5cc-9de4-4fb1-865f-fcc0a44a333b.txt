Kamieniec, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] (German: Schöningen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kołbaskowo, within Police County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland, close to the German border. It lies approximately 28 kilometres (17 mi) south of Police and 17 km (11 mi) south-west of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 300.
