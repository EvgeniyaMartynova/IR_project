Kamieniec, Toruń County

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zławieś Wielka, within Toruń County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 15 km (9 mi) east of Bydgoszcz and 28 km (17 mi) west of Toruń.
