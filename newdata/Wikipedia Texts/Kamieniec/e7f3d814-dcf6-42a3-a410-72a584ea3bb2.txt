Kamieniec, Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Cewice, within Lębork County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south of Cewice, 15 km (9 mi) south of Lębork, and 60 km (37 mi) west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
