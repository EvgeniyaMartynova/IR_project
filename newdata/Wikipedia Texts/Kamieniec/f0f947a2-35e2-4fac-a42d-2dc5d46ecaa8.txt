Kamieniec, Radziejów County

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Topólka, within Radziejów County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south of Topólka, 22 km (14 mi) south-east of Radziejów, and 64 km (40 mi) south of Toruń.
