Kamieniec, Silesian Voivodeship

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] (German: Kamienitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zbrosławice, within Tarnowskie Góry County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Zbrosławice, 13 km (8 mi) south-west of Tarnowskie Góry, and 27 km (17 mi) north-west of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 829.
