Kamieniec, Aleksandrów County

Kamieniec [kaˈmjɛɲɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Koneck, within Aleksandrów County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) north of Koneck, 8 km (5 mi) south of Aleksandrów Kujawski, and 27 km (17 mi) south of Toruń.
