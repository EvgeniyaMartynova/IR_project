Turów, Gmina Głogów

Turów [ˈturuf] (German: Tauer) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Głogów, within Głogów County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 6 km (4 mi) south-east of Głogów, and 85 km (53 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has an approximate population of 150.
