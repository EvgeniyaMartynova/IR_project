Turów, Lubusz Voivodeship

Turów [ˈturuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogród Bobrzański, within Zielona Góra County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) north of Nowogród Bobrzański and 24 km (15 mi) south-west of Zielona Góra.
