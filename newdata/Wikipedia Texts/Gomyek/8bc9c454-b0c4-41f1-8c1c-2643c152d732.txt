Gomyek-e Vosta

Gomyek-e Vosta (Persian: گميك وسطي‎, also Romanized as Gomyek-e Vosţá; also known as Gomīk and Gom Yek) is a village in Holayjan Rural District, in the Central District of Izeh County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 92, in 17 families.
