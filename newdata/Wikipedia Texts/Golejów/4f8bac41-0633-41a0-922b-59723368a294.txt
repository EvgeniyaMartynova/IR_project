Golejów, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Golejów [ɡɔˈlɛjuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubomierz, within Lwówek Śląski County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Lubomierz, 11 kilometres (7 mi) south of Lwówek Śląski, and 103 kilometres (64 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
