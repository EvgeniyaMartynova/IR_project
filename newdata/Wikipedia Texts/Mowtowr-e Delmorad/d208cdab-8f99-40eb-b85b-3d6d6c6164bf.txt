Mowtowr-e Delmorad, Dalgan

Mowtowr-e Delmorad (Persian: موتور دلمراد‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Delmorād) is a village in Dalgan Rural District, in the Central District of Dalgan County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 84, in 19 families.
