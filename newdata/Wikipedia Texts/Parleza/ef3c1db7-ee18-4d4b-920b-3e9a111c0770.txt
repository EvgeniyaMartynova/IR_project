Parleza Wielka

Parleza Wielka [parˈlɛza ˈvjɛlka] (German: Groß Parlösen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Biskupiec, within Olsztyn County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of Biskupiec and 34 km (21 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 179.
