Buczek, Białogard County

Buczek [ˈbut͡ʂɛk] (German: Butzke) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Białogard, within Białogard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-east of Białogard and 122 km (76 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
