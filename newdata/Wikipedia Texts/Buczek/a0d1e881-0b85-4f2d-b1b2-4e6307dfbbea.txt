Buczek, Brzeziny County

Buczek [ˈbut͡ʂɛk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brzeziny, within Brzeziny County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Brzeziny and 16 km (10 mi) east of the regional capital Łódź.
