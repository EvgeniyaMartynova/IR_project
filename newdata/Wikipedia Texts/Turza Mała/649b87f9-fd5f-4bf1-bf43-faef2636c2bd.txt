Turza Mała, Mława County

Turza Mała [ˈtuʐa ˈmawa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lipowiec Kościelny, within Mława County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-east of Lipowiec Kościelny, 8 km (5 mi) west of Mława, and 112 km (70 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
