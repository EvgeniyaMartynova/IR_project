Turza Mała, Płock County

Turza Mała [ˈtuʐa ˈmawa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brudzeń Duży, within Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Brudzeń Duży, 23 km (14 mi) north-west of Płock, and 117 km (73 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
