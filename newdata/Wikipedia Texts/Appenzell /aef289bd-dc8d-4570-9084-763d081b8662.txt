Appenzell Creek

Appenzell Creek is a 11.8-mile-long (19.0 km) tributary of McMichael Creek in Monroe County, Pennsylvania in the United States.
Its headwaters feed into Trout Lake in Jackson Township at the Poconos. The creeks course then meanders in a southwestern direction and joins McMichael Creek just above of Sciota.
