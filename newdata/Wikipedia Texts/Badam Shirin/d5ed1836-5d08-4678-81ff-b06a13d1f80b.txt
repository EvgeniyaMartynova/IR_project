Badam Shirin, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari

Badam Shirin (Persian: بادام شيرين‎, also Romanized as Bādām Shīrīn; also known as Bādām Shīrīndān) is a village in Doab Rural District, Bazoft District, Kuhrang County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
