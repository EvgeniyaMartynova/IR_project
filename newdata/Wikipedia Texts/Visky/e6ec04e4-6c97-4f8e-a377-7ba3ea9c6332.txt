Vísky (Rokycany District)

Vísky is a village and municipality (obec) in Rokycany District in the Plzeň Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 1.37 square kilometres (0.53 sq mi), and has a population of 42 (as at 2 October 2006).
Vísky lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) south-east of Rokycany, 25 km (16 mi) south-east of Plzeň, and 73 km (45 mi) south-west of Prague.
