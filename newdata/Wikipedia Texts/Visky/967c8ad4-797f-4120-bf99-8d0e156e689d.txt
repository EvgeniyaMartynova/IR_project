Vísky (Blansko District)

Vísky is a village and municipality (obec) in Blansko District in the South Moravian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.78 square kilometres (1.46 sq mi), and has a population of 233 (as at 3 July 2006).
Vísky lies approximately 19 kilometres (12 mi) north of Blansko, 38 km (24 mi) north of Brno, and 170 km (106 mi) east of Prague.
