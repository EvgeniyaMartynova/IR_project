Vilusi, Nikšić

Vilusi (Montenegrin and Serbian Cyrillic: Вилуси) is a village in the Nikšić Municipality of Montenegro. It is the village closest the Klobuk border crossing with Bosnia and Herzegovina (for Trebinje) though the actual main road does not go through the heart of the village.
According to the 2011 census, the village was inhabited by 87 Montenegrins and 75 Serbs. According to the 2003 census, the village was inhabited by 113 Serbs and 91 Montenegrins.
