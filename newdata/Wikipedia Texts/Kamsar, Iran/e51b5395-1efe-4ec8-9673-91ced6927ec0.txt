Kamsar, Gilan

Kamsar (Persian: كمسر‎, also Romanized as Kamsār and Komsār; also known as Komsara, Kūchak Komsār, and Kūmsār) is a village in Gasht Rural District, in the Central District of Fuman County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 232, in 67 families.
