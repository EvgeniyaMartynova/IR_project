Mowtowr-e Gami, Khash

Mowtowr-e Gami (Persian: موتورگمي‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Gamī) is a village in Gowhar Kuh Rural District, Nukabad District, Khash County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
