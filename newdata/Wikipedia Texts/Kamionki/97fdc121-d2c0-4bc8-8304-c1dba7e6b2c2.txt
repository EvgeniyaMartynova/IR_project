Kamionki, Giżycko County

Kamionki [kaˈmjɔnki] (German: Steintal) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Giżycko, within Giżycko County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-west of Giżycko and 82 km (51 mi) north-east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945, the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
