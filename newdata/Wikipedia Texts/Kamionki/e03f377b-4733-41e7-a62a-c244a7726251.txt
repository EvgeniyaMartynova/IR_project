Kamionki, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamionki [kaˈmjɔnki] (German: Unter Schöningen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kołbaskowo, within Police County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland, close to the German border. It lies approximately 26 kilometres (16 mi) south of Police and 15 km (9 mi) south-west of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
