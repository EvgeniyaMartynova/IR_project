Bob Herman

Robert Stephen Herman (born 30 November 1946 in Southampton, Hampshire) was an English cricketer who played County Cricket for Middlesex from 1965 to 1971 and Hampshire from 1972 to 1977. He played in South Africa for Border cricket team in 1972/3 and for Griqualand West cricket team in 1974/5. He also played in the Minor Counties Championship for Dorset in 1978 and 1979. Between 1979 and 1982, he umpired 61 first-class cricket matches.
