Michael Henderson (fencer)

Michael Henderson is a former New Zealand fencer.
He won the bronze medal as part of the men's sabre team at the 1962 British Empire and Commonwealth Games. His teammates in the event were Bob Binning and Brian Pickworth.
Henderson competed individually and in teams in the sabre, épée and foil at the 1962, 1966, and 1970 Commonwealth Games.
