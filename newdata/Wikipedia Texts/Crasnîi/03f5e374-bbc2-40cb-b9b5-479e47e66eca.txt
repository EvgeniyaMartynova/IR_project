Crasnîi Octeabri

Crasnîi Octeabri (Ukrainian: Красний Октябр, Krasnyi Oktiabr, Russian: Красный Октябрь) is a commune in the Camenca sub-district of Transnistria, Moldova. It is composed of two villages, Alexandrovca (Александровка) and Crasnîi Octeabri. It is currently under the administration of the breakaway government of the Transnistrian Moldovan Republic.
