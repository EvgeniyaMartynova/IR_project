Crasnîi Vinogradari

Crasnîi Vinogradari (Ukrainian: Красний Виноградар, Krasnyi Vynohradar, Russian: Красный Виноградарь, Krasnyi Vinogradar) is a commune in the Dubăsari sub-district of Transnistria, Moldova. It is composed of five villages: Afanasievca (Афанасіївка, Афанасьевка), Alexandrovca Nouă (Нова Олександрівка, Новая Александровка), Calinovca (Калинівка, Калиновка), Crasnîi Vinogradari and Lunga Nouă (Нова Лунга, Новая Лунга). It is currently under the administration of the breakaway government of the Transnistrian Moldovan Republic.
