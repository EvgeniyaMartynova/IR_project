Crawford Township, Washington County, Arkansas

Township of Crawford is one of thirty-seven townships in Washington County, Arkansas, USA. As of the 2000 census, its total population was 800.


== Geography ==

According to the United States Census Bureau, Crawford Township covers an area of 23.8 square miles (62 km2); all land. Crawford Township was created in 1878 from part of West Fork Township. It gave part to Lee's Creek Township in 1880.
