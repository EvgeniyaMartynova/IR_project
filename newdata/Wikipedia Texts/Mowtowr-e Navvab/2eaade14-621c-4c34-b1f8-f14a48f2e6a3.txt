Mowtowr-e Navvab, Dalgan

Mowtowr-e Navvab (Persian: موتورنواب‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Navvāb) is a village in Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem Rural District, Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem District, Dalgan County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
