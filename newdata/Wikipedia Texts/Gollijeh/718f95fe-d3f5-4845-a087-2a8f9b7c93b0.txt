Gollijeh-ye Olya

Gollijeh-ye Olya (Persian: گليجه عليا‎, also Romanized as Gollījeh-ye ‘Olyā; also known as Golījeh, Goljeh, Gollījeh, Gollījeh-ye Emām (Persian: گليجه امام), Gollūjeh, Guludzhan, and Gulūja) is a village in Bonab Rural District, in the Central District of Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 41, in 13 families.
