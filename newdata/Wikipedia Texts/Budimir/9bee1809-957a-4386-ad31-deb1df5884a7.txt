Budimir Janošević

Budimir Janošević (Serbian Cyrillic: Будимир Јаношевић, Serbo-Croatian pronunciation: [budimir janǒːʃeʋitɕ]; born 21 October 1989) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for AIK in the Swedish first tier Allsvenskan.
His only appearance for the Serbian national under-21 team came on 18 November 2009 in a friendly match against Denmark U21.
