Budimir Đukić

Budimir Đukić (Serbian Cyrillic: Будимиp Ђукић; also transliterated Djukić; born 26 July 1977 in Kragujevac) is a Serbian footballer. He currently plays as a midfielder for FK Šumadija Radnički 1923. His first club was FK Radnički Kragujevac, that in the beginning of the 2009/10 season, together with FK Šumadija 1903, formed a new club, his current club, FK Šumadija Radnički 1923.
