Golina, Jarocin County

Golina [ɡɔˈlina] (German: Golina, 1939-45: Annenhof) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jarocin, within Jarocin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south of Jarocin and 67 km (42 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 1,700.
