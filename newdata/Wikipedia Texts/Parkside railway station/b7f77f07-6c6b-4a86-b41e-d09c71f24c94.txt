Parkside railway station (Derbyshire)

Parkside railway station is the only station on the Derbyshire Dales Narrow Gauge Railway. Derbyshire Dales Narrow Gauge Railway is a short narrow-gauge railway operating at Rowsley South, and operates ex-industrial diesels and carriages similar to the Golden Valley light railway at the Midland Railway Centre. Presently all services on the DDNGR are cancelled until further notice.
