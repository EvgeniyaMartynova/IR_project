Vilovataya (tributary of Vyala)

Vilovataya is a river in the Murmansk region of Russia. It flows through Tersky District of Murmansk Oblast. It is a tributary of Vyala. The river is 11 km (6.8 mi) long.
Originating in Small Lambina lake, the river flows through the forest and marshland. It passes through the Upper and Lower Vilovatoe lakes, emptying into the right side of Vyala River 31 km (19 mi) from its mouth at a height of 80.5 m (264 ft) above sea level. There are no settlements on the river.
According to the Russian state registry of water, it belongs to the "Barents and White Sea basin district".
