Kamran Khan (squash player)

Kamran Khan, (born 22 February 1990 in England) is a professional squash player who represented Malaysia. He reached a career-high world ranking of World No. 56 in January 2010.He is the son to Jansher Khan, the former world No. 1 professional Pakistani squash player. His Malaysian mother divorced with his father and brought Kamran back from Pakistan to Malaysia to make a living. He is of Pashtun descent.
