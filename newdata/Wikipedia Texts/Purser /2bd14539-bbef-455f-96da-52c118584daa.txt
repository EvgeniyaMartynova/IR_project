Purser, California

Purser is a former settlement in Lassen County, California. It was located 3 miles (4.8 km) west of Wendel.A post office operated at Purser from 1902 to 1903, and from 1908 to 1915, when the service was transferred to Wendel. The name honored Edward T. Purser, rancher and irrigation developer.
