Chris Turner (footballer, born 1959)

Chris Turner is a New Zealand former footballer who represented the New Zealand national team at the international level.Turner scored on full All Whites debut in a 3–0 win over Fiji on 3 July 1979 and ended his international playing career with eight A-international caps and one goal to his credit, his final cap an appearance in a 6–1 win over Solomon Islands on 29 February 1980 before an injury to his knee ended his international career.
