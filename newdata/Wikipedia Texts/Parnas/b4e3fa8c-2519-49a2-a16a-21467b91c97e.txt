Maya Parnas

Maya Ivanovna Parnas (Russian: Майя Ивановна Парнас, Romanian: Maia Parnas; born 15 May 1974 in Tiraspol, Moldavian SSR) is a Transnistrian politician and was the former acting Prime Minister of Transnistria, temporarily replacing Tatiana Turanskaya, who was focusing on running in the 2015 elections.
