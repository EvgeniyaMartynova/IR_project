Bílý Kámen

Bílý Kámen [ˈbiːliː ˈkaːmɛn] is a village and municipality (obec) in Jihlava District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 5.12 square kilometres (1.98 sq mi), and has a population of 206 (as at 28 August 2006).
Bílý Kámen lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Jihlava and 106 km (66 mi) south-east of Prague.
