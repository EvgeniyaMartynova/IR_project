Kámen (Havlíčkův Brod District)

Kámen is a village and municipality (obec) in Havlíčkův Brod District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 11.55 square kilometres (4.46 sq mi), and has a population of 392 (as at 28 August 2006).
Kámen lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) north of Havlíčkův Brod, 36 km (22 mi) north of Jihlava, and 89 km (55 mi) south-east of Prague.
