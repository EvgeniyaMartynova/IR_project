Kámen (Pelhřimov District)

Kámen is a village and municipality (obec) in Pelhřimov District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 8.07 square kilometres (3.12 sq mi), and has a population of 280 (as at 28 August 2006).
Kámen lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) west of Pelhřimov, 42 km (26 mi) west of Jihlava, and 86 km (53 mi) south-east of Prague.
