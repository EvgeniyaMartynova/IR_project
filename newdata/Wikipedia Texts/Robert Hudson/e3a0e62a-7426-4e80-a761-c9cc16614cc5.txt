Bob Hudson (offensive lineman)

Robert Willard Hudson (born April 5, 1930) is a former American football offensive lineman in the National Football League for the New York Giants, the Philadelphia Eagles, and the Washington Redskins.  Hudson also played in the American Football League for the Dallas Texans and the Denver Broncos.  He played college football at Clemson University and was drafted in the 12th round of the 1951 NFL Draft.
Currently living in Rowlett, Texas.
