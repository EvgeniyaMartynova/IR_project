Kamtuleh-ye Yusefabad

Kamtuleh-ye Yusefabad (Persian: كمتوله يوسف اباد‎, also Romanized as Kamtūleh-ye Yūsefābād) is a village in Howmeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Ramhormoz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 146, in 31 families.
