Gołkowo, West Pomeranian Voivodeship

Gołkowo [ɡɔu̯ˈkɔvɔ] (German: Birkenfelde) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Rymań, within Kołobrzeg County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
