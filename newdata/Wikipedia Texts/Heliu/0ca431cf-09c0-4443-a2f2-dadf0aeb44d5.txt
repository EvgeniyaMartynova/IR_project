Heliu, Shandong

Heliu (Chinese: 河流; pinyin: Héliú) is a town of Yangxin County in northwestern Shandong province, China, located about 6 kilometres (3.7 mi) southeast of the county seat. As of 2011, it has 78 villages under its administration.
