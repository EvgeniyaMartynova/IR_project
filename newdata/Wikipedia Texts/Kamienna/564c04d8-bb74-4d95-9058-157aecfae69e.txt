Kamienna, Pomeranian Voivodeship

Kamienna [kaˈmjɛnna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Prabuty, within Kwidzyn County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Prabuty, 20 km (12 mi) north-east of Kwidzyn, and 71 km (44 mi) south-east of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 100.
