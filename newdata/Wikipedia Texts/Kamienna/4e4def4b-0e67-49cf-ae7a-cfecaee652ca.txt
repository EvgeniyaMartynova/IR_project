Kamienna, Lubusz Voivodeship

Kamienna [kaˈmjɛnna] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Sława, within Wschowa County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Sława, 20 km (12 mi) west of Wschowa, and 38 km (24 mi) east of Zielona Góra.
