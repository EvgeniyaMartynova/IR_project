Kamienna, Piotrków County

Kamienna [kaˈmjɛnna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wola Krzysztoporska, within Piotrków County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Wola Krzysztoporska, 13 km (8 mi) south-west of Piotrków Trybunalski, and 51 km (32 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
