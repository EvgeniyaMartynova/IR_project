Viștea Mare

Viștea Mare (Romanian pronunciation: [ˈviʃte̯a ˈmare]) is a mountain peak in the Făgăraș Mountains of the Southern Carpathians of Brașov County in Romania, with an elevation of 2,527 metres (8,291 ft). It is the third highest peak in Romania after Moldoveanu Peak (2544m) and Negoiu Peak (2535m).
