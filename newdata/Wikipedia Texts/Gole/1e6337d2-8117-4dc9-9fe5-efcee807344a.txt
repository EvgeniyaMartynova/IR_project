Gole, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship

Gole [ˈɡɔlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubień Kujawski, within Włocławek County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) west of Lubień Kujawski, 28 km (17 mi) south of Włocławek, and 78 km (48 mi) south-east of Toruń.
