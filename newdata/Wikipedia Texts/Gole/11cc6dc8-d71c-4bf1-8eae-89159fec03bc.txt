Gole, Masovian Voivodeship

Gole [ˈɡɔlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Baranów, within Grodzisk Mazowiecki County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Baranów, 11 km (7 mi) north-west of Grodzisk Mazowiecki, and 35 km (22 mi) west of Warsaw.
