Budy, Białystok County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Michałowo, within Białystok County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus. It lies approximately 20 kilometres (12 mi) south-east of Michałowo and 50 km (31 mi) south-east of the regional capital Białystok.
