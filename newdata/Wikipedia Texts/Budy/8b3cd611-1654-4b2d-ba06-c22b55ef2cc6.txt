Budy, Gmina Bobrowo

Budy [ˈbudɨ] (German: Schöndorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bobrowo, within Brodnica County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Bobrowo, 6 km (4 mi) west of Brodnica, and 54 km (34 mi) north-east of Toruń.
