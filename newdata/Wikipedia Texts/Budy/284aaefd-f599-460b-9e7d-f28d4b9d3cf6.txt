Budy, Staszów County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bogoria, within Staszów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-east of Bogoria, 15 km (9 mi) north-east of Staszów, and 60 km (37 mi) south-east of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of  199.


== Demography ==
According to the 2002 Poland census, there were 195 people residing in Budy village, of whom 49.7% were male and 50.3% were female. In the village, the population was spread out with 26.2% under the age of 18, 34.4% from 18 to 44, 21% from 45 to 64, and 18.5% who were 65 years of age or older.
