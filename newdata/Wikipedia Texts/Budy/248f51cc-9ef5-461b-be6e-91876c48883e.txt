Budy, Krotoszyn County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rozdrażew, within Krotoszyn County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Rozdrażew, 18 km (11 mi) north-east of Krotoszyn, and 80 km (50 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
