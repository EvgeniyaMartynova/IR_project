Budy, Złotów County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] (German: Jagdhaus) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jastrowie, within Złotów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) west of Jastrowie, 27 km (17 mi) west of Złotów, and 112 km (70 mi) north of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 110.
