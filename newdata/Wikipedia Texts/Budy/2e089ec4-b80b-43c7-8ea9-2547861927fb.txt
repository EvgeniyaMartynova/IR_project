Budy, Mońki County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Trzcianne, within Mońki County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Trzcianne, 16 km (10 mi) west of Mońki, and 50 km (31 mi) north-west of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 10.
