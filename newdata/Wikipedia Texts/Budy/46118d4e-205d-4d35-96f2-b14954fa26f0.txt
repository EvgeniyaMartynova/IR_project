Budy, Grajewo County

Budy [ˈbudɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rajgród, within Grajewo County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Rajgród, 18 km (11 mi) east of Grajewo, and 72 km (45 mi) north-west of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 90.
