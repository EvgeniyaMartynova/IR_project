Badarah-ye Sofla

Badarah-ye Sofla (Persian: بادره سفلي‎, also Romanized as Bādarah-ye Soflá; also known as Bādarah) is a village in Kuhdasht-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Kuhdasht County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 21, in 4 families.
