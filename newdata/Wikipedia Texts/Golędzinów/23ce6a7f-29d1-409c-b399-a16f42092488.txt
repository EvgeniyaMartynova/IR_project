pl:Golędzinów

Golędzinów [ɡɔlɛnˈd͡ʑinuf] (German: Kunzendorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Oborniki Śląskie, within Trzebnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Oborniki Śląskie, 11 kilometres (7 mi) south-west of Trzebnica, and 18 kilometres (11 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
