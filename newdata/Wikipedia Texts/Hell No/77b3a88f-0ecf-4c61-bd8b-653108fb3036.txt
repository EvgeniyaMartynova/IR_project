Hell No (Leave Home)

"Hell No (Leave Home)" is a song by American singer Monica. It was written by Bryan Michael Cox, Sean Garrett, and Carl Mitchell and co-produced by Cox and Garrett, and features guest vocals by rapper Twista for her fourth studio album, The Makings of Me (2006). The song was released as the album's fourth and final single on May 14, 2007 (see 2007 in music) in the United States during the second quarter of 2007 (see 2007 in music). With a lack of promotion from the record label, the single failed to chart on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100. It reached a peak position of number 114 on the U.S. Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.
