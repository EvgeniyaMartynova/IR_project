Gangothri (film)

Gangothri is a 1997 Indian Malayalam film, directed by S. Anil and produced by S. Satheesh. The film stars Suresh Gopi, Thilakan, Ratheesh and Rajan P. Dev in the lead roles. The film has musical score by S. P. Venkatesh. The film was dubbed into Telugu as Political War.
