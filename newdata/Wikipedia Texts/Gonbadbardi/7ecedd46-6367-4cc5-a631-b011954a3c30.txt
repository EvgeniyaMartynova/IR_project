Gonbadbardi-ye Olya

Gonbadbardi-ye Olya (Persian: گنبدبردي عليا‎, also Romanized as Gonbadbardī-ye ‘Olyā) is a village in Bahmai-ye Garmsiri-ye Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Bahmai County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 311, in 64 families.
