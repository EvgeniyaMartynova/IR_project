WLWE

WLWE (1360 AM, "Eagle Sports 1360") is a radio station licensed to serve Roanoke, Alabama.  The station is owned by Eagle's Nest, Inc.  It airs a sports format.The station was assigned the WELR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission. The station changed its call sign to the current WLWE on September 16, 2013.
