Budkov (Třebíč District)

Budkov is a village and municipality (obec) in Třebíč District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 17.29 square kilometres (6.68 sq mi), and has a population of 375 (as at 2005).
Budkov lies approximately 24 kilometres (15 mi) south-west of Třebíč, 39 km (24 mi) south of Jihlava, and 147 km (91 mi) south-east of Prague.
