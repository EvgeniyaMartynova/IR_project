Bad Axe High School

Bad Axe High School is a high school located in Bad Axe, Michigan.  Their mascot is the Hatchets.  It is the only high school in the district but one of three schools in the Bad Axe Public Schools District.  Roughly 500 students, including eighth through twelfth graders, attend the high school and it employs twenty-three teachers, as of the 2014-2015 school year. The motto of the high school is "S.T.R.I.V.E", or "Successful Thoughtful Respectful Involved Visionary and Efficient".
The school building opened in 1996, consists of media center, cafetorium and gymnasium in the center with two main wings branching out in a "V" shape.
