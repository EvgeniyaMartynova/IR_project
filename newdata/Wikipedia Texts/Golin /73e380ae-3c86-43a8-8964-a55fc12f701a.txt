Golin, Myślibórz County

Golin [ˈɡɔlin] (German: Gollin) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogródek Pomorski, within Myślibórz County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Nowogródek Pomorski, 15 km (9 mi) east of Myślibórz, and 67 km (42 mi) south-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 60.
