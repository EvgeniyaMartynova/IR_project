Lino Golin

Lino Golin (born January 31, 1945 in Soave) is an Italian former footballer who made nearly 250 appearances in the Italian professional leagues. He played 6 seasons (65 games, 7 goals) in Serie A for Milan, Varese and Foggia.


== Honours ==
Serie A champion: 1967/68.
Coppa Italia winner: 1971/72, 1972/73.
UEFA Cup Winners' Cup winner: 1972/73.
Intercontinental Cup winner: 1969.
