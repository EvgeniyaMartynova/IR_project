Baczyn, Sucha County

Baczyn [ˈbat͡ʂɨn] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Budzów, within Sucha County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north-east of Sucha Beskidzka and 34 km (21 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kraków.
The village has a population of 840.
