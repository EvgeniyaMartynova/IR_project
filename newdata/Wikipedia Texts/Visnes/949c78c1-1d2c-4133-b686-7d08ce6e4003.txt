Visnes, Møre og Romsdal

Visnes is a small village in Eide Municipality in Møre og Romsdal county, Norway.  It is located along the Kornstadfjorden, about 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) north of the municipal center of Eide.
Visnes is home to Visnes Kalk AS, a limestone quarry.
