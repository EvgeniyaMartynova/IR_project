Ganj Darreh-ye Olya

Ganj Darreh-ye Olya (Persian: گنج دره عليا‎, also Romanized as Ganj Darreh-ye ‘Olyā; also known as Ganjereh-ye ‘Olyā) is a village in Mirbag-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 241, in 50 families.
