Ganj Darreh-ye Sofla

Ganj Darreh-ye Sofla (Persian: گنج دره سفلي‎, also Romanized as Ganj Darreh-ye Soflá; also known as Ganjereh-ye Vostā) is a village in Mirbag-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 74, in 18 families.
