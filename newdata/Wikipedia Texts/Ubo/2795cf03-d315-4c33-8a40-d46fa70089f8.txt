Balud, Masbate

Balud, officially the Municipality of Balud,  is a 4th class municipality in the province of Masbate, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 38,124 people.The name Balud came from the name of a bird.

== Demographics ==
In the 2015 census, the population of Balud, Masbate, was 38,124 people, with a density of 170 inhabitants per square kilometre or 440 inhabitants per square mile.
