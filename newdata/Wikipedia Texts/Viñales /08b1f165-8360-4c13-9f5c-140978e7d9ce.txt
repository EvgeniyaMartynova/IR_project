Isaac Viñales

Isaac Viñales Mares (born 6 November 1993) is a Grand Prix motorcycle racer from Spain. He is the cousin of former Moto3 World Champion and current MotoGP racer Maverick Viñales, and has previously competed in the Spanish 125GP Championship and the Spanish Moto2 series.
