Crandall Farm Complex

Crandall Farm Complex is a historic home and farm complex located at Cazenovia in Madison County, New York.  The frame farmhouse was built about 1870 and is a two-story, frame residence in the vernacular Italianate style. Also on the property are two barns, carriage house, privy, shed, and cobblestone well house.It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1987.
