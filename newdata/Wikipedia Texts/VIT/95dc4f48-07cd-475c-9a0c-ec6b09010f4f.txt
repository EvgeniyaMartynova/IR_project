Venkateshwara Institute of Technology

Venkateshwara Institute of Technology (VIT) is an engineering college located on NH-58 Meerut-Delhi Bypass, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India.  The college is affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow.


== History ==
The institute was established in 2007. The educationist Er. Sudheer Giri is the founding chairman of the institute.
