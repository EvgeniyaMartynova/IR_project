Walker Buckner House

The Walker Buckner House, in Bourbon County, Kentucky near Paris, Kentucky, was built in 1841.  It has also been known as Buknore and as Locust Grove.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997.It was designed by self-proclaimed architect Matthew Kennedy, and like some other of his works was built by Butler & DeJarnett.
