Buckner House (Marshall, Missouri)

Buckner House is a historic home located at Marshall, Saline County, Missouri. It was built in 1906, and is a two-story, three bay, Classical Revival style frame dwelling with a hipped roof. It measures 48 feet by 48 feet and rests on a cut stone and concrete foundation. The front facade features an elaborate double porch.It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
