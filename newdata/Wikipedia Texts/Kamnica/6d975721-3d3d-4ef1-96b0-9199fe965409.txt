Kamnica, Poland

Kamnica [kamˈnit͡sa] (German: Kamnitz) is a village in Gmina Miastko, Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 42 kilometres (26 mi) south-west of Bytów and 120 km (75 mi) south-west of Gdańsk (capital city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship). 
From 1975 to 1998 the village was in Słupsk Voivodeship. 
It has a population of 373.
