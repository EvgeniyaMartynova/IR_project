Mowtowr-e Safar, Chabahar

Mowtowr-e Safar (Persian: موتور صفر‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr-e Şafar) is a village in Pir Sohrab Rural District, in the Central District of Chabahar County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 7 families.
