Haathkadi (1982 film)

Haathkadi is a 1982 Hindi-language Indian feature film directed by Surendra Mohan, starring Sanjeev Kumar, Shatrughan Sinha, Reena Roy & Ranjeeta Kaur in pivotal role. This movie contains some super hit songs, especially, the song "Disco Station Disco" is one of the most popular & super hit disco songs in Indian film industry.


== Plot ==
Haathkadi is a suspense thriller.
