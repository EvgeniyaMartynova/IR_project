Golestan Rural District (Golestan Province)

Kerend Rural District (Persian: دهستان كرند‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Dashli Borun District, Gonbad-e Qabus County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 7,607, in 1,567 families.  The rural district has 5 villages.
