Buckhorn Township, Brown County, Illinois

Buckhorn Township is one of nine townships in Brown County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 98 and it contained 73 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 37.95 square miles (98.3 km2), of which 37.86 square miles (98.1 km2) (or 99.76%) is land and 0.08 square miles (0.21 km2) (or 0.21%) is water.

=== Cemeteries ===
The township contains these eight cemeteries: Benville, Bixler, Bowman, Butler, DeHart, Dobey, Linn and Morrelville.
