Bucze, Żary County

Bucze [ˈbut͡ʂɛ] (formerly German Buchwalde) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przewóz, within Żary County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland, close to the German border.It lies approximately 24 kilometres (15 mi) south-west of Żary and 67 km (42 mi) south-west of Zielona Góra.
The village has a population of 100.
