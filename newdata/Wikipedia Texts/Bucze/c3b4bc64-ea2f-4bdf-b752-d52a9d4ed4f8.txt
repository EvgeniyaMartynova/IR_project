Bucze, Świebodzin County

Bucze [ˈbut͡ʂɛ] (German Wutschdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubrza, within Świebodzin County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Lubrza, 13 km (8 mi) west of Świebodzin, 40 km (25 mi) north of Zielona Góra, and 50 km (31 mi) south of Gorzów Wielkopolski.
The village has a population of 280.
