Bucze, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Bucze [ˈbut͡ʂɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Grębocice, within Polkowice County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Grębocice, 17 kilometres (11 mi) north-east of Polkowice, and 79 kilometres (49 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
