HMCS Ville de Quebec

Several Canadian naval units have been named HMCS Ville de Quebec (meaning "Quebec City" in English).

HMCS Ville de Quebec (K242) (I) was a Flower-class corvette that served in the Royal Canadian Navy during the Second World War.
HMCS Ville de Québec (FFH 332) (II) is a Halifax-class frigate serving the Canadian Forces from 1993 to present.


== Battle honours ==
Atlantic, 1942–1944.
Gulf of St. Lawrence, 1942.
Mediterranean, 1943.
English Channel, 1944–1945.
