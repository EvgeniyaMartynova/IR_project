Great Midwest Marijuana Harvest Festival

The Great Midwest Marijuana Harvest Festival is the longest running cannabis rights festival in the United States, held annually in Madison, Wisconsin since 1971.The festival was initiated and organized by Yippie and cannabis activist Ben Masel until his death in 2011.In 2014, the 44th annual festival was held at Library Mall. The same space was used in 2016.
