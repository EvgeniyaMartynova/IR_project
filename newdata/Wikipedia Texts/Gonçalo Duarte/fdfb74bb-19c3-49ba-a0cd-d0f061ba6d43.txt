Gonçalo Duarte (footballer, born 1998)

Gonçalo Duarte Amaral Sousa (born 19 April 1998) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays for Académico de Viseu F.C. as a midfielder.


== Football career ==
On 12 May 2018, Duarte made his professional debut with Académico Viseu in a 2017–18 LigaPro match against Santa Clara.
