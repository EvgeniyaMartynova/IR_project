Vista Tower (Kuala Lumpur)

Vista Tower (formerly Empire Tower) is part of an integrated development known as The Intermark. Phase 1 of the development includes the Vista Tower, retail podium and a Doubletree by Hilton Hotel, the first in Southeast Asia.Integra Tower, a 41-storey office building, is expected to be completed mid-2012.
