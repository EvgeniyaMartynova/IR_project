James Buckley House

The James Buckley House is a historic house located on Joseph Street in Cape Vincent, Jefferson County, New York.


== Description and history ==
It is a ​1 1⁄2-story frame structure built in about 1845 in the Gothic Cottage style. The central section is flanked by one-story wings with parapets hiding the shed roof.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on September 27, 1985.
