Vítonice (Znojmo District)

Vítonice is a village and municipality (obec) in Znojmo District in the South Moravian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 5.75 square kilometres (2.22 sq mi), and has a population of 244 (as at 28 August 2006).
Vítonice lies approximately 16 kilometres (10 mi) north-east of Znojmo, 43 km (27 mi) south-west of Brno, and 183 km (114 mi) south-east of Prague.
