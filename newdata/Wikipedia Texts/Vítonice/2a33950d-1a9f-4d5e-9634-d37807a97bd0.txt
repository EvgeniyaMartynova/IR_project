Vítonice (Kroměříž District)

Vítonice is a village and municipality (obec) in Kroměříž District in the Zlín Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 7.65 square kilometres (2.95 sq mi), and has a population of 460 (as at 3 July 2006).
Vítonice lies approximately 28 kilometres (17 mi) north-east of Kroměříž, 24 km (15 mi) north of Zlín, and 246 km (153 mi) east of Prague.
