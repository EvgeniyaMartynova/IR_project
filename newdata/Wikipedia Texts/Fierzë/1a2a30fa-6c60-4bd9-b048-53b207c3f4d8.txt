Fierzë, Fushë-Arrëz

Fushë-Arrëz is a town and a municipality in Shkodër County, northern Albania. It was formed at the 2015 local government reform by the merger of the former municipalities Blerim, Fierzë, Fushë-Arrëz, Iballë and Qafë-Mali, that became municipal units. The seat of the municipality is the town Fushë-Arrëz. The total population is 7,405 (2011 census), in a total area of 540.77 km2. The population of the former municipality at the 2011 census was 2,513. The town sits along a long street, connecting shops, flats, homes and other establishments.
