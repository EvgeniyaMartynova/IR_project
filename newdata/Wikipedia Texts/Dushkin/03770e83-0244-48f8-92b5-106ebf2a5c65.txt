Pavel Dushkin

Pavel Vyacheslavovich Dushkin (Russian: Павел Вячеславович Душкин; born 27 January 1995) is a Russian football midfielder.
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Metallurg Lipetsk on 16 July 2013 in a game against FC Vityaz Podolsk.
