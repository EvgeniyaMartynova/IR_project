Kamýk (Litoměřice District)

Kamýk (German: Kamaik) is a village and municipality (obec) in Litoměřice District in the Ústí nad Labem Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 5.77 square kilometres (2.23 sq mi), and has a population of 139 (as of 31 December 2007).
Kamýk lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Litoměřice, 11 km (7 mi) south of Ústí nad Labem, and 59 km (37 mi) north-west of Prague.
