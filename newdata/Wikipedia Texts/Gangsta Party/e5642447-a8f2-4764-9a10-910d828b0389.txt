Gangsta Party (Yo Gotti song)

Back 2 da Basics is the fifth studio album by American rapper Yo Gotti. It was released on May 23, 2006, by TVT Records, serving as Yo Gotti's second and final studio release with TVT.


== Track listing ==
Sample credits"I Got Dem" contains samples of "Fireman" performed by Lil Wayne, and "I Got That Work" performed by Big Tymers.
"Gangsta Party" contains a sample of "Far Cry", which was written and performed by Marvin Gaye.
