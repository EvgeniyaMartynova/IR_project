Snow Lake (Idaho)

Snow Lake is an alpine lake in Custer County, Idaho, United States, located in the White Cloud Mountains in the Sawtooth National Recreation Area.  Although no trails lead to the lake, it can be accessed from Sawtooth National Forest trail 601.Snow Lake is southeast of D. O. Lee Peak, upstream of Cove Lake, and in the same basin as Gentian and Boulder Lakes.
