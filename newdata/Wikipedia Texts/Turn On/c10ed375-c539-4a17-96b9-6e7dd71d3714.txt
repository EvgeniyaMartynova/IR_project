Turn on (The Beat Box)

"Turn On (The Beat Box)" is a single by R&B/funk band Earth, Wind & Fire issued in 1988 by Columbia Records. The single rose to No. 26 on the Billboard Hot Soul Singles chart.


== Overview ==
Turn on (The Beat Box) was written by Maurice White, Rhett Lawrence and Martin Page. The song also came upon EWF's 1988 compilation album The Best of Earth, Wind & Fire, Vol. 2.Additionally Turn on (The Beat Box) appeared on the soundtrack of the feature film Caddyshack II.
