So Good (soy beverage)

So Good (also known as SoGood or So-Good) is a brand of fortified non-dairy beverages that are lactose, cholesterol and gluten-free. So Good is manufactured by Sanitarium in Australia and New Zealand. In Canada it is prepared by Earth's Own. So Good sold in the United Kingdom is manufactured by So Good International Ltd.
In Australia, So Good produces not only soy milk, but also almond milk and almond & coconut milk. They also produce flavored soy milk and frozen soy desserts.
