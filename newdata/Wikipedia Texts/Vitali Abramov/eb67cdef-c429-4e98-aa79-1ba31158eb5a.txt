Vitaliy Abramov

Vitaliy Sergeyevich Abramov (Russian: Виталий Серге́евич Абрамов; born 12 July 1974) is a Kazakhstani professional football coach and a former player. He is an assistant coach with FC Rotor-2 Volgograd. He also holds Russian citizenship.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Premier League in 1995 for FC Tekstilshchik Kamyshin.


== Honours ==
Russian Premier League runner-up: 1997.
Russian Premier League bronze: 1996.
Ukrainian Premier League champion: 2002.
Ukrainian Premier League runner-up: 2000, 2001.


== European club competitions ==
With FC Rotor Volgograd.

UEFA Intertoto Cup 1996: 8 games, 3 goals.
UEFA Cup 1997–98: 5 games, 1 goal.
UEFA Cup 1998–99: 2 games, 1 goal.
