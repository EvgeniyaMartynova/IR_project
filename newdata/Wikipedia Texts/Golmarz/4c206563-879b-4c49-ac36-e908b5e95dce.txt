Golmarz-e Sofla

Golmarz-e Sofla (Persian: گلمرز سفلی‎, also Romanized as Golmarz-e Soflá) is a village in Bash Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
