Kamionka, Wieluń County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pątnów, within Wieluń County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Pątnów, 7 km (4 mi) south of Wieluń, and 91 km (57 mi) south-west of the regional capital Łódź.
