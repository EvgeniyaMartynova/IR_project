Kamionka, Bytów County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Studzienice, within Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) east of Studzienice, 13 km (8 mi) east of Bytów, and 68 km (42 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
