Kamionka, Wieruszów County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bolesławiec, within Wieruszów County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Bolesławiec, 10 km (6 mi) south-east of Wieruszów, and 107 km (66 mi) south-west of the regional capital Łódź.
