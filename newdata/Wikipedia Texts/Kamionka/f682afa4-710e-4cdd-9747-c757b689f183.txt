Kamionka, Sieradz County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Burzenin, within Sieradz County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Burzenin, 22 km (14 mi) south of Sieradz, and 66 km (41 mi) south-west of the regional capital Łódź.The village has a population of 253.
