Kamionka, Golub-Dobrzyń County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radomin, within Golub-Dobrzyń County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Golub-Dobrzyń and 36 km (22 mi) east of Toruń.
The village has an approximate population of 120.
