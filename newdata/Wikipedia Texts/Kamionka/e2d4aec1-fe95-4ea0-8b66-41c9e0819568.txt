Kamionka, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kozłów, within Miechów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south of Kozłów, 12 km (7 mi) north of Miechów, and 45 km (28 mi) north of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 420.
