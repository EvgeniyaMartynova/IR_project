Kamionka, Stargard County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German Glashagen) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Chociwel, within Stargard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Chociwel, 30 km (19 mi) north-east of Stargard, and 57 km (35 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
The settlement has a population of 4.
Between 1871 and 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
