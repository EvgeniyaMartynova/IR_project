Kamionka, Ostróda County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German: Steinsdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Morąg, within Ostróda County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) west of Morąg, 28 km (17 mi) north of Ostróda, and 48 km (30 mi) west of the regional capital Olsztyn.
