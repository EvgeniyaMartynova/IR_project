Kamionka, Nidzica County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German: Steintal) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nidzica, within Nidzica County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of Nidzica and 53 km (33 mi) south of the regional capital Olsztyn.
