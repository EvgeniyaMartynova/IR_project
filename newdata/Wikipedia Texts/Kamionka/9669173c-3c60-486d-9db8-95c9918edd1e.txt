Kamionka, Olsztyn County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German: Königlich Kamiontken; 1931-1945: Steinau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Biskupiec, within Olsztyn County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) east of Biskupiec and 40 km (25 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
