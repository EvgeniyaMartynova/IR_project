Kamionka, Konin County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kleczew, within Konin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-west of Kleczew, 24 km (15 mi) north-west of Konin, and 80 km (50 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 210.
