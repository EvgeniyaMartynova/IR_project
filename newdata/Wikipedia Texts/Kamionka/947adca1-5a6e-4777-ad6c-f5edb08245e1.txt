Kamionka, Biłgoraj County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Księżpol, within Biłgoraj County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Księżpol, 17 km (11 mi) south of Biłgoraj, and 95 km (59 mi) south of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 12.
