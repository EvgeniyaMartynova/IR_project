Kamionka, Chodzież County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German: Kamionke) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chodzież, within Chodzież County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Chodzież and 69 km (43 mi) north of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 64.
