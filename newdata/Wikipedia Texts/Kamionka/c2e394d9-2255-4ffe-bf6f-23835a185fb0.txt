Kamionka, Chojnice County

Kamionka [kaˈmjɔnka] (German: Heidefließ) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czersk, within Chojnice County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 132.
