Bad Trip (film)

Bad Trip is an upcoming American hidden camera comedy film directed by Kitao Sakurai and produced by Eric Andre, David Bernad, Ruben Fleischer, and Jeff Tremaine. The film, which stars Andre, Lil Rel Howery and Tiffany Haddish, is scheduled to be released on April 24, 2020 by Orion Pictures.


== Premise ==
Directed by Kitao Sakurai, Andre's helmer on his Adult Swim series The Eric Andre Show, the prank comedy follows two best friends on a cross-country road trip full of inventive pranks, pulling its real-life audience into the mayhem.


== Cast ==
Eric Andre
Lil Rel Howery
Tiffany Haddish


== Production ==
During the conceptualization of the film, Andre attended Robert McKee's Story Seminars and consulted Jeff Tremaine and Nathan Fielder. Andre also screened the film before release for Sacha Baron Cohen.


== Release ==
It is scheduled to be released on April 24, 2020. It was previously scheduled to be released on October 25, 2019 and February 28, 2020.
