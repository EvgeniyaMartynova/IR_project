Robert Hopkins (screenwriter)

Robert E. Hopkins (September 21, 1886, Ottawa, Kansas – December 22, 1966, Hollywood, California) was a screenwriter. He was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Story for the 1936 film San Francisco.


== Partial filmography ==

The Better 'Ole (1926)
Señorita (1927)

Wickedness Preferred (1928)
The Smart Set (1928)
Spite Marriage (1929)
Chasing Rainbows (1930)
San Francisco (1936)
