Edgar Villamarín

Edgar Villamarín Arguedas (born 1 April 1982) is a Peruvian footballer. He currently plays for Universidad César Vallejo in the Peruvian Segunda División, as a defender.


== International career ==
Villamarín also featured four times for the Peru national team.
