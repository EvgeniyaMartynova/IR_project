Crane Creek Township, Mason County, Illinois

Crane Creek Township is located in Mason County, Illinois. As of the 2010 census, its population was 136 and it contained 73 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 34.2 square miles (89 km2), of which 34.01 square miles (88.1 km2) (or 99.44%) is land and 0.19 square miles (0.49 km2) (or 0.56%) is water.
