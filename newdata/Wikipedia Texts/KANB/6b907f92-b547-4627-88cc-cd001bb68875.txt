KANB-LP

KANB-LP (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a religious format. Licensed to Kalispell, Montana, United States, the station is currently owned by Flathead Adventist Radio, Inc., an ownership division of the Three Angels Broadcasting Network. It carries 3ABN's radio network without any local continuity or programming.
