Kamon, Israel

Kamon (Hebrew: כַּמּוֹן, lit. Cumin) is a community settlement in northern Israel. Located in the Galilee on the two eastern peaks of Mount Kammon, it falls under the jurisdiction of Misgav Regional Council. In 2018 it had a population of 1,384.


== History ==
The village was established in 1980 by seven families, who initially lived in mobile homes.
