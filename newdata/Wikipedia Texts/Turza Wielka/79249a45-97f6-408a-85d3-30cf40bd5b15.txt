Turza Wielka, Mława County

Turza Wielka [ˈtuʐa ˈvjɛlka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lipowiec Kościelny, within Mława County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Lipowiec Kościelny, 9 km (6 mi) west of Mława, and 113 km (70 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
