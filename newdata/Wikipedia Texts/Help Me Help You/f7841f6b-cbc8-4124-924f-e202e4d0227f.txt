Help Me Help You (song)

"Help Me Help You" is the debut single of American internet personality Logan Paul featuring American boy band Why Don't We. It was released on May 18, 2017, alongside a music video on YouTube starring Shay Mitchell.As of January 2019, the music video for the song has over 240 million views.The song debuted at number 113 on the US Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart, eventually peaking at number five on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart, the equivalent of number 105 on the Billboard Hot 100.


== Music video ==
The music video was uploaded on Paul's personal YouTube account on May 18, 2017.


== Digital Download ==
The song was still only uploaded to the Logan Paul Spotify artist account. The song can also be downloaded from Apple Music.
