Uchqoʻrgʻon

Uchqoʻrgʻon (Russian: Учкурган, Uchkurgan) is a city and seat of Uchqoʻrgʻon District in Namangan Province in eastern Uzbekistan. In 1989 it had a population of 23,772.In 1969 Uchqoʻrgʻon was granted city status. The city has cotton-cleaning and oil-extracting factories.
