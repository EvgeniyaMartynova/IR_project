Badamestan, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari

Badamestan (Persian: بادامستان‎, also Romanized as Bādāmestān) is a village in Shalil Rural District, Miankuh District, Ardal County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
