Badamestan-e Mashhadiamir

Badamestan-e Mashhadiamir (Persian: بادامستان مشهدي امير‎, also Romanized as Bādāmestān-e Mashhadīāmīr) is a village in Barez Rural District, Manj District, Lordegan County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 31, in 5 families.
