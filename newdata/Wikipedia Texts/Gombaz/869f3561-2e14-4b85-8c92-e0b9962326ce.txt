Gombaz, Sistan and Baluchestan

Gonbad-e Alavi (Persian: گنبدعلوي‎, also Romanized as Gonbad-e ‘Alavī; also known as Gombaz, Gonbad, Gumbaz, and Gunbad) is a village in Dalgan Rural District, in the Central District of Dalgan County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 512, in 80 families.
