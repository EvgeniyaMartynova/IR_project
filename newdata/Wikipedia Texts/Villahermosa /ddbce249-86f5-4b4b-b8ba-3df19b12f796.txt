Villa Hermosa

Villa Hermosa is a town in the La Romana province of the Dominican Republic. Most of the crime that happens in La Romana occur in Villa Hermosa. 
Villa Hermosa is seen as the most dangerous neighborhood within the province. Tourist are recommended not to visit this area.
