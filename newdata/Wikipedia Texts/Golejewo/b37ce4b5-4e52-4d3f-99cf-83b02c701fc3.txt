Golejewo, Greater Poland Voivodeship

Golejewo [ɡɔlɛˈjɛvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pakosław, within Rawicz County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) west of Pakosław, 12 km (7 mi) east of Rawicz, and 88 km (55 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
