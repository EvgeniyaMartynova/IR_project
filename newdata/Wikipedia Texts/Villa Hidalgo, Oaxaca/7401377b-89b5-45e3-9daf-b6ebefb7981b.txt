Chalcatongo de Hidalgo

Chalcatongo de Hidalgo (also, Chalcatongo and Villa Hidalgo) is a municipality in the Mexican state of Oaxaca. It is part of the Tlaxiaco District in the south of the Mixteca Region.It is the birthplace of former Governor Ulises Ruiz Ortiz.
