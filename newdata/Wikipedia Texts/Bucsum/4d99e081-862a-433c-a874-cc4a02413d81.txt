Sântămăria-Orlea

Sântămăria-Orlea (Hungarian: Őraljaboldogfalva) is a commune in Hunedoara County, Romania. It is composed of nine villages: Balomir (Balomir), Bărăștii Hațegului (Baresd), Bucium-Orlea (Bucsum), Ciopeia (Csopea), Săcel (Szacsal), Sânpetru (Szentpéterfalva), Sântămăria-Orlea, Subcetate (Hátszegváralja) and Vadu (Vád).


== Sânpetru Formation ==

Near Sânpetru, one of the villages comprising the commune, Sânpetru Formations (Sânpetru Beds) are located.
