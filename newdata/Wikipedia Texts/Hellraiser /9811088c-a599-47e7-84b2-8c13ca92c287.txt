Hollowman

Hollowman is an EP released by Entombed on Earache Records in 1993. The 1996 reissue featured bonus track "God of Thunder" which was previously released on Out of Hand single.
The track "Serpent Speech" was covered later by French technical death metal band Scarve on their album Luminiferous.
