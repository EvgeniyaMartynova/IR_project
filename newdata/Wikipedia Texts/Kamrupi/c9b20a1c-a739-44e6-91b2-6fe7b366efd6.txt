Kamrupi Dholiya

Kamrupi Dholiya is a professional drummers from Kamrup. Though associated with playing dhols or drums, are too known for mixing acting and acrobatic skills with same. Spontaneity of expressions and quickness of mind and feet were the hallmarks of Kamrupi Dholiya's. Drumming is accompanied by songs and acting skills.
