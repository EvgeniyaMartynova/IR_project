Kamienica, Podlaskie Voivodeship

Kamienica [kamjɛˈnit͡sa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Janów, within Sokółka County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-east of Janów, 19 km (12 mi) north-west of Sokółka, and 42 km (26 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 200.
