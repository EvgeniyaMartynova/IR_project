Kamienica, Bytów County

Kamienica [kamjɛˈnit͡sa] (German: Kamenz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Borzytuchom, within Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Borzytuchom, 17 km (11 mi) north-west of Bytów, and 90 km (56 mi) west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
