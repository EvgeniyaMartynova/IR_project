Kamienica, Silesian Voivodeship

Kamienica [kamjɛˈnit͡sa] (German Kaminitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Woźniki, within Lubliniec County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 23 km (14 mi) east of Lubliniec and 43 km (27 mi) north of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 666.
