Kamienica, Miechów County

Kamienica [kamjɛˈnit͡sa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gołcza, within Miechów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Gołcza, 11 km (7 mi) west of Miechów, and 33 km (21 mi) north of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 277.
