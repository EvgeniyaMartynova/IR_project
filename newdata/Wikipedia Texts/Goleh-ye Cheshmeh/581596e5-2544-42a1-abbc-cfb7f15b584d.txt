Galleh Cheshmeh, Razavi Khorasan

Galleh Chashmeh (Persian: گله چشمه‎; also known as Gul-i-Chashmeh and Gol Cheshmeh) is a village in Pain Velayat Rural District, Razaviyeh District, Mashhad County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
