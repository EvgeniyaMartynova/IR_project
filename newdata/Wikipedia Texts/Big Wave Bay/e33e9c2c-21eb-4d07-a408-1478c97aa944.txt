Tai Long Wan, Chi Ma Wan

For other places with the same name in Hong Kong, see Tai Long Wan (disambiguation).Tai Long Wan (Chinese: 大浪灣; literally: 'Big Wave Bay') is a bay at the south of the Chi Ma Wan Peninsula on Lantau Island, Hong Kong. It is the next bay east of Yi Long Wan (二浪灣), where the Sea Ranch is located.
