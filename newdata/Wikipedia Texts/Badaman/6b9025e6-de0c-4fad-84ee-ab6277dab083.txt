Badaman, Kerman

Badamuiyeh (Persian: باداموئيه‎, also Romanized as Bādāmū’īyeh; also known as Bādāman, Bādāmīyeh, Bādāmlū, and Badammo’iyeh) is a village in Ekhtiarabad Rural District, in the Central District of Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 334, in 96 families.
