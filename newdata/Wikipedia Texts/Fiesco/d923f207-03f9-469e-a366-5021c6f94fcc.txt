Fiesco, Lombardy

Fiesco (Cremunés: Fièsc) is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Cremona in the Italian region Lombardy, located about 50 kilometres (31 mi) southeast of Milan and about 30 kilometres (19 mi) northwest of Cremona.  
Fiesco borders the following municipalities: Castelleone, Izano, Salvirola, Trigolo.
