== Biography ==
Huber was born on August 29, 1920 in Eckelson, North Dakota. Later, Huber graduated from West Allis Central High School in West Allis, Wisconsin. He was a member of the Knights of Columbus and the Society of the Holy Name. He died on October 20, 1991.


== Career ==
Huber was first elected to the Assembly in 1948. He was a Democrat.
