Vilémovice (Havlíčkův Brod District)

Vilémovice (German: Wilimowitz) is a village and municipality (obec) in Havlíčkův Brod District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.84 square kilometres (1.48 sq mi), and has a population of 220 (as at 3 July 2006).
Vilémovice lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-west of Havlíčkův Brod, 38 km (24 mi) north-west of Jihlava, and 79 km (49 mi) south-east of Prague.
