Crater Peak (Colorado)

Crater Peak is the highest summit of Grand Mesa in the Rocky Mountains of North America.  The prominent 11,333-foot (3,454 m) peak is located on the drainage divide separating Grand Mesa National Forest and Gunnison National Forest, 12.3 miles (19.8 km) north-northwest (bearing 342Â°) of the Town of Paonia in Delta County, Colorado, United States.
