Mega Movie Magic

Mega Movie Magic is an American television program that aired on Discovery Channel as part of its Sunday-morning lineup  kids-targeted lineup.  The program explored special effects used in movies. It was produced by GRB Entertainment. The program was nominated for a CableAce Award in 1998, and "drew healthy ratings" in its first season.
