UDR, Inc.

UDR Inc. (formerly United Dominion Realty) is a publicly traded real estate investment trust that invests in apartments. The company is organized in Maryland with its headquarters in Highlands Ranch, Colorado. As of December 31, 2017, the company owned interests in 127 apartment communities containing 39,998 apartment units.The company was founded in Highlands Ranch, Colorado in 1972. It became a public company via an initial public offering in January 1978 and was added to the S&P 500 Index in March 2016.
