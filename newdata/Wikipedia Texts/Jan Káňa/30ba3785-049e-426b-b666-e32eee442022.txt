Jan Káňa (ice hockey, born 1992)

Jan Káňa (born January 4, 1992) is a Czech professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for Olomouc HC of the Czech Extraliga.Káňa made his Czech Extraliga debut playing with HC Kometa Brno during the 2011-12 Czech Extraliga season.
