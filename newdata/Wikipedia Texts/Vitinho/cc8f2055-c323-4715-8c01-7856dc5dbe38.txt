Vitinho (footballer, born July 1999)

Victor Alexander da Silva (born 23 July 1999, Belo Horizonte), commonly known as Vitinho, is a Brazilian footballer who currently plays as a right back for Cercle Brugge.


== Club career ==
Vitinho arrived at Cruzeiro Esporte Clube at the age of six. In the 2018 U-20 Copa Libertadores, he scored two goals in three matches, and was promoted to the first squad. Soon after, Brazil U20 coach Carlos Amadeu, called up Vitinho for the Brazilian Under-20 National team.
