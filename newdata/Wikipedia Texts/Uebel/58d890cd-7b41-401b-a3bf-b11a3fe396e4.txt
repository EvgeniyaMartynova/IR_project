Lars Uebel

Lars Uebel (born 14 October 1980) is a retired German tennis player and coach.Uebel has a career high ATP singles ranking of 242 achieved on 20 March 2006. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 168 achieved on 9 January 2006.
Uebel made his ATP main draw debut at the 2005 Ordina Open in the singles draw facing Guillermo Coria.
