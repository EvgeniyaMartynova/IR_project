The Visitation (Magnum album)

The Visitation is the 16th studio album by the English rock band Magnum, released in 2011 by SPV. The album registered the following chart positions: Germany No. 19, Sweden No. 28, UK No. 55, and Switzerland No. 56.The cover was painted by Rodney Matthews. It refers to the track Doors to Nowhere, in which Tony Clarkin looks back at his childhood.


== Track listing ==
All tracks are written by Tony Clarkin.


== Personnel ==

Bob Catley — vocals
Al Barrow — bass guitar
