Fish baronets

The Fish Baronetcy, of Lissameon in the County of Cavan, was a title in the Baronetage of Ireland.  It was created on 12 February 1622 for John Fish.  The title became extinct on the death of the third Baronet in circa 1670.


== Fish baronets, of Lissameon (1622) ==
Sir John Fish, 1st Baronet (died 1623)
Sir Edward Fish, 2nd Baronet (c. 1598 – 1658)
Sir Edward Fish, 3rd Baronet (c. 1628 – c. 1670)
