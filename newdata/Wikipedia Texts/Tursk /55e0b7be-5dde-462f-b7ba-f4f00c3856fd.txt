Tursk, Lubusz Voivodeship

Tursk [tursk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sulęcin, within Sulęcin County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south of Sulęcin, 39 km (24 mi) south of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and 58 km (36 mi) north-west of Zielona Góra.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
