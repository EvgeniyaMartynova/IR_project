Kamimachi Station

Kamimachi Station (上町駅, Kamimachi-eki) is a stop on the Setagaya Line by Tokyu Corporation and is located in Segataya, Tokyo, Japan.


== Station layout ==
There are two side platforms on two tracks either side of a level crossing.
