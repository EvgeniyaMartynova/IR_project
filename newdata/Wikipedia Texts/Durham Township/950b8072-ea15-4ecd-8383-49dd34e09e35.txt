Durham Township, Hancock County, Illinois

Durham Township is one of twenty-four townships in Hancock County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 278 and it contained 129 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 36.93 square miles (95.6 km2), of which 36.91 square miles (95.6 km2) (or 99.95%) is land and 0.03 square miles (0.078 km2) (or 0.08%) is water.
