Gołocin, Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Gołocin [ɡɔˈwɔt͡ɕin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chojnów, within Legnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Chojnów, 14 kilometres (9 mi) west of Legnica, and 76 kilometres (47 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
