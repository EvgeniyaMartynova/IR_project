Apradhi Kaun? (1982 film)

Apradhi Kaun? is a 1982 Bollywood film.
It is considered as the most underrated suspense thriller. Along with being a hit film, it also marked the debut of Javed Khan as "Anand".


== Story ==
This is a suspense thriller. The story revolves around a young boy, Anand, who is wrongfully accused of the murder of a lady, Madhvi, who was bored of her marriage and eyed Anand. Anand is blackmailed with pictures and threats. The story has a sensational ending.
