Amt Golzow

Amt Golzow is an Amt ("collective municipality") in the district of Märkisch-Oderland, in Brandenburg, Germany. Its seat is in Golzow.
The Amt Golzow consists of the following municipalities:

Alt Tucheband
Bleyen-Genschmar
Golzow
Küstriner Vorland
Zechin
