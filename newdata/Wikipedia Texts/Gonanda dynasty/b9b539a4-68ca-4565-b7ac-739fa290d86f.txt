Gonanda dynasty of Kashmir III

The Gonanda dynasty III, was the last dynasty of Gonanda line of rulers of Kashmir; was established by Yudhisthira II. This dynasty was preceded by Gonanda dynasty of Kashmir I (80 B.C.-252 A.D.), Gonanda dynasty of Kashmir II and followed by Karkoṭa dynasty of Kashmir.
