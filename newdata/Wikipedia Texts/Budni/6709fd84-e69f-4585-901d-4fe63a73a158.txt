Budhni railway station

Budhni railway station is a small railway station of Budhni city, Madhya Pradesh. Its code is BNI. Budhni is a small broad gauge railway station of Bhopal-Itarsi. Budhni is well connected to Chhindwara, Indore, Bhopal, Gorakhpur Junction, Jhansi, Itarsi, Amritsar and Mumbai. The station consist of two platforms.The platforms are not well sheltered. It lacks many facilities including water and sanitation.


== Trains ==
These following trains stop at Budhni:
Panchvalley Fast Passenger
Jhansi-Itarsi Passenger
Pathankot Express
