Vítor Pereira (footballer, born 1985)

Vítor Manuel Ferreira Pereira (born 8 January 1985 in Chaves) is a Portuguese footballer who plays for C.D.C. Montalegre as a central defender.
Pereira spent most of his career in the lower leagues of his country, appearing in the second division for S.C. Freamunde (11 games in the 2007–08 season) and hometown club G.D. Chaves (two matches in the 2009–10 campaign, team relegation).
