Ganjabad-e Olya, East Azerbaijan

Ganjabad-e Olya (Persian: گنج ابادعليا‎, also Romanized as Ganjābād-e ‘Olyā; also known as Ganjābād-e Bālā) is a village in Nazarkahrizi Rural District, Nazarkahrizi District, Hashtrud County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 23 families.
