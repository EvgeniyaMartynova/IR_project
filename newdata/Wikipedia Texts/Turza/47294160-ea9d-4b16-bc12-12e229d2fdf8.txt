Turza, Greater Poland Voivodeship

Turza [ˈtuʐa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Damasławek, within Wągrowiec County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Damasławek, 20 km (12 mi) east of Wągrowiec, and 63 km (39 mi) north-east of the regional capital Poznań.
The village has a population of 360.
