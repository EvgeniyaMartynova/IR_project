Turza, Lubliniec County

Turza [ˈtuʐa] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Herby, within Lubliniec County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north-east of Lubliniec and 58 km (36 mi) north of the regional capital Katowice.
The settlement has a population of 2.
