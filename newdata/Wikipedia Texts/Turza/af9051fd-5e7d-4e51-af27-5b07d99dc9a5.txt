Turza, Opole Voivodeship

Turza [ˈtuʐa] (German: Thursy) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dobrodzień, within Olesno County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Dobrodzień, 18 km (11 mi) south of Olesno, and 31 km (19 mi) east of the regional capital Opole.
The village has a population of 22.
