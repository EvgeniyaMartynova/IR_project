Turza, Zawiercie County

Turza [ˈtuʐa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łazy, within Zawiercie County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Łazy, 6 km (4 mi) south-west of Zawiercie, and 36 km (22 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
