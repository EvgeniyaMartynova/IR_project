Turza, Lesser Poland Voivodeship

Turza [ˈtuʐa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, within Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of Rzepiennik Strzyżewski, 31 km (19 mi) south of Tarnów, and 88 km (55 mi) east of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 1,100.
