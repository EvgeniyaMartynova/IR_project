Golovinsky District

Golovinsky District  (Russian: Головинский райо́н) is an administrative district (raion) of Northern Administrative Okrug, and one of the 125 raions of Moscow, Russia.  The area of the district is 8.806 square kilometers (3.400 sq mi).
