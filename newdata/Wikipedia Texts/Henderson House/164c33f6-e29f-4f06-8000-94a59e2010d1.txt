Otway Henderson House

Otway Henderson House is a historic home located at McCormick in McCormick County, South Carolina.  It was built about 1889, and is a 1 1/2-story, frame dwelling in a vernacular Second Empire style.  It features a dual-pitched mansard roof and a one-story porch with rectangular posts and railing. It was built by Otway Henderson, a prominent cotton farmer of the county.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.
