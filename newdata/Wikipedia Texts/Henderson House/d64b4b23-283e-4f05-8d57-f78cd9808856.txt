Isham Henderson House

The Isham Henderson House, on Main Cross Rd. in New Castle in Henry County, Kentucky, was built c.1830.  It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1987.  The listing included two contributing buildings (a stone house and a frame dependency) and a contributing structure (a root cellar).The house is a six-bay one-story hipped roof dry stone house with a rear ell.  It has nine-stone voussoirs without keystones.  It had Federal/Greek transitional-style woodwork.
