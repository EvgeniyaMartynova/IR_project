SMS Möwe

Two ships of the Imperial German Navy and one of the Austro-Hungarian Navy have been named SMS Möwe:

SMS Möwe (1879), a German gunboat launched in 1879
SMS Möwe (1907), an Austro-Hungarian Kaiman-class torpedo boat launched in 1907
SMS Möwe (1914), a German commerce raider during World War I, launched as Pungo in 1914
