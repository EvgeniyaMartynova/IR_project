Villemer, Seine-et-Marne

Villemer is a commune in the Seine-et-Marne department in the Île-de-France region in north-central France.


== Geography ==
The commune is traversed by the Lunain river.


== Demographics ==
Inhabitants of Villemer are called Villemériannaisois.
