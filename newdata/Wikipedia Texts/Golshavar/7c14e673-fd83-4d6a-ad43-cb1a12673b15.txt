Golshavar, Minab

Golshavar (Persian: گلشوار‎, also Romanized as Golshavār, Golashvār, and Goleshvār; also known as Gūshvār) is a village in Howmeh Rural District, in the Central District of Minab County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 458, in 87 families.
