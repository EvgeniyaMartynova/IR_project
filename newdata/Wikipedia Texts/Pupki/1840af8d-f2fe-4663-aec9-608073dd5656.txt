Pupki, Olsztyn County

Pupki ([Ëˆpupki]; translation: Small Bottoms) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jonkowo, within Olsztyn County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Jonkowo and 19 km (12 mi) north-west of the regional capital Olsztyn.
