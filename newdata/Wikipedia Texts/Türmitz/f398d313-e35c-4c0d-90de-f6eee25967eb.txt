Ciermięcice

Ciermięcice [t͡ɕɛrmjɛnˈt͡ɕit͡sɛ] (Czech: Trmantice, German: Türmitz or formerly Tirmanz) is a village located in Poland, in the Opole Voivodeship, Głubczyce County and Gmina Głubczyce.Through the village flows the Potok Ciermięcicki (Czech: Trmantický potok or "Thirmický potok", German: Türmitzer Bach), the left tributary of the Opava river in Krnov.
