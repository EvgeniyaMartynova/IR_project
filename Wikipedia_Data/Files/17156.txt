Title: Zaborze, Pułtusk County
Aspects: {'Zaborze'}

Zaborze [zaˈbɔʐɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pokrzywnica, within Pułtusk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Pokrzywnica, 9 km (6 mi) south of Pułtusk, and 46 km (29 mi) north of Warsaw.
