Title: I Am You (EP)
Aspects: {'I Am You'}

I Am You (stylized as I am YOU) is the third extended play by South Korean boy group Stray Kids. The EP was released digitally and physically on October 22, 2018, by JYP Entertainment and distributed through Iriver. A showcase titled Stray Kids Unveil: Op. 03: I Am You was held the day before the release at Olympic Hall. The album sold 76,547 physical copies in the month of October.The album was released in two versions-an “I am” version and a “YOU” version.
