Title: Qalandarabad, Kerman
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad (Persian: قلندراباد‎, also Romanized as Qalandarābād) is a village in Gonbaki Rural District, Gonbaki District, Rigan County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
