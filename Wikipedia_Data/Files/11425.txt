Title: Qaleh-ye Madraseh, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari
Aspects: {'Qaleh Madreseh'}

Qaleh-ye Madraseh (Persian: قلعه مدرسه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Madraseh) is a village in Barez Rural District, Manj District, Lordegan County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 677, in 126 families.
