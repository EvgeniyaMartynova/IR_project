Title: Zabrodzie, Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Żarnowiec, within Zawiercie County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 33 kilometres (21 mi) east of Zawiercie and 68 km (42 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
