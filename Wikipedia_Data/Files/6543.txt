Title: Samsung Galaxy J5
Aspects: {'J5'}

The Samsung Galaxy J5 is an Android smartphone produced by Samsung Electronics. It was unveiled and released in June 2015. It has an advanced 64-bit class system on a chip (SoC) backed by 1.5 GB RAM and uses a Snapdragon 410.
The Galaxy J5 has a 13 megapixels rear camera with LED flash, f/1.9 aperture, auto-focus and a 5 megapixels wide-angle front facing camera which can extend up to 120Â°, also equipped with LED flash.


== Specifications ==


=== Hardware ===
The phone is powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 410 chipset, a 1.2 GHz processor, Adreno 306 GPU and 1.5 GB RAM with 8 GB of internal storage and a 2600 mAh battery. The J500G comes with a 2100 mAh battery. The Samsung Galaxy J5 is fitted with a 5-inch HD Super AMOLED display. The South Korean variant adds support for T-DMB & Smart DMB. The phone has a metallic chassis and a plastic back cover.According to the BLE Checker app, the model supports Bluetooth Low Energy devices.


=== Software ===
This phone comes with Android 5.1.1, which is upgradable to Android 6.0.1. It supports 4G LTE with dual SIM enabled 4G and Voice over LTE. It also supports Samsung Knox. On 2016 the phone received update to support samsung S Bike Mode
All models later received an optional update to Android 7.1.1 based on official Samsung firmware from the A530F via a custom update.
