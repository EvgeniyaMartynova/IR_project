Title: Záchlumí (Ústí nad Orlicí District)
Aspects: {'Záchlumí'}

Záchlumí is a village in the Ústí nad Orlicí District, Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. It is located 5 km west of Žamberk and has a population of 803 (2006).
