Title: Qaleh Kandi, Kaleybar
Aspects: {'Qaleh Kandi'}

Qaleh Kandi (Persian: قلعه كندي‎, also Romanized as Qal’eh Kandī and Qal‘eh Kandī) is a village in Abish Ahmad Rural District, Abish Ahmad District, Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 817, with 193 families.
