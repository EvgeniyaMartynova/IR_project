Title: WAAO-LD
Aspects: {'WAAO'}

WAAO-LD is a low-powered television station that is licensed to Andalusia, Alabama. Owned by Three Notch Communications, who also operates country music-formatted radio station WAAO-FM, the TV station broadcasts a digital signal on UHF channel 40. The station is an affiliate of the Retro Television Network.Although Andalusia is in the Montgomery-Selma DMA, WAAOâ€™s signal mainly covers the southernmost reaches of that media market, along with nearby areas of neighboring DMAs. Although the signal does not reach the principal cities of the market areas, they include those served by full-power stations in Dothan, Alabama, the Mobile-Pensacola area, and Panama City, Florida.
