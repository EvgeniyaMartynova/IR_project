Title: German submarine U-28
Aspects: {'U-28'}

U-28 may refer to one of the following German submarines:

SM U-28 (Germany), was a Type U 27 submarine launched in 1913 and that served in the First World War until sunk on 2 September 1917
During the First World War, Germany also had these submarines with similar names:
SM UB-28, a Type UB II submarine launched in 1916 and surrendered on 24 November 1918
SM UC-28, a Type UC II submarine launched in 1916 and surrendered on 12 February 1919
German submarine U-28 (1936), a Type VIIA submarine that served in the Second World War until sunk at pier on 17 March 1944; later raised, but stricken 4 August 1944
German submarine U-28 (S177), a Type 206 submarine of the Bundesmarine that was launched in 1974 and scrapped in 1996
