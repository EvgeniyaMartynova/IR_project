Title: Te Kari Waaka
Aspects: {'Waaka (disambiguation)'}

Te Kari Waaka (6 March 1916 – 28 November 1991) was a notable New Zealand Ringatu minister, community leader. Of Māori descent, he identified with the Ngati Pukeko and Tuhoe iwi. He was born in Poroporo, Bay of Plenty, New Zealand in 1916.
