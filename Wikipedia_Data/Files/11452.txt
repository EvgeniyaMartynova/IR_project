Title: Qaleh Sangi, Lorestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Sangi'}

Qaleh Sangi (Persian: قلعه سنگي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Sangī) is a village in Robat Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 979, in 199 families.
