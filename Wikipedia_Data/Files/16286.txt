Yadanarbon Football Club (Burmese: ရတနာပုံ ဘောလုံး အသင်း [jədənàbòʊɴ]) is a Burmese football club, based at the Bahtoo Stadium, in Mandalay, Myanmar. The club was a founding member of the Myanmar National League in 2009. In the first round of the 2017 domestic league season, Yadanabon FC drew a home attendance of 4000, the highest in that round.
