Title: Qaleh Khalifeh, Lorestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Khalifeh'}

Qaleh Khalifeh (Persian: قلعه خليفه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Khalīfeh and Qal‘eh-ye Khalīfeh; also known as Qal‘eh Ḩanīfeh) is a village in Japelaq-e Gharbi Rural District, Japelaq District, Azna County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
