Title: Żabno, Greater Poland Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brodnica, within Śrem County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Brodnica, 15 km (9 mi) north-west of Śrem, and 25 km (16 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
