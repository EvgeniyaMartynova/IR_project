Title: Zabłocie, Rawa County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sadkowice, within Rawa County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Sadkowice, 18 km (11 mi) south-east of Rawa Mazowiecka, and 71 km (44 mi) east of the regional capital Łódź.
