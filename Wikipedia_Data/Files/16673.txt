Title: Z Camelopardalis
Aspects: {'Z Cam'}

Z Camelopardalis (Z Cam) is a cataclysmic variable star in the constellation of Camelopardalis.  It has an apparent visual magnitude which varies between 10.0 and 14.5.  It is the prototype star for the family of Z Camelopardalis variable stars, dwarf novae with standstills at a brightness intermediate between their maxima and minima.Z Camelopardalis was discovered photographically in 1904 by Henry Park Hollis during work for the Astrographic Catalogue.  It is surrounded by an extensive shell thought to have been ejected in a nova explosion.
