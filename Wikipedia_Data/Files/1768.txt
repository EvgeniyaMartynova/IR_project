Title: Coursetia dubia
Aspects: {'C. dubia'}

Coursetia dubia is a species of legume in the family Fabaceae. It is found only in Ecuador. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forests and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.
