Title: A flor de piel (TV series)
Aspects: {'A flor de piel'}

A Flor De Piel was a short-lived Spanish-language telenovela (daily hour-long dramatic show) first broadcast in 1996 in many Spanish-speaking countries and in the US.   It starred Mariana Garza as Mariana, a student struggling to deal with a difficult life.   
The series was produced by Television Azteca (TV Azteca), a Mexican company.   
The show's theme-song was also titled "A Flor De Piel", and was sung by Mariana Garza.
