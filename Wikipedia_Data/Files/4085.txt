Title: Jordan FA Cup
Aspects: {'FA Cup (disambiguation)'}

The Jordan FA Cup is Jordan's premier knockout tournament in men's football (soccer), It is currently known as the Jordan Cup Al Manaseer, after the FA signed a sponsorship deal with Ziyad AL-Manaseer Companies Group. Al-Faisaly have the record for the most FA Cup wins in their history with a total of 18. The tournament is controlled by the Jordan Football Association.The winner gains entry to the AFC Cup.
