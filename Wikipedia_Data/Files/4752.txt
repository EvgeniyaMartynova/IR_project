Title: China National Highway 107
Aspects: {'G107'}

China's National Highway 107 runs from Beijing to Shenzhen via Wuhan. It runs to approximately 2,698 km, and, on a map, runs broadly on a straight line from Beijing to Shenzhen.
In between, the highway runs parallel to national highways 105 and 106.
