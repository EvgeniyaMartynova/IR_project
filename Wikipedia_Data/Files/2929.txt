Title: Euterpe edulis
Aspects: {'E. edulis'}

Euterpe edulis, commonly known as juçara, jucara (misspelling of the former name, of Luso-Tupian origin), jussara (an archaic alternative spelling), açaí-do-sul or palmiteiro, is a palm species in the genus Euterpe. It is now predominantly used for hearts of palm. It is closely related to the açaí palm (Euterpe oleracea), a species cultivated for its fruit and superior hearts of palm.  The larvae of Caligo brasiliensis are reported to feed on E. edulis.
