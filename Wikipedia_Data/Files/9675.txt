Title: Onoba grisea
Aspects: {'O. sulcata'}

Onoba grisea is a species of minute sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk or micromollusk in the family Rissoidae.


== Distribution ==


== Description ==
The maximum recorded shell length is 2.52 mm.


== Habitat ==
Minimum recorded depth is 0 m. Maximum recorded depth is 102 m.
