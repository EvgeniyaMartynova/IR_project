Title: Zabiele, Ostrołęka County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rzekuń, within Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Rzekuń, 10 km (6 mi) east of Ostrołęka, and 107 km (66 mi) north-east of Warsaw.
