Title: Zeidora nesta
Aspects: {'N. candida'}

Zeidora nesta is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Fissurellidae, the keyhole limpets and slit limpets.


== Description ==
The size of the shell is 4 mm.
