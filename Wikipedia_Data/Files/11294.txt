Title: Qahremanluy-e Sofla
Aspects: {'Qahremanluy'}

Qahremanluy-e Sofla (Persian: قهرمانلوی سفلی‎, also Romanized as Qahremānlūy-e Soflá; also known as Qahremānlū-ye Soflá) is a village in Bash Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 257, in 77 families.
