Title: Qaleh-ye Bala, Semnan
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Bala'}

Qaleh-ye Bala (Persian: قلعه بالا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Bālā and Qal‘eh Bālā) is a village in Beyarjomand Rural District, Beyarjomand District, Shahrud County, Semnan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 397, in 139 families.
