Title: KADD
Aspects: {'K-Hits'}

KADD (93.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an ethnic  (Mandarin) format. Licensed to Logandale, Nevada, United States, it serves the Las Vegas Valley. The station is owned by James Su, through licensee Golden City Broadcast, LLC.
