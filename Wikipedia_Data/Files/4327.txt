Title: Goniothalamus gardneri
Aspects: {'G. gardneri'}

Goniothalamus gardneri is a species of plant in the Annonaceae family. It is endemic to Sri Lanka.


== Culture ==
Known as ක‍ටු කෙර (katu kera) in Sinhala.
