Title: Zaboršt, Kostanjevica na Krki
Aspects: {'Zaboršt'}

Zaboršt (pronounced [zaˈbɔɾʃt]) is a small settlement west of the town of Kostanjevica na Krki in eastern Slovenia. The area was traditionally part of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Lower Sava Statistical Region.
