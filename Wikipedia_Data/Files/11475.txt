Title: Qaleh-ye Abbasabad, Khondab
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Abbasabad'}

Qaleh-ye Abbasabad (Persian: قلعه عباس اباد‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye ‘Abbāsābād; also known as ‘Abbasābād and ‘Abbāsābād) is a village in Sang Sefid Rural District, Qareh Chay District, Khondab County, Markazi Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 274, in 57 families.
