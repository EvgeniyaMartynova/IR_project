Title: Zabiele, Gmina Kolno
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kolno, within Kolno County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Kolno and 93 km (58 mi) west of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 744.
