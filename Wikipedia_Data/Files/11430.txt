Title: Qaleh-ye Meydan, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Meydan'}

Qaleh-ye Meydan (Persian: قلعه ميدان‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Meydān) is a village in Hati Rural District, Hati District, Lali County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 421, in 80 families.
