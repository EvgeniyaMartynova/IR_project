Title: Inside E Street
Aspects: {'E Street (disambiguation)'}

Inside E Street is an American talk show dedicated to issues concerning the elderly, geriatrics, and retirement. In its first season, in 2010, the show was hosted by Sheilah Kast. In 2011 Lark McCarthy became the host. Guest hosts have included Cokie Roberts and Dave Marash.
