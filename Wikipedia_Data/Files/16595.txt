Title: Yalabad, Qazvin
Aspects: {'Yalabad'}

Yalabad (Persian: يل اباد‎, also Romanized as Yalābād and Yelābād) is a village in Ilat-e Qaqazan-e Gharbi Rural District, Kuhin District, Qazvin County, Qazvin Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 437, in 99 families.
