Title: Zafarabad, Dehgolan
Aspects: {'Zafarabad'}

Zafarabad (Persian: ظفر آباد‎, also Romanized as Z̧afarābād; also known as Ja‘farābād) is a village in Yeylan-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Dehgolan County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 82, in 19 families.
