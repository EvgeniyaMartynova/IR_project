Title: Zabłocie, Łask County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Widawa, within Łask County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Widawa, 28 km (17 mi) south-west of Łask, and 60 km (37 mi) south-west of the regional capital Łódź.
