Title: German submarine U-111
Aspects: {'U111'}

U-111 may refer to one of the following German submarines:

SM U-111, a Type U 93 submarine launched in 1917 that served in the First World War and was surrendered in 1918
During the First World War, Germany also had this submarine with a similar name:
SM UB-111, a Type UB III submarine launched in 1917 and was surrendered in 1918
German submarine U-111 (1940), a Type IXB submarine that served in the Second World War and sank in 1941
