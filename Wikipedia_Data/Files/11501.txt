Title: Qaleh Khvajeh, Isfahan
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Khvajeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaleh Khvajeh (Persian: قلعه خواجه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Khvājeh and Qal‘eh-ye Khvājeh; also known as Ghal‘eh Khajeh) is a village in Yeylaq Rural District, in the Central District of Buin va Miandasht County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 117, in 21 families.
