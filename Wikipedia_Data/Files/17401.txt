Title: Zagacie, Łódź Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagacie'}

Zagacie [zaˈɡat͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przedbórz, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south-east of Przedbórz, 42 km (26 mi) east of Radomsko, and 94 km (58 mi) south-east of the regional capital Łódź.
