Title: Zabukovje, Kranj
Aspects: {'Zabukovje'}

Zabukovje (pronounced [zaˈbuːkɔu̯jɛ]) is a dispersed settlement below Sveti Jošt in the City Municipality of Kranj in the Upper Carniola region of Slovenia.
