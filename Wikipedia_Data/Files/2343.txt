Title: DJ Johnson (politician)
Aspects: {'D. J. Johnson'}

DJ Johnson (born 1957) is an American politician who served in the Kentucky House of Representatives as a Republican for the 13th district representing parts of Daviess County from 2017 to 2019. He lives in Owensboro, Kentucky. In 2018, he was defeated for re-election by Jim Glenn (whom he had defeated in 2016) by a margin of one vote.
