Title: La Hotte bush frog
Aspects: {'E. bakeri'}

The La Hotte bush frog or Baker's robber frog  (Eleutherodactylus bakeri) is a species of frog in the Eleutherodactylidae family endemic to the Massif de la Hotte in southwestern Haiti. Its natural habitats are closed-canopy forests (now largely gone). This arboreal frog hides by day in bromeliads where it also lays the eggs. It is threatened by habitat loss.
