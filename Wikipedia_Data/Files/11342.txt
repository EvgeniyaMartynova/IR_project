Title: Qalandarabad Rural District
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad Rural District (Persian: دهستان قلندرآباد‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Qalandarabad District, Fariman County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 10,243, in 2,338 families.  The rural district has 38 villages.
