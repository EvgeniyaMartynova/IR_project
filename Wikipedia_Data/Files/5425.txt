Title: I Adore You (film)
Aspects: {'I Adore You'}

I Adore You is a 1933 British musical comedy film set in a movie studio. It was most notable for Errol Flynn appearing as an extra.Margot Grahame was the heroine and Clifford Heatherley plays a film magnate.The choreography was done by Ralph Reader, a protege of Busby Berkeley.It is considered a lost film.


== Plot ==
Norman Young (Harold French) wants to marry Margot Grahame (Margot Grahame) but her contract with a producer prohibits her from marrying during a five-year period. Norman spends millions to take over the contact.
