Title: Caryocar glabrum
Aspects: {'C. glabrum'}

Caryocar glabrum is a species of tree in the family Caryocaraceae. It is native to South America.


== Chemical compounds ==
Dihydroisocoumarin glucosides can be found in C. glabrum.
