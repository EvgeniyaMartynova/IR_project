Title: Chauliodus danae
Aspects: {'C. danae'}

Chauliodus danae, or dana viperfish, is a species of viperfish in the family Stomiidae. The Dana Viperfish is mostly found in the Bathyal zone. They typically grow to lengths of up to 30 cm (12 in).
