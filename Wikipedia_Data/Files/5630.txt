Title: I Can't Say Goodbye (Kim Wilde song)
Aspects: {"I Can't Say Goodbye"}

"I Can't Say Goodbye" is the fourth single from Love Moves, the 1990 album by Kim Wilde. 
The single was released exclusively in the UK (the third UK single from the album). The ballad, which featured backing vocals by Jaki Graham, was edited for the 7" format and extended for the 12" and CD-single formats. The B-side was a megamix of some of Wilde's songs entitled "Sanjazz Megamix", an edit of which was used for the 7".
