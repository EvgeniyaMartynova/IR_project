Title: Sturisoma robustum
Aspects: {'S. robustum'}

Sturisoma robustum is a species of armored catfish native to the La Plata Basin of Argentina, Brazil and Paraguay.  This species grows to a length of 25 centimetres (9.8 in) SL.
