Title: Żabowo, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabowo'}

Żabowo [ʐaˈbɔvɔ] (German: Groß Sabow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogard, within Goleniów County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north-east of Nowogard, 32 km (20 mi) north-east of Goleniów, and 53 km (33 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see history of Pomerania.
