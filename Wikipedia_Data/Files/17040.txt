Title: Zabiele, Wysokie Mazowieckie County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Szepietowo, within Wysokie Mazowieckie County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Szepietowo, 9 km (6 mi) south of Wysokie Mazowieckie, and 56 km (35 mi) south-west of the regional capital Białystok.
