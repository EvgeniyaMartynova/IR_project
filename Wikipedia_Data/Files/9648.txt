Title: Oncoba ovalis
Aspects: {'O. ovalis'}

Oncoba ovalis is a species of plant in the Salicaceae family. It is found in Cameroon and Nigeria. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry forests. It is threatened by habitat loss.
