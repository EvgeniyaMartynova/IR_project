Aspects: {'KAAP'}

KQBG (99.5 FM, "99-5 The Bridge") is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Licensed to Rock Island, Washington, United States, the station serves the Wenatchee area. It is currently owned by Cherry Creek Radio and licensed to CCR-Wenatchee IV, LLC and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks.


== History ==
The station went on the air as KXAA on June 24, 1988. On 1 February 2000, it changed its call sign to KAAP.On May 6, 2016, at noon, KAAP changed its format from adult contemporary to hot adult contemporary, branded as "99.5 The Bridge". The first song on The Bridge was "SexyBack" by Justin Timberlake. The station also changed its call sign from KAAP to KQBG.


== Ownership ==
In June 2006, a deal was reached for KAAP to be acquired by Cherry Creek Radio from Fisher Radio Regional Group as part of a 24-station deal with a total reported sale price of $33.3 million.
