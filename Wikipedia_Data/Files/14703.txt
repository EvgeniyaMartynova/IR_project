Title: Mihăești, Argeș
Aspects: {'Văcarea'}

Mihăești is a commune in Argeş County, south central Romania. It is composed of seven villages: Drăghici, Furnicoși, Mihăești, Rudeni, Valea Bradului, Valea Popii and Văcarea.
The Târgului River crosses the commune from north to south.
