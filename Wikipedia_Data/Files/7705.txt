Title: Leptothyra candida
Aspects: {'L. candida'}

Leptothyra candida is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Colloniidae.


== Description ==


== Distribution ==
This marine species occurs off Hawaii and the Seychelles and Aldabra Atoll.
