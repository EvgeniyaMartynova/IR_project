Title: Lines in Wax
Aspects: {"I Can't Stop"}

Lines in Wax is the debut EP by the English dubstep artist, Flux Pavilion. It was released on 11 October 2010 through Circus Records. The third track, "I Can't Stop", was sampled by Kanye West and Jay-Z on "Who Gon Stop Me" from Watch the Throne. The original song entered the UK Singles Chart at number 115 on 17 March 2012.
