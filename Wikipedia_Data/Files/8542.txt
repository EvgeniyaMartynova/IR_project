Title: Myopopone castanea
Aspects: {'M. maculata'}

Myopopone castanea is a species of ant in the genus Myopopone. It was described by Smith in 1860.


== Distribution ==
Myopopone castanea is distributed in several nations and in Australia, where they prefer to nest in rotten wood or bark.
