Title: Neza-e Kuchek
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Kohneh'}

Neza-e Kuchek (Persian: نزاع كوچك‎, also Romanized as Nezā‘-e Kūchek; also known as Nezā‘-e Soflá and Qal‘eh Kohneh) is a village in Mishan Rural District, Mahvarmilani District, Mamasani County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 24, in 6 families.
