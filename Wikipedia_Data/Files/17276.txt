Title: Zacisze, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zacisze'}

Zacisze [zaˈt͡ɕiʂɛ] (German: Hartha) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Leśna, within Lubań County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) east of Leśna, 14 km (9 mi) south-east of Lubań, and 118 km (73 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
