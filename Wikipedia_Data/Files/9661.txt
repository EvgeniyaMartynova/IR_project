Title: Ogasawarana rex
Aspects: {'O. rex'}

Ogasawarana rex is a species of land snail with an operculum, a terrestrial gastropod mollusk in the family Helicinidae, the helicinids.


== Distribution ==
This species is endemic to Japan.
