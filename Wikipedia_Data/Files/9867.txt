Title: Garberville Airport
Aspects: {'O16'}

Garberville Airport (FAA LID: O16) is a public airport located 2 miles (3.2 km) southwest of the central business district (CBD) of Garberville, a town in Humboldt County, California, USA. The airport covers 44 acres (180,000 m2) and has one runway.
