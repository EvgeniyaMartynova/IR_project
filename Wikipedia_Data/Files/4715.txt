Title: G59 Hohhot–Beihai Expressway
Aspects: {'G59'}

The Hohhot–Beihai Expressway (Chinese: 呼和浩特–北海高速公路), commonly referred to as the Hubei Expressway (Chinese: 呼北高速公路; not to be confused with the province of Hubei), is an incomplete expressway in the People's Republic of China. It is a major north-south expressway that when complete, will connect the cities of Hohhot, the capital of Inner Mongolia, with Beihai, on the southern coast, in the autonomous region of Guangxi. The expressway was announced as one of the eleven primary north-south expressways in China's expressway network on 24 May 2013.
