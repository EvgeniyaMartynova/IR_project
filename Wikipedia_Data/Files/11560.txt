Title: R7 expressway (Slovakia)
Aspects: {'R-7 expressway'}

R7 is an expressway (Slovak: rýchlostná cesta) in southern Slovakia.
It starts in Bratislava and ends near Lučenec. It goes through or around Dunajská Streda, Nové Zámky and Veľký Krtíš.
