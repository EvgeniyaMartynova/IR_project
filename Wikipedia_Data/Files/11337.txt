Title: Qalandarabad-e Bala
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad-e Bala (Persian: قلندرابادبالا‎, also Romanized as Qalandarābād-e Bālā; also known as Qalandarābād) is a village in Soltanali Rural District, in the Central District of Gonbad-e Qabus County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 992, in 197 families.
