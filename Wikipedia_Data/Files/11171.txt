Title: Qabaq Tappeh, Hamadan
Aspects: {'Qabaq Tappeh'}

Qabaq Tappeh (Persian: قباق تپه‎, also Romanized as Qabāq Tappeh and Qabaq Tepe; also known as Ghabagh Tappeh Hajebloo, Qābākh Tappeh, and Qapāq Tepe) is a village in Raheb Rural District, in the Central District of Kabudarahang County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,653, in 889 families.
