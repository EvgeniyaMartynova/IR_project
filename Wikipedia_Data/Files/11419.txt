Title: Qaleh Kesh, Babol
Aspects: {'Qaleh Kesh'}

Qaleh Kesh (Persian: قلعه كش‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kesh) is a village in Firuzjah Rural District, Bandpey-ye Sharqi District, Babol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
