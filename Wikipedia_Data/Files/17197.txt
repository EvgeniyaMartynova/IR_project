Title: Ząbrowo, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Ząbrowo'}

Ząbrowo [zɔmˈbrɔvɔ] (German: Sommerau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Iława, within Iława County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Iława and 72 km (45 mi) west of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 1,032.
