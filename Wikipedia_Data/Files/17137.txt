Title: Zaborowo, Konin County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wierzbinek, within Konin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) west of Wierzbinek, 24 km (15 mi) north of Konin, and 101 km (63 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
