Title: I Love America (Patrick Juvet song)
Aspects: {'I Love America'}

"I Love America" is 1978 disco hit by Swiss-born performer Patrick Juvet. Along with the tracks, "Where Is My Woman" and "Got A Feeling", it peaked at number five on the disco chart in the United States. It was included in the compilation album, A Night at Studio 54, in 1979.
In 1996, "I Love America" was sampled for Full Intention's dance hit "America (I Love America)".
