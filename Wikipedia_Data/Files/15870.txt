Title: Xanthe Huynh
Aspects: {'Xanthe'}

Xanthe Huynh () is an American voice actress known for her voicework in English dubs of anime and video games. Her most well known roles include Siesta in The Familiar of Zero, Suzuka Tsukimura in the Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha series, Ui Hirasawa in K-On!, Sanae Nagatsuki in Squid Girl, Sachi in Sword Art Online, Yuki in Yuki Yuna is a Hero, and Hanayo Koizumi in Love Live. In 2017, she voiced Haru Okumura in the Persona 5 video game.
