Title: Pseudothyretes carnea
Aspects: {'P. carnea'}

Pseudothyretes carnea is a moth of the subfamily Arctiinae. It was described by George Hampson in 1898. It is found in Angola, the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Gabon.
