Title: Qaleh Cheh, North Khorasan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Cheh'}

Qaleh Cheh (Persian: قلعه چه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Cheh) is a village in Sivkanlu Rural District, in the Central District of Shirvan County, North Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 576, in 140 families.
