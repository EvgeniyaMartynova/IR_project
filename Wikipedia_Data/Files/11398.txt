Title: Qaleh Gelineh-ye Monfared
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gelineh (disambiguation)'}

Qaleh Gelineh-ye Monfared (Persian: قلعه گلينه منفرد‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gelīneh-ye Monfared; also known as Qal‘eh Gelīneh-ye Vosţá) is a village in Sanjabi Rural District, Kuzaran District, Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
