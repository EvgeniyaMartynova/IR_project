Title: Wacławów, Radom County
Aspects: {'Wacławów'}

Wacławów [vat͡sˈwavuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wolanów, within Radom County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north of Wolanów, 12 km (7 mi) west of Radom, and 89 km (55 mi) south of Warsaw.
