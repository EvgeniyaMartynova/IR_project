Title: Zabrodzie, Wyszków County
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] is a village in Wyszków County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It is the seat of the gmina (administrative district) called Gmina Zabrodzie. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) south of Wyszków and 43 km (27 mi) north-east of Warsaw.
