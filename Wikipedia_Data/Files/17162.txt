Title: Zaborze, Świdwin County
Aspects: {'Zaborze'}

Zaborze [zaˈbɔʐɛ] (German: Waldhof) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Połczyn-Zdrój, within Świdwin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-east of Połczyn-Zdrój, 25 km (16 mi) east of Świdwin, and 112 km (70 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 60.
