Title: Zagaje, Kwidzyn County
Aspects: {'Zagaje'}

Zagaje [zaˈɡajɛ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Prabuty, within Kwidzyn County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south of Prabuty, 16 km (10 mi) east of Kwidzyn, and 82 km (51 mi) south-east of the regional capital Gdańsk.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
