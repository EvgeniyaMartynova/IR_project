Title: John Rowson Smith
Aspects: {'J. R. Smith (disambiguation)'}

John Rowson Smith (1810 - 1864) was a panorama painter in the United States. His father was John Rubens Smith. Several publications about him and his work were published. He produced a successful three reel rendition of the Mississippi River. It was also published in book form.He was born in Boston and grew up in Brooklyn before moving to Philadelphia.Smith was a pioneer in the creation of moving panoramas. He was a rival of John Banvard.Artist Russell Smith considered him a great scamp and reportedly gave his own son a distinctive name so there would be no confusion between the two.
