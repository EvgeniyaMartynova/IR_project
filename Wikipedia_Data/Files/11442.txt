Title: Qaleh-ye Qanbar
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qonbar'}

Qaleh-ye Qanbar (Persian: قلعه قنبر‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Qanbar and Qal‘eh Qanbar) is a village in Beyranvand-e Jonubi Rural District, Bayravand District, Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 26, in 6 families.
