Title: Zabłocie, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zagnańsk, within Kielce County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Zagnańsk and 9 km (6 mi) north-east of the regional capital Kielce.The village has a population of 380.
