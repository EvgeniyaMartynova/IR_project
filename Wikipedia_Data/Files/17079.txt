Title: Zabłocie, Bolesławiec County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] (German: Thiergarten) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogrodziec, within Bolesławiec County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-east of Nowogrodziec, 11 km (7 mi) south-west of Bolesławiec, and 112 km (70 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 270.
