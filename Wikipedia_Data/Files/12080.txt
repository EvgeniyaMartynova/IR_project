Title: D35 motorway (Czech Republic)
Aspects: {'R35'}

D35 motorway (Czech: Dálnice D35), formerly Expressway R35 (Czech: Rychlostní silnice R35) is a highway in the Czech Republic. If completed, it will be second longest highway in Czech Republic, running from Úlibice to the D1 at Lipník nad Bečvou. The first segment was opened in the 1970s.
As of 2018 63 km of full expressway are in operation in two segments.
