Title: J. Leo Fairbanks House (Corvallis, Oregon)
Aspects: {'J. Leo Fairbanks House'}

The J. Leo Fairbanks House is a historic residence in Corvallis, Oregon, United States.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985.
