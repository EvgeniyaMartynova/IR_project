Title: Qaleh-ye Heydar, Kerman
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Heydar'}

Qaleh-ye Heydar (Persian: قلعه حيدر‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Ḩeydar, Qal‘eh Ḩeydar, and Qal‘eh-i-Haidar; also known as Dal‘eh Heydar and Ghal‘eh Heidar) is a village in Hoseynabad-e Goruh Rural District, Rayen District, Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
