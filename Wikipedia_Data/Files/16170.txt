Title: Ya nunca más (film)
Aspects: {'Ya nunca más'}

Ya nunca más is a 1984 Mexican musical film directed by Abel Salazar. It starred the young Luis Miguel in his first film role. The soundtrack album Ya nunca más became Miguel's first gold record.


== Cast ==
Luis Miguel – Luis Aranda
Gonzalo Vega – Enrique Aranda
Rosa Salazar Arenas – Lorena
Ariadna Welter – Sra. Cecilia
Sergio Kleiner – Doctor
