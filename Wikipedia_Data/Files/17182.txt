Title: Zabrody, Gmina Karsin
Aspects: {'Zabrody'}

Zabrody [zaˈbrɔdɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Karsin, within Kościerzyna County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Karsin, 17 km (11 mi) south of Kościerzyna, and 62 km (39 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
