Title: Qaleh-ye Nasir, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Nasir'}

Qaleh-ye Nasir (Persian: قلعه نصير‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Naşīr; also known as Helāl and Qal‘eh-ye Nāşer) is a village in Ben Moala Rural District, in the Central District of Shush County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 271, in 41 families.
