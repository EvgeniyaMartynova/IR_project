Title: Qaemiyeh, Mazandaran
Aspects: {'Qaemiyeh (disambiguation)'}

Qaemiyeh (Persian: قائميه‎, also Romanized as Qā’emīyeh and Qa‘emīyeh) is a village in Feyziyeh Rural District, in the Central District of Babol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 507, in 135 families.
