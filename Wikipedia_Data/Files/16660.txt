Title: Yalveh-ye Sofla
Aspects: {'Yalveh'}

Yalveh-ye Sofla (Persian: يلوه سفلي‎, also Romanized as Yalveh-ye Soflá; also known as Yalvā Pā'īn) is a village in Baladarband Rural District, in the Central District of Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 67, in 14 families.
