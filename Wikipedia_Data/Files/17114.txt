Title: Żabno, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sławno, within Sławno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) east of Sławno and 184 km (114 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
