Title: Oak Creek Township, Butler County, Nebraska
Aspects: {'Oak Creek Township, Nebraska'}

Oak Creek Township is one of seventeen townships in Butler County, Nebraska, United States. The population was 552 at the 2000 census. A 2006 estimate placed the township's population at 533.The Village of Brainard lies within the Township.
