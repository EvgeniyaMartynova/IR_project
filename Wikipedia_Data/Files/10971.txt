Q'ara Qullu (Aymara q'ara bare , bald, qullu mountain, "bare mountain", also spelled Khara Khollu, Khara Kkollu) is a 4,930 m (16,170 ft) mountain in the Bolivian Andes. It is located in the La Paz Department, Loayza Province, Luribay Municipality. Q'ara Qullu lies southwest of Suka Sukani, northwest of Urqu Jipi√±a and southeast of K'ark'ani. The little lake northwest of Q'ara Qullu is Wila Quta ("red lake").
