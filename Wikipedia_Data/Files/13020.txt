Title: Reishia armigera
Aspects: {'T. armigera'}

Reishia armigera, common name : the belligerent rock shell, is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Muricidae, the murex snails or rock snails.


== Description ==
The size of an adult shell varies between 50 mm and 105.5 mm.


== Distribution ==
This species is distributed in the Indian Ocean along Chagos, the Aldabra Atoll and Tanzania; in the Pacific Ocean along Japan.
