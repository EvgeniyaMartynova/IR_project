Title: Qabaleh-ye Firuzi
Aspects: {'Qabaleh'}

Qabaleh-ye Firuzi (Persian: قباله فيروزي‎, also Romanized as Qabāleh-ye Fīrūzī and Qabālehfīrūzī) is a village in Banesh Rural District, Beyza District, Sepidan County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 153, in 38 families.
