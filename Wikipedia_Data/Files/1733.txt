Title: Calosima darwini
Aspects: {'C. darwini'}

Calosima darwini is a moth in the family Blastobasidae. It is found on the Galapagos Islands.The length of the forewings is 4-6.9 mm. The forewings are mostly white intermixed with tricolored scales. Both surfaces of the hindwings are uniform pale brownish grey.


== Etymology ==
The species is named after Charles Darwin.
