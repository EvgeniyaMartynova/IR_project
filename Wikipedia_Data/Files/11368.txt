Title: Qaleh Bid, North Khorasan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bid'}

Qaleh Bid (Persian: قلعه بيد‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Bīd) is a village in Atrak Rural District, Maneh District, Maneh and Samalqan County, North Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 144, in 32 families.
