Title: Qajar Ab-e Sofla
Aspects: {'Qajar Ab'}

Qajar Ab-e Sofla (Persian: قجراب سفلي‎, also Romanized as Qajar Āb-e Soflá; also known as Qajarāb and Qajar Āb) is a village in Kuh Sardeh Rural District, in the Central District of Malayer County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 50, in 13 families.
