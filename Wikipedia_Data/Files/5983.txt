Title: Redemption (Huey album)
Aspects: {'I Go Crazy'}

Redemption is the second studio album by American rapper Huey, released on September 14, 2010.


== Background ==
Huey originally recorded songs for an album entitled Strictly Business, but it ended up being released as a DVD. Redemption was supposed to be released in 2009. The album release got delayed and the album was released on September 14, 2010.Art Direction by: Damgwoodwork.com
