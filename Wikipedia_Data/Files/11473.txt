Title: Qaleh Tork-e Sofla
Aspects: {'Qaleh Tork'}

Qaleh Tork-e Sofla (Persian: قلعه ترك سفلي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Tork-e Soflá) is a village in Harasam Rural District, Homeyl District, Eslamabad-e Gharb County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 133, in 29 families.
