Title: Qaleh Asgar Rural District
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Askar'}

Qaleh Asgar Rural District (Persian: دهستان قلعه عسگر‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Lalehzar District, Bardsir County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,930, in 938 families. The rural district has 37 villages.
