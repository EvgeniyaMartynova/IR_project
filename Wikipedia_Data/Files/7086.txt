Title: K. V. Kuppam (state assembly constituency)
Aspects: {'K. V. Kuppam'}

K. V. Kuppam, or Kilvaithinankuppam, is a state assembly constituency in Tamil Nadu, India, formed after constituency delimitation in 2007. The seat is reserved for candidates from the Scheduled Castes. It is included in the Vellore parliamentary constituency for national elections.
