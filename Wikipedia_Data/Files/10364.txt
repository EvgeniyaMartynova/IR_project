Title: Phrynobatrachus cornutus
Aspects: {'P. cornutus'}

Phrynobatrachus cornutus is a species of frog in the Phrynobatrachidae family.
It is found in Cameroon, Central African Republic, Republic of the Congo, Equatorial Guinea, and Gabon.
Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, swamps, freshwater marshes, intermittent freshwater marshes, and heavily degraded former forest.
It is threatened by habitat loss.
