Title: Jorge D'Elía
Aspects: {"D'Elía"}

Jorge D'Elía (born April 28, 1938 in La Plata, Argentina) is a film and television actor.He's been very famous acting in the "Los Simuladores" Argentinian television series. His son is the actor Federico D'Elia.
Two of his most recent films have been critically well received: El Abrazo partido (2004) and El Aura (2005).
