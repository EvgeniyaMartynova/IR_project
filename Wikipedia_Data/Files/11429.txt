Title: Masur, Iran
Aspects: {'Qaleh Mansur'}

Masur (Persian: ماسور‎, also Romanized as Māsūr; also known as Qal‘eh Mansūr) is a village in Koregah-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 15,037, in 3,093 families.
