Title: Qaemabad, Kerman
Aspects: {'Qaemabad'}

Qaemabad (Persian: قائم اباد‎, also Romanized as Qā’emābād; also known as Deh Lārī, Gha’em Abad, Qā’īmābād, and Shahrak-e Qā’emābād) is a village in Ekhtiarabad Rural District, in the Central District of Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,207, in 515 families.
