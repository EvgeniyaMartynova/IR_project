Title: Honda X11
Aspects: {'X11 (disambiguation)'}

The Honda X11 (also called CB1100SF)  is a  standard motorcycle produced by Honda with a 1,137 cc (69.4 cu in) liquid-cooled, inline-four engine and a 1990s-style cooling unit at its fore end. It is based on the CBR1100XX Super Blackbird.
