Title: Cat Lake Airport
Aspects: {'YAC'}

Cat Lake Airport, (IATA: YAC, ICAO: CYAC), is located adjacent to the Cat Lake First Nation, Kenora District, Ontario, Canada, on the central north shore of Cat Lake.
