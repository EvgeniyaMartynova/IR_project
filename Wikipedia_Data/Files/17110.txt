Title: Żabno, Bytów County
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] (Kashubian: Żabno) is a village in Gmina Miastko, Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) south-west of Bytów and 63 km (39 mi) south-west of Gdańsk (capital city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship). 
From 1975 to 1998 the village was in Słupsk Voivodeship. 
It has a population of 87.
