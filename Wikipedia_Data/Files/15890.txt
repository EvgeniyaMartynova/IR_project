Title: Chapecó Airport
Aspects: {'XAP'}

Serafin Enoss Bertaso Airport (IATA: XAP, ICAO: SBCH) is the airport serving Chapecó, Brazil.


== History ==
The terminal was inaugurated on 18 March 1978.


== Airlines and destinations ==


== Accidents and incidents ==
22 January 1976: a Transbrasil Embraer EMB 110C Bandeirante registration PT-TBD operating flight 107 from Chapecó to Erechim, crashed upon take-off from Chapecó. Seven of the nine passengers and crew on board died.


== Access ==
The airport is located 10 km (6 mi) from downtown Chapecó.


== Future developments ==
On 1 October 2010 the State Government of Santa Catarina authorized renovation works at Chapecó Airport focusing mainly on the runway.
