Title: Lonchocarpus salvadorensis
Aspects: {'L. salvadorensis'}

Lonchocarpus salvadorensis, the Sangre de Chucho, is a plant species in the genus Lonchocarpus.
The rotenoids deguelin, rotenone, elliptone and Î±-toxicarol can be found in the seeds of L. salvadorensis.
