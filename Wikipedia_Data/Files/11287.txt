Title: Qahab-e Shomali Rural District
Aspects: {'Qahab (disambiguation)'}

Qahab-e Shomali Rural District (Persian: دهستان قهاب شمالي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 33,784, in 9,075 families.  The rural district has 21 villages.
