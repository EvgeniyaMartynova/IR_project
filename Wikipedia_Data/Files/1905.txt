Title: Cephalopholis formosa
Aspects: {'C. formosa'}

Cephalopholis formosa or Blue line grouper is a grouper from the Indo-West Pacific that occasionally makes its way into the aquarium trade. It grows to a size of 34 cm in length.


== Common Name ==
Bluelined hind


== Habitat ==
Salt water


== Dispersion ==
The gulf of Thailand and Andaman Sea
and Persian Gulf


== Utilization ==
Fishery : Sustaining
