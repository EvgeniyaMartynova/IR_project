Title: Zadębie, Parczew County
Aspects: {'Zadębie'}

Zadębie [zaˈdɛmbjɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dębowa Kłoda, within Parczew County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) east of Dębowa Kłoda, 20 km (12 mi) east of Parczew, and 59 km (37 mi) north-east of the regional capital Lublin.
