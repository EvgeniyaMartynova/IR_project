Title: J'Adore (fragrance)
Aspects: {"J'Adore"}

J'adore (pronounced [ʒ‿adɔʁ]; French for "I love", and a pun on the brand Dior) is a perfume for women that was created in 1999 by Calice Becker for Parfums Christian Dior. Some perfume critics have detected a change in the recipe since the 1999 launch.
