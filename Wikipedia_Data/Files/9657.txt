Title: Ophryosporus
Aspects: {'O. pubescens'}

Ophryosporus is a genus of South American flowering plants in the boneset tribe within the sunflower family.
Species
formerly includedSeveral once considered part of Ophryosporus now regarded as better suited to other genera: Decachaeta Koanophyllon Mikania Stomatanthes
