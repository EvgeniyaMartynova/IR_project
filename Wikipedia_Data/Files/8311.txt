Title: Miltasura
Aspects: {'M. celebensis'}

Miltasura is a monotypic moth genus in the subfamily Arctiinae. Its only species, Miltasura celebensis, can be found on Sulawesi in Indonesia. Both the genus and species were first described by Walter Karl Johann Roepke in 1946.
