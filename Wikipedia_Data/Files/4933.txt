Title: Hortonia floribunda
Aspects: {'H. floribunda'}

Hortonia floribunda is a species of plant in the Monimiaceae family. It is endemic to Sri Lanka.


== Culture ==
Known as "වෑවිය - wewiya" in Sinhala.
