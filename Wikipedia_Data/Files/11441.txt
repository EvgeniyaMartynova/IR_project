Title: Qaleh Qazi Rural District
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh Qazi Rural District (Persian: دهستان قلعه قاضي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Qaleh Qazi District of Bandar Abbas County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 12,011, in 2,566 families.  The rural district has 21 villages.
