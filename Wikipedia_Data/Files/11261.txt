Title: Qaflankuh-e Sharqi Rural District
Aspects: {'Qaflankuh'}

Qaflankuh-e Sharqi Rural District (Persian: دهستان قافلانكوه شرقي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,274, in 911 families. The rural district has 18 villages.
