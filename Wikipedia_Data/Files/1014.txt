Title: Burchia spectabilis
Aspects: {'B. spectabilis'}

Burchia spectabilis is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Pseudomelatomidae.


== Description ==
The length of the shell attains 36.9 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species occurs off Western Australia.
