Title: G18 Rongcheng–Wuhai Expressway
Aspects: {'G18'}

The Rongcheng–Wuhai Expressway (Chinese: 荣成—乌海高速公路), commonly referred to as the Rongwu Expressway (Chinese: 荣乌高速公路) is an expressway that connects the cities of Rongcheng, Shandong, China, and Wuhai, Inner Mongolia. When fully complete, it will be 1,820 km (1,130 mi) in length.
The expressway is complete from Weihai, Shandong to Bazhou, Hebei, and from Jungar Banner to Qipanjing Town in Otog Banner, entirely in Ordos City, Inner Mongolia.
