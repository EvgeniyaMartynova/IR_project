Title: Żabiniec, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabiniec'}

Żabiniec [ʐaˈbiɲet͡s] (German: Wiesenhof) is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Białogard, within Białogard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Białogard and 110 km (68 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
