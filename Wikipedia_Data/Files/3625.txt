Title: F2F (TV series)
Aspects: {'F2F'}

F2F was a youth chat show series on the channel Granada Talk TV; it featured phone ins, studio guests and comedy sketches within interstitials. The series ran between October 1996 and August 1997 (when the channel was closed). It was presented by Sacha Baron Cohen and Natasha Kaplinsky, in their first major roles on television.
