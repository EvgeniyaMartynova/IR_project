Title: Zabideh, South Khorasan
Aspects: {'Zabideh'}

Zabideh (Persian: زبيده‎, also Romanized as Zābīdeh, Zobeideh, and Zobeydeh; also known as Zabīden) is a village in Momenabad Rural District, in the Central District of Sarbisheh County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 68, in 16 families.
