Title: Zagaje, Strzelce-Drezdenko County
Aspects: {'Zagaje'}

Zagaje [zaˈɡajɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zwierzyn, within Strzelce-Drezdenko County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland.Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
