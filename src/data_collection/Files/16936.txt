Title: WBIZ-FM
Aspects: {'Z100'}

WBIZ-FM (100.7 FM, "Z100") is a Top 40 (CHR) radio station in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. The station signed on in 1967. It is known as Z-100.
