Title: Rodriguezia lanceolata
Aspects: {'B. rosea'}

Rodriguezia lanceolata is a species of orchid found from the St. Vincent, Trinidad, Panama, Colombia, Venezuela, the Guianas,Suriname, Peru, Ecuador, and Brazil.
