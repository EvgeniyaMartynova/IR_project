Title: Záblatí (Jindřichův Hradec District)
Aspects: {'Záblatí'}

Záblatí is a village and municipality (obec) in Jindřichův Hradec District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 10.26 square kilometres (3.96 sq mi), and has a population of 85.
Záblatí lies approximately 25 kilometres (16 mi) west of Jindřichův Hradec, 21 km (13 mi) north-east of České Budějovice, and 111 km (69 mi) south of Prague.
