Title: Bucculatrix montana
Aspects: {'B. montana'}

Bucculatrix montana is a moth in the Bucculatricidae family. It is found in North America, where it has been recorded from Ontario, Nova Scotia, Indiana, Maryland, Virginia, Ohio, Michigan, Maine, New York, New Jersey, Massachusetts and Georgia.
