Title: Zagajnik, Lower Silesian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zagajnik'}

Zagajnik [zaˈɡai̯nik] (German: Haidewaldau in der Oberlausitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogrodziec, within Bolesławiec County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Nowogrodziec, 20 km (12 mi) west of Bolesławiec, and 123 km (76 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 193.
