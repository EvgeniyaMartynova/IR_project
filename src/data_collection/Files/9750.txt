Title: Howard O'Neal Barn
Aspects: {"O'Neal House"}

The Howard O'Neal Barn was a historic barn near Russell, Arkansas.  It was located southeast of the city off Roetzel Road.  It was a two-story wood frame structure, with a gambrel roof.  In layout it has a transverse crib plan, and was designed to house equipment, farm animals, and feed.  Built about 1938, it was a good example of a period barn in White County.The barn was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1992.  It has been listed as destroyed in the Arkansas Historic Preservation Program database.
