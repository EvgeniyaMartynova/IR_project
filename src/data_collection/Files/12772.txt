Title: Orraya
Aspects: {'S. occultus'}

For the African genus called leaf-tailed geckos, see Uroplatus

Orraya occultus, also known as McIlwraith leaf-tailed gecko or the long-necked northern leaf-tailed gecko, is a species in a monotypic genus of Australian gecko. It is restricted to higher elevations in the McIlwraith Range in northeastern Queensland.
