Title: Zaborowo, Inowrocław County
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] (German: Hinterwalden) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kruszwica, within Inowrocław County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) east of Kruszwica, 21 km (13 mi) south-east of Inowrocław, and 44 km (27 mi) south of Toruń.
