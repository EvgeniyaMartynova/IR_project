Title: Council Airport
Aspects: {'K29'}

Council Airport (IATA: CIL, FAA LID: K29) is a state-owned public-use airport located one nautical mile (1.8 km) north of the central business district of Council, in the Nome Census Area in the U.S. state of Alaska.


== Facilities ==
Council Airport covers an area of 114 acres (46 ha) at an elevation of 85 feet (26 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 10/28 with a gravel and dirt surface measuring 3,000 by 60 feet (914 x 18 m).
