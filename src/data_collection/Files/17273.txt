Title: Zachełmie, West Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zachełmie'}

Zachełmie [zaˈxɛwmʲɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łobez, within Łobez County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) west of Łobez and 68 km (42 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 70.
