Title: S. Rajendran (CPI(M) politician)
Aspects: {'S. Rajendran'}

S. Rajendran is an Indian politician and former Member of the Legislative Assembly of Kerala. He was elected to the Kerala Legislative Assembly as a Communist Party of India (Marxist) candidate from Devikulam constituency in 2001 election.
