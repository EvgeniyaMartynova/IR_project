Title: SJ X14
Aspects: {'X14'}

X14 is a series of two-car electric multiple units operated by Statens Järnvägar (SJ) of Sweden as local trains. They were built in 18 copies by Asea Brown Boveri between 1994-95, and based on the older X12-series. In addition two X12 units was converted to X14. It is operated by SJ, SSRT, Västtrafik, Tåg i Bergslagen (TiB) and Östgötatrafiken.
