Title: Qaleh Bin, Talesh
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bin'}

Qaleh Bin (Persian: قلعه بين‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Bīn) is a village in Kuhestani-ye Talesh Rural District, in the Central District of Talesh County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 119, in 23 families.
