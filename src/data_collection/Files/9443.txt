Title: NA-37 (Tank)
Aspects: {'NA-25'}

NA-37 (Tank) is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. The constituency was created in 2018 by bifurcating NA-25 (Dera Ismail Khan-cum-Tank) where Tank district shared its representation with a portion of Dera Ismail Khan District. The current constituency completely comprises Tank District while the Dera Ismail Khan areas of the old constituency are now included in NA-39 (Dera Ismail Khan-II) .
