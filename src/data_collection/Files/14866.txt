Title: W2 Poppodium
Aspects: {'W2'}

W2 Poppodium is a pop concert hosting venue in the Dutch city 's-Hertogenbosch. The venue got its name in 2000. Before 2000 it was named Willem II Concertzaal.
The W2 Poppodium is situated in a former cigar factory Willem II.
