Title: Zaborze, Nowy Dwór Mazowiecki County
Aspects: {'Zaborze'}

Zaborze [zaˈbɔʐɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nasielsk, within Nowy Dwór Mazowiecki County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Nasielsk, 12 km (7 mi) north of Nowy Dwór Mazowiecki, and 41 km (25 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
