Title: Zabrodzie, Łódź Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gidle, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Gidle, 17 km (11 mi) south of Radomsko, and 96 km (60 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
