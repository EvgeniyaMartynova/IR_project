Title: Rusty-capped fulvetta
Aspects: {'S. dubia'}

The rusty-capped fulvetta (Alcippe dubia) is a species of bird in the Pellorneidae family.

It is found in Bhutan, China, India, Laos, Myanmar, and Vietnam. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.
