Title: Trigonodes cephise
Aspects: {'T. maxima'}

Trigonodes cephise  is a moth of the family Noctuidae. It is found from the Indo-Australian tropics to northern Australia, the Caroline Islands, Samoa and New Caledonia, Nias and other islands on the south-west of Sumatra.
The larvae feed on Vigna species, including Vigna marina.


== Subspecies ==
