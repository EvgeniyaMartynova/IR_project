Title: Żabno, Starogard County
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] (German: Saaben) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Starogard Gdański, within Starogard County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Starogard Gdański and 44 km (27 mi) south of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 486.
