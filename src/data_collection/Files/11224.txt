Title: Qaderabad, Asadabad
Aspects: {'Qadirabad, Iran (disambiguation)'}

Qaderabad (Persian: قادراباد‎, also Romanized as Qāderābād; also known as Qādīrābād) is a village in Chaharduli Rural District, in the Central District of Asadabad County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 382, in 93 families.
