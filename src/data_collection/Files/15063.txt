Title: Cheryl Moana Waaka
Aspects: {'Waaka (disambiguation)'}

Cheryl Waaka (born 12 May 1970) is a former female rugby union player. She represented New Zealand and Auckland. She was in the squad that won the 1998 and 2002 Women's Rugby World Cup.In 2005, Waaka became the first woman in Northland to coach a senior men's club rugby team.
