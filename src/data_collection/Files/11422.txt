Title: Qaleh Kharabeh, Fars
Aspects: {'Qaleh Kharabeh'}

Qaleh Kharabeh (Persian: قلعه خرابه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kharābeh; also known as Deh Kharābeh) is a village in Hamaijan Rural District, Hamaijan District, Sepidan County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 245, in 54 families.
