Title: Zaduszniki, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zaduszniki'}

Zaduszniki [zaduʂˈniki] is a village in north-central Poland, located in the administrative district of Gmina Wielgie, within Lipno County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Wielgie, 16 km (10 mi) south of Lipno, and 53 km (33 mi) south-east of Toruń.
