Title: Zagajów, Kazimierza County
Aspects: {'Zagajów'}

Zagajów [zaˈɡajuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czarnocin, within Kazimierza County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of Czarnocin, 7 km (4 mi) east of Kazimierza Wielka, and 66 km (41 mi) south of the regional capital Kielce.
