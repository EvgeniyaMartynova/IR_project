Title: Spilarctia costata
Aspects: {'S. costata'}

Spilarctia costata is a moth in the family Erebidae. It was described by Jean Baptiste Boisduval in 1832. It is found in Papua, Papua New Guinea and on the Bismarck Archipelago.
