Title: Qalatuiyeh, Jiroft
Aspects: {'Qalatuiyeh'}

Qalatuiyeh (Persian: قلاتوييه‎, also Romanized as Qalātū’īyeh; also known as Kalato, Kalātū, and Qalātū) is a village in Gevar Rural District, Sarduiyeh District, Jiroft County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 193, in 44 families.
