Title: Zabiele, Radzyń Podlaski County
Aspects: {'Zabiele'}

Zabiele [zaˈbjɛlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radzyń Podlaski, within Radzyń Podlaski County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Radzyń Podlaski and 58 km (36 mi) north of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 569.
