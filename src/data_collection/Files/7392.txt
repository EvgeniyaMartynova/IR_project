Title: Kaadu (1973 Malayalam film)
Aspects: {'Kaadu'}

Kaadu is a 1973 Indian Malayalam film, directed by P. Subramaniam. The film stars Madhu, Thikkurissi Sukumaran Nair, Shanthi and C. L. Anandan in the lead roles. The film had musical score by Vedpal Verma. This movie was simultaneously made in Tamil as Malai Naattu Mangai, with Gemini Ganesan, SasiKumar, Cho and C. L. Anandan in the same roles as the Malayalam version.
