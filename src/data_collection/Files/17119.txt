Title: Záboří (České Budějovice District)
Aspects: {'Záboří'}

Záboří is a village and municipality (obec) in České Budějovice District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 16.57 square kilometres (6.40 sq mi), and has a population of 314 (as at 31 December 2007).
Záboří lies approximately 16 kilometres (10 mi) west of České Budějovice and 123 km (76 mi) south of Prague.
