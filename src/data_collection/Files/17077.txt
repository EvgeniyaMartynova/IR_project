Title: Záblatí (Žďár nad Sázavou District)
Aspects: {'Záblatí'}

Záblatí is a village and municipality (obec) in Žďár nad Sázavou District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.


== Location ==
Záblatí lies approximately 32 kilometres (20 mi) south-east of Žďár nad Sázavou, 43 km (27 mi) east of Jihlava, and 152 km (94 mi) south-east of Prague.


== Area ==
The municipality covers an area of 4.14 square kilometres (1.60 sq mi).


== Population ==
Záblatí has a population of 175 (as at 3 July 2006).
