Fabio D'Elia (born 19 January 1983) is a half-Italian Liechtenstein football midfielder who currently plays for FC Ruggell, a Liechtenstein club. He also played for the Liechtenstein national football team and won 50 caps and scored two goals since his debut against Austria in a World Cup qualifier in April 2001.
