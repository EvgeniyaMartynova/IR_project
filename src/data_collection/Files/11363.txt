Title: Qaleh-ye Bahman, Fars
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bahman'}

Qaleh-ye Bahman (Persian: قلعه بهمن‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Bahman and Qal‘eh Bahman) is a village in Rostaq Rural District, in the Central District of Neyriz County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 349, in 89 families.
