Title: I Apologize (Anita Baker song)
Aspects: {'I Apologize'}

"I Apologize" is a song by American recording artist Anita Baker. The song was released in 1994 as the second single from her Rhythm of Love album. 
"I Apologize" peaked to number eight on Billboard's Hot Black Singles chart. The record won her a Grammy Award for Best Female R&B Vocal Performance.
