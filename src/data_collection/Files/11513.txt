Title: Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali, Kermanshah
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali'}

Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali (Persian: قلعه محمدعلي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Moḩammad ‘Alī; also known as Qal‘a Imam Ali, Qal‘eh Imām ‘Ali, and Qal‘eh-ye Moḩammad ‘Alīkhān) is a village in Shizar Rural District, Bisotun District, Harsin County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 159, in 40 families.
