Title: Yadabad-e Olya
Aspects: {'Yadabad'}

Yadabad-e Olya (Persian: يادابادعليا‎, also Romanized as Yādābād-e Olyá) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 95, in 17 families.
