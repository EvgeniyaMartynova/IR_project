Title: Kabgian District
Aspects: {'Kabgian'}

Kabgian District (Persian: بخش کبگیان‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Dana County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 10,391, in 2,228 families.  The District has one city: Chitab.  The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Chenar Rural District and Kabgian Rural District.
