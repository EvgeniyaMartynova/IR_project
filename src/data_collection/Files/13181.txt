Title: Tilakiella
Aspects: {'T. indica'}

Tilakiella is a genus of fungi in the class Dothideomycetes. The relationship of this taxon to other taxa within the class is unknown (incertae sedis). A monotypic genus, it contains the single species Tilakiella indica.
