Title: No One Does It Better (album)
Aspects: {"I Don't Need Anyone"}

No One Does It Better is the debut studio album by Canadian pop group soulDecision. The album was recorded in 1999 and released in 2000. The album reached #1 on the Billboard Heatseekers chart. No One Does It Better has sold over one million copies worldwide.


== Track listing ==
All tracks are written by Trevor Guthrie except where noted.
