Title: Żabowo, Masovian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabowo'}

Żabowo [ʐaˈbɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zawidz, within Sierpc County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) east of Zawidz, 18 km (11 mi) south-east of Sierpc, and 100 km (62 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 150.
