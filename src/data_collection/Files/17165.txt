Title: Žabovřesky nad Ohří
Aspects: {'Žabovřesky'}

Žabovřesky nad Ohří is a village and municipality (obec) in Litoměřice District in the Ústí nad Labem Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 4.78 square kilometres (1.85 sq mi), and has a population of 216 (as at 31 December 2007).
Žabovřesky nad Ohří lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south of Litoměřice, 28 km (17 mi) south of Ústí nad Labem, and 44 km (27 mi) north-west of Prague.
