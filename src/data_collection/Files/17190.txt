Title: Zabrodzie, Ostrołęka County
Aspects: {'Zabrodzie'}

Zabrodzie [zaˈbrɔd͡ʑe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Olszewo-Borki, within Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north of Olszewo-Borki, 5 km (3 mi) north of Ostrołęka, and 106 km (66 mi) north of Warsaw.
