Title: WACC (AM)
Aspects: {'WACC'}

WACC (830 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Religious format. Licensed to Hialeah, Florida, United States.  The station is currently owned by Radio Peace Catholic Broadcasting, Inc.


== History ==
The station went on the air as WTIW on 1984-11-08.  on 1987-05-28, the station changed its call sign to WRFM, on 1995-04-03 to WXTO, on 1997-12-01 to the current WACC,
