Title: A Face in the Crowd (Tom Petty song)
Aspects: {'A Face in the Crowd'}

"A Face in the Crowd" is a song co-written and recorded by Tom Petty. It was released in February 1990 as the fourth single from his first solo album Full Moon Fever. It peaked at number 46 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart.


== Content ==
The narrator discusses falling in love with someone who used to be just "a face in the crowd."


== Music video ==
The music video was directed by Jesse Dylan and premiered in early 1990.


== Other performances ==
Josh Klinghoffer of the Red Hot Chili Peppers briefly performed the song in tribute to Petty, who died five days earlier, at the band's October 7, 2017 show in Texas.
