Title: WABO-FM
Aspects: {'WABO', 'Wabo'}

WABO-FM (105.5 FM) is a radio station  broadcasting a variety hits format. Licensed to Waynesboro, Mississippi, United States.  The station is currently owned by Bubba Diamond .


== History ==
The station went on the air as WABO-FM on 1979-02-01.  on 1979-03-20, the station changed its call sign to WABO,
