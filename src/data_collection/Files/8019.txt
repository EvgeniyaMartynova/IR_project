Title: The Other One (film)
Aspects: {"L'Autre"}

The Other One (French: L'Autre) is a 2008 French drama film directed by Patrick Mario Bernard and Pierre Trividic, based on the novel L'Occupation by Annie Ernaux. It was an entrant at the 65th Venice International Film Festival in 2008. For her performance, Dominique Blanc won the Volpi Cup for best actress.


== Cast ==

Cyril Gueï as Alex
Peter Bonke as Lars
