Title: Dysschema tricolor
Aspects: {'T. tricolor'}

Dysschema tricolor is a moth of the family Erebidae first described by Sulzer in 1776. It is found in Suriname, Bolivia, Paraguay and Brazil.


== Subspecies ==
Dysschema tricolor tricolor (Suriname, Bolivia, Paraguay)
Dysschema tricolor romani (Bryk, 1953) (Brazil)
