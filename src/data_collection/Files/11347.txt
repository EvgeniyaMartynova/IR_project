Title: Qaleh Tak, Kuhrang
Aspects: {'Qalatak'}

Qaleh Tak (Persian: قلعه تك‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Tak; also known as Ghal‘eh Tak and Qalātak) is a village in Shurab-e Tangazi Rural District, in the Central District of Kuhrang County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 111, in 21 families.
