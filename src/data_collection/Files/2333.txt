Title: Dipsas indica
Aspects: {'D. indica'}

Dipsas indica, also known as the Neo-tropical snail-eater, is a snake species found in South America. It feeds on slugs and snails, which the snake can extract from their shells using its slender jaw.There are two subspecies:
Dipsas indica indica Laurenti, 1768
Dipsas indica ecuadoriensis Peters, 1960
