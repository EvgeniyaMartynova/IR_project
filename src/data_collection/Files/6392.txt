Title: Joshua J. Johnson
Aspects: {'J. J. Johnson (disambiguation)'}

Joshua J. Johnson (born May 10, 1976) is an American sprinter who specialized in the 200 metres. Johnson is a world champion in the 4x100 m relay.


== Achievements ==


=== Personal bests ===
100 metres - 9.95 s (2002)
200 metres - 19.88 s (2001)
