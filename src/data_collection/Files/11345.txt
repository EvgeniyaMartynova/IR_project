Title: Qalandari, Khuzestan
Aspects: {'Qalandari, Iran'}

Qalandari (Persian: قلندري‎, also Romanized as Qalandarī) is a village in Asiab Rural District, in the Central District of Omidiyeh County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 167, in 31 families.55.909, 3.557
