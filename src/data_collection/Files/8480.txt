Title: Vexillum intermedium
Aspects: {'M. intermedia'}

Vexillum (Vexillum) intermedium, common name : the intermediate mitre,  is a species of small sea snail, marine gastropod mollusk in the family Costellariidae, the ribbed miters.


== Description ==


== Distribution ==
This species is distributed in the Red Sea, in the Indian Ocean along Madagascar and in the Pacific Ocean along Queensland, Australia.
