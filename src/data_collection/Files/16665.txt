Title: Siu Yam-yam
Aspects: {'Yam yam'}

Siu Yam-yam (Chinese: 邵音音; pinyin: Shào Yīnyīn, born 17 August 1950), born Ngai Siu-ngan (倪小雁), is a Hong Kong actress. She has been active in the Hong Kong film industry since 1970.
