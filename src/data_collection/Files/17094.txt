Title: Zabłocie, Puławy County
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Markuszów, within Puławy County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) west of Markuszów, 21 km (13 mi) east of Puławy, and 26 km (16 mi) north-west of the regional capital Lublin.
In 2009, the village had a population of 318.
