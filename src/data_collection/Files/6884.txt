Title: Gemerský Jablonec
Aspects: {'Jablonec'}

Gemerský Jablonec (Hungarian: Almágy) is a village and municipality in the Rimavská Sobota District of the Banská Bystrica Region of southern Slovakia.


== History ==
In historical records, the village was first mentioned in 1275 (Almag). In 1554 it hed to pay tributes to Turks. Until 1918 and between 1938 and 1945 it was part of Hungary.


== Genealogical resources ==
The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Banska Bystrica, Slovakia"
