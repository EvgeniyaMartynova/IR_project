Title: Cormocephalus elegans
Aspects: {'C. elegans elegans'}

Cormocephalus elegans is a species of centipede of the family Scolopendridae found in North Africa.The name Cormocephalus elegans zuluinus is a synonym for Cormocephalus westwoodi anceps.
