Title: Zabłocie, Gmina Kodeń
Aspects: {'Zabłocie'}

Zabłocie [zaˈbwɔt͡ɕe] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kodeń, within Biała Podlaska County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland, close to the border with Belarus. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-west of Kodeń, 37 km (23 mi) south-east of Biała Podlaska, and 96 km (60 mi) north-east of the regional capital Lublin.
