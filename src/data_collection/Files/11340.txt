Title: Qalandarabad, Roshtkhar
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad (Persian: قلندراباد‎, also Romanized as Qalandarābād) is a village in Roshtkhar Rural District, in the Central District of Roshtkhar County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 214, in 59 families.
