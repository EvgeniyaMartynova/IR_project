Title: Qalaychi, West Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Qalaychi'}

Qalaychi (Persian: قلايچي‎, also Romanized as Qalāychī) is a village in Behi-e Feyzolah Beygi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 101, in 23 families.
