Title: Statik KXNG
Aspects: {'I Hear Voices'}

Statik KXNG is the eponymous debut studio album by American hip hop duo Statik KXNG, consisting of Statik Selektah and KXNG Crooked. The album was released on February 12, 2016, through  Penalty Entertainment and Showoff Records. The album features only one guest appearance and that is Termanology who is in the group 1982 with Statik Selektah as well as being signed to Statik's label Showoff Records.


== Track listing ==
All tracks are produced by Statik Selektah.
