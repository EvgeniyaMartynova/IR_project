Title: Scirpophaga nepalensis
Aspects: {'S. nepalensis'}

Scirpophaga nepalensis is a moth in the family Crambidae. It was described by Lewvanich in 1981. It is found in Nepal.The wingspan is 16.5â€“18 mm for males and 16.5-18.5 mm for females. The forewings of the males are ochreous yellow, but darker along the venations. There is a fuscous spot at the lower angle of the cell. The hindwings are pale ochreous white. The forewings of the females are pale ochreous white with
a dark fuscous spot at the lower angle of the cell. The hindwings are pale ochreous white with an ochreous yellow anal tuft.
