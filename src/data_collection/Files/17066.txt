Title: Tolkyn Zabirova
Aspects: {'Zabirova'}

Tolkyn Zabirova (Cyrillic: Толкын Забирова, born 17 October 1970 in Ayagoz) is a singer from Ayagoz, Kazakhstan. Most of her songs are in Kazakh or Russian, though several exist in other languages.
