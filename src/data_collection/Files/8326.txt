Title: Discodoris concinna
Aspects: {'M. concinna'}

Discodoris concinna is a species of sea slug, a dorid nudibranch, shell-less marine gastropod mollusks in the family Discodorididae.This name is considered a "nomen dubium" 


== Distribution ==
This species occurs in European waters (as an introduced species) and in the Indian Ocean off Madagascar.
