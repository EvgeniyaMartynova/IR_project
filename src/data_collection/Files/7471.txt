== Production ==
Sandow M. M. A. Chinnappa Thevar was producing films under Devar Films featuring MGR in the lead role and K. V. Mahadevan composing the music. He floated Dhandayuthapani Films to produce films with a different team. This is the maiden production under the new banner.


== Soundtrack ==
Music was composed by P. S. Diwakar and the lyrics were penned by Kannadasan (Assistants: Panju Arunachalam and Rama. Muthaiah). Playback singers are T. M. Soundararajan and P. Susheela.
