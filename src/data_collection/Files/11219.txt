Title: Qadi Mahalleh, Amol
Aspects: {'Qadi Mahalleh'}

Qadi Mahalleh (Persian: قادي محله‎, also Romanized as Qādī Maḩalleh) is a village in Harazpey-ye Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Amol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 698, in 193 families.
