Title: Zaborze, Łosice County
Aspects: {'Zaborze'}

Zaborze [zaˈbɔʐɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Platerów, within Łosice County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) west of Platerów, 9 km (6 mi) north of Łosice, and 120 km (75 mi) east of Warsaw.
