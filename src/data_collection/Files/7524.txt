Title: Qabaqtəpə
Aspects: {'Kabakhtapa'}

Qabaqtəpə (also, Kabagtepe, Kabakhtapa, and Kabakhtepe) is a village and municipality in the Dashkasan Rayon of Azerbaijan.  It has a population of 788.  The municipality consists of the villages of Qabaqtəpə, Gəlinqaya, and Rəsullu.
