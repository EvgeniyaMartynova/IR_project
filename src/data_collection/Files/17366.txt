Title: Jafarabad, Borujerd
Aspects: {'Zafarabad'}

Jafarabad (Persian: جعفراباد‎, also Romanized as Ja‘farābād and Zafarābād) is a village in Bardesareh Rural District, Oshtorinan District, Borujerd County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,410, in 616 families.
