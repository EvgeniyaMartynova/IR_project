Title: Eurya japonica
Aspects: {'E. japonica'}

Eurya japonica, known as East Asian eurya, is a 1-3.5 m tall shrub in the Pentaphylacaceae family found in eastern China, Korea, and Japan. It is used as an ornamental plant.
