Title: Chaetocnema concinna
Aspects: {'C. concinna'}

Chaetocnema concinna, known generally as the brassy flea beetle, is a species of flea beetle in the family Chrysomelidae. Other common names include hop flea beetle, beet flea beetle, and brassy-toothed flea beetle. It is found in Europe & Northern Asia (excluding China) and North America.
