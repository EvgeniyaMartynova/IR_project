Title: Qaleh Kohneh, Kermanshah
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Kohneh'}

Qaleh Kohneh (Persian: قلعه كهنه‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Kohneh) is a village in Baladarband Rural District, in the Central District of Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 19, in 5 families.
