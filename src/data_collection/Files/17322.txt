Title: Zadębie, Pomeranian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zadębie'}

Zadębie [zaˈdɛmbjɛ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Rzeczenica, within Człuchów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) west of Rzeczenica, 31 km (19 mi) west of Człuchów, and 133 km (83 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The settlement has a population of 3.
