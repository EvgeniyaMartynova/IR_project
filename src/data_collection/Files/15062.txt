Tampa Padang Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Tampa Padang) (IATA: MJU, ICAO: WAFJ), is an airport near Mamuju, the capital city of the province of West Sulawesi on the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.


== Facilities ==
