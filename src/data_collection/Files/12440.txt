Title: Synaptocochlea concinna
Aspects: {'S. concinna'}

Synaptocochlea concinna is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Trochidae, the top snails.


== Description ==
The size of the shell varies between 3 mm and 8 mm.


== Distribution ==
This marine species occurs in the tropical Indo-West Pacific, Indo-Malaysia, Oceania and off Australia (Northern Territory, Queensland, Western Australia).
