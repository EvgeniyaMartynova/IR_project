Title: Záchlumí (Tachov District)
Aspects: {'Záchlumí'}

Záchlumí (German Eisenhuttel) is a village and municipality (obec) in Tachov District in the Plzeň Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 4.41 square kilometres (1.70 sq mi), and has a population of 408 (as at 3 July 2006).
Záchlumí lies approximately 25 kilometres (16 mi) east of Tachov, 31 km (19 mi) west of Plzeň, and 110 km (68 mi) west of Prague.
