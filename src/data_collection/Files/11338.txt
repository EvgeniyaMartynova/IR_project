Title: Qalandarabad-e Pain
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad-e Pain (Persian: قلندرابادپائين‎, also Romanized as Qalandarābād-e Pā’īn; also known as Qalandarābād) is a village in Soltanali Rural District, in the Central District of Gonbad-e Qabus County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 747, in 148 families.
