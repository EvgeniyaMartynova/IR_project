Title: Qalandarabad District
Aspects: {'Qalandarabad (disambiguation)'}

Qalandarabad District (Persian: بخش قلندرآباد‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Fariman County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 22,734, in 5,243 families.  The District has two cities: Qalandarabad and Sefid Sang.  The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Qalandarabad Rural District and Sefid Sang Rural District.
