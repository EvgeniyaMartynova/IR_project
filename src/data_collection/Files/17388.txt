Title: Zafarobod, Bukhara Region
Aspects: {'Zafarobod (disambiguation)'}

Zafarobod (Uzbek: Zafarobod/Зафаробод, Russian: Зафарабад) is an urban-type settlement in Bukhara Region, Uzbekistan. The town population in 2002 year was 5800 people.
