Title: Qaleh Qazi, Hamadan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Qazi'}

Qaleh Qazi (Persian: قلعه قاضي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Qāẕī) is a village in Hayaquq-e Nabi Rural District, in the Central District of Tuyserkan County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 693, in 171 families.
