Title: Jaani (film)
Aspects: {'Jaani'}

Jaani is a romantic comedy Punjabi film starring Gippy Grewal and Navneet Kaur Dhillon.


== Plot ==
It revolves around the love story of a taxi driver, played by Gippy, and a girl who has eloped from her home, played by Navneet. Supporting actors include Karamjit Anmol, BN Sharma, Anita Devgun and Seema KaushalRelease 
The film was shot in Fiji in June 2016 until 6 July 2016 and is expected to be released in October 2016.
