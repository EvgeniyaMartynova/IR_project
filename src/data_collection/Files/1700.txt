Title: Castanopsis concinna
Aspects: {'C. concinna'}

Castanopsis concinna is a species of plant in the Fagaceae family. It is a tree found in broad-leaved evergreen forests of southern Guangdong and Guangxi in China and in Hong Kong. It is under second-class national protection in China. It is threatened by habitat loss.
