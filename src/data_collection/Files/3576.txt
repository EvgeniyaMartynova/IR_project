Title: F.A.M.E. Tour (Maluma)
Aspects: {'F.A.M.E. Tour'}

The F.A.M.E. Tour was the second concert tour by Colombian singer Maluma, in support of his third studio album, F.A.M.E. (2018). The tour began on March 23, 2018, in Washington, D.C., and is scheduled to conclude on July 29, 2019, in Auckland, New Zealand .


== Setlist ==
This set list is representative of the show on March 25, 2018 in New York City. It is not representative of all concerts for the duration of the tour.
