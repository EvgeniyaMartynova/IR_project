Title: D. Srinivas (politician)
Aspects: {'D. Srinivas'}

Dharmapuri Srinivas is an Indian politician, He is the former President of  Andhra Pradesh Congress Party PCC and Ex-MLA of State Assembly. He was elected as mla for three times in the year 1989,1999 and 2004. he served as minister for higher education and intermediate education in rajashekhar reddy government by backstop him in 2009. Also worked with different portfolio's in state cabinet of Andhra pradesh. Played key role to bring back congress party government in 2004 and 2009 in A.p. Good reputation with high command in new delhi and known as key strategic  BC leader. He is popularly known as DS and dyanamic leader.Being true follower of High command.He is nephew of former finance minister ARGUL RAJARAM of A.P. state.Lost the elections in 2009, 2012 to  BJP and 2014 to TRS PARTY respectively.On 2 July 2015 he resigned for congress party.


== Early life ==
D. Srinivas was born in Nizamabad. He did his graduation from Nizam College, Hyderabad.


== Personal life ==
D. Srinivas is married and he has two sons. Elder son Sanjay is ex-Mayor of Nizamabad. His younger son Arvind is a businessman and a politician of Bharatiya Janata Party .
