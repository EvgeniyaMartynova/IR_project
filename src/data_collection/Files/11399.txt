Title: Qaleh Gelineh-ye Olya
Aspects: {'Qaleh Gelineh (disambiguation)'}

Qaleh Gelineh-ye Olya (Persian: قلعه گلينه عليا‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Gelīneh-ye ‘Olyā and Qal‘eh-ye Galīneh-ye ‘Olyā; also known as Qal‘eh Gelīneh-ye Bālā) is a village in Sanjabi Rural District, Kuzaran District, Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 79, in 20 families.
