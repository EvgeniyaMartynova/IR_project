Title: Qaleh Bardi, Lorestan
Aspects: {'Qaleh Bardi'}

Qaleh Bardi (Persian: قلعه بردي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh Bardī and Ghal‘eh Bardi; also known as Qalāvardīn and Qalāvardīr) is a village in Pachehlak-e Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Aligudarz County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 210, in 39 families.
