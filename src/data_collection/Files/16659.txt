Title: Yalveh-ye Olya
Aspects: {'Yalveh'}

Yalveh-ye Olya (Persian: يلوه عليا‎, also Romanized as Yalveh-ye ‘Olyā; also known as Fereydūn and Yalvāy-e Fereydūn) is a village in Baladarband Rural District, in the Central District of Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 60, in 15 families.
