Title: Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali, Fars
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali'}

Qaleh-ye Mohammad Ali (Persian: قلعه محمدعلي‎, also Romanized as Qal‘eh-ye Moḩammad ‘Alī and Qal‘eh Moḩammad ‘Alī; also known as Qal‘eh-ye Bolandī) is a village in Arudan Rural District, in the Central District of Mohr County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,234, in 238 families.
