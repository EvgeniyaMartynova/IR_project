Title: Die Wache
Aspects: {'Wache'}

Die Wache was a German weekly police procedural that was broadcast from 1994 until 2006 by RTL Television. Set in a (fictional) police station in Cologne, it was modelled on the British series The Bill.
The total series consisted of 245 episodes of 45 minutes during 12 seasons between 3 January 1994 and 8 June 2006.
