Title: Żabin, Masovian Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Zabin'}

Żabin [ˈʐabin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Goworowo, within Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Goworowo, 14 km (9 mi) south of Ostrołęka, and 91 km (57 mi) north-east of Warsaw.
