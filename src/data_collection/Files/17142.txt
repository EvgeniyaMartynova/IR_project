Title: Zaborowo, Gmina Kozłowo
Aspects: {'Zaborowo'}

Zaborowo [zabɔˈrɔvɔ] (German: Saberau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kozłowo, within Nidzica County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Nidzica and 57 km (35 mi) south of the regional capital Olsztyn.
The village has a population of 150.


== Notable residents ==
Albrecht Konrad Finck von Finckenstein (1660–1735), tutor of Frederick II of Prussia
