Title: Isle of Man FA Cup
Aspects: {'FA Cup (disambiguation)'}

Isle of Man FA Cup is the foremost football cup competition for teams playing on the Isle of Man. The tournament, founded in 1889, features the twenty six teams from the Isle of Man Premier League and Division 2. The tournament is overseen by the Isle of Man Football Association.
Peel are the current champions, having beaten St Marys in the 2019 final. 
Peel are the most successful club, with 32 titles.
