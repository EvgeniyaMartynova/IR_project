Title: Cinnamon red bat
Aspects: {'L. poeppigii'}

The cinnamon red bat (Lasiurus varius) is a species of bat from the family Vespertilionidae. It is often listed as a synonym of the eastern red bat or the desert red bat, but is distinct.


== Distribution and conservation ==
The cinnamon red bat lives in southern Argentina and Chile. There is no information about its population, but due to its large distribution it is presumed to have a large population and is therefore considered Least Concern.
