Title: Sabri Jaballah
Aspects: {'Jaballah'}

Sabri Jaballah (Arabic: صبري جاب الله‎) (born 28 June 1973) is a Tunisian footballer.He played for a few clubs, including Club Africain, CS Sfaxien and Stade Tunisien.
He played for the Tunisia national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup.
