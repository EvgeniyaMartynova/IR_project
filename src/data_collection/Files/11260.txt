Title: Qaflankuh-e Gharbi Rural District
Aspects: {'Qaflankuh'}

Qaflankuh-e Gharbi Rural District (Persian: دهستان قافلانكوه غربي‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 12,166, in 2,990 families. The rural district has 16 villages.
