Title: Faranah Airport
Aspects: {'FAA (disambiguation)'}

Faranah Airport (IATA: FAA, ICAO: GUFH) is an airport serving the city of Faranah in Guinea. The airport is 2 kilometres (1.2 mi) west of the city, across the Niger River.
The Faranah VOR/DME (Ident: FRH) and Faranah non-directional beacon (Ident: FH) are located 1.1 nautical miles (2.0 km) north-northeast of the airport.
