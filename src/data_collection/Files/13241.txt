Title: Oxymeris maculata
Aspects: {'T. maculata'}

Oxymeris maculata, common name the marlinspike auger,  is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Terebridae, the auger snails.


== Distribution ==
This species occurs in the Red Sea and in the Indian Ocean off Aldabra, Chagos, Madagascar, the Mascarene Basin, Mauritius and Tanzania; in the Pacific Ocean off Mexico.
