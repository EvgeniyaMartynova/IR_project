Title: Qader Marz, Kurdistan
Aspects: {'Qader Marz'}

Qader Marz (Persian: قادرمرز‎, also Romanized as Qāder Marz; also known as Qāder Maz and Qadīr Maz) is a village in Yeylan-e Jonubi Rural District, Bolbanabad District, Dehgolan County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 390, in 93 families.
