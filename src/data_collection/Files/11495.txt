Title: Kallehgan, Kerman
Aspects: {'Qaleh-ye Khan'}

Kallehgan (Persian: كله گان‎, also Romanized as Kallehgān; also known as Kalagan and Qal‘eh Khān) is a village in Rameshk Rural District, Chah Dadkhoda District, Qaleh Ganj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 41, in 11 families.
