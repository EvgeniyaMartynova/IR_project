Title: Żabno, Lublin Voivodeship
Aspects: {'Żabno (disambiguation)'}

Żabno [ˈʐabnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Turobin, within Biłgoraj County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of Turobin, 34 km (21 mi) north of Biłgoraj, and 47 km (29 mi) south of the regional capital Lublin.
The village has a population of 536.
