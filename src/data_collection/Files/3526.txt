Title: Fluviopupa gracilis
Aspects: {'F. gracilis'}

Fluviopupa gracilis is a species of very small freshwater snails that have an operculum, aquatic gastropod mollusks in the family Tateidae.


== Distribution ==
This species is endemic to Australia.
