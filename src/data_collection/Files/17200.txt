Title: Ząbrsko Dolne
Aspects: {'Ząbrsko'}

Ząbrsko Dolne [ˈzɔmbrskɔ ˈdɔlnɛ] (German: Nieder Sommerkau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przywidz, within Gdańsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north of Przywidz, 20 km (12 mi) west of Pruszcz Gdański, and 24 km (15 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 35.
