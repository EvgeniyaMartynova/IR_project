Title: R. T. Greer and Company Root and Herb Warehouse
Aspects: {'R. T. Greer and Company'}

R. T. Greer and Company Root and Herb Warehouse is a historic warehouse located near Todd, Ashe County, North Carolina.  It was built about 1918, and is a two-story, rectangular, timber framed commercial building.  It is sheathed in corrugated metal sheet siding and rests on a brick pier foundation.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2003.
