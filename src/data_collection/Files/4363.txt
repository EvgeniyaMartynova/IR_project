Title: Gagea lutea
Aspects: {'G. lutea'}

Gagea lutea, the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a Eurasian flowering plant species in the family Liliaceae. It is widespread in central Europe with scattered populations in Great Britain, Spain and Norway to  Siberia and Japan.Gagea lutea is a bulb-forming perennial herb with lanceolate leaves and yellow flowers.
