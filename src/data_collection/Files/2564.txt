Title: Musée d'Orsay station
Aspects: {"D'Orsay (disambiguation)"}

Musée d'Orsay is a station in line C of the Paris Region's Réseau express régional (RER) rapid transit system, named after the Musée d'Orsay. It is situated in the 7th arrondissement of Paris. It was one of several stations attacked during the 1995 Paris Métro and RER bombings. Its precursor the Gare d'Orsay is the world's first electrified train station.


== Adjacent stations ==
Assemblée Nationale and Solférino on Paris Métro Line 12 are both within walking distance.
