Title: Chryseobacterium bovis
Aspects: {'C. bovis'}

Chryseobacterium bovis  is a Gram-negative, rod-shaped, aerobic and non-motile bacteria from the genus of Chryseobacterium which has been isolated from raw cow milk in Israel.
