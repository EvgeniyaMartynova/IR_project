Title: Ericameria crispa
Aspects: {'E. crispa'}

Ericameria crispa (crisped goldenbush) is a rare North American species of flowering plants in the daisy family. It has been found only on mountain slopes in the state of Utah in the western United States.Ericameria crispa is a branching shrub up to 40 cm (16 inches) tall.
