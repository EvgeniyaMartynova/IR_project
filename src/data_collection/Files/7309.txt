Title: Dialog K45
Aspects: {'K45'}

The Dialog K45 is a dual-SIM slate format smartphone designed, developed in China by Innos and marketed in Sri Lanka by Dialog Axiata that runs the Android operating system. It has a 1.2 GHz ARM Cortex-A5 CPU with 768 MB LPDDR1 SD-RAM.  This handset has a 540x960 pixels 4.5-inch IPS QHD Capacitive display.  This device has a 4 GB internal storage and also supports micro SD card up to 32 GB.  Compatible networks for this device are GPRS, EDGE and 3G. It has Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and USB connectivity. This device can also be used as a Wi-Fi hotspot. The Dialog K45 has an 8-megapixel rear camera with dual LED flash and a 2-megapixel front-facing camera for video calls. The device was launched with Android 4.0.4 Ice Cream Sandwich.
