Title: Qelichi
Aspects: {'Qaleh Cheh'}

Qelichi (Persian: قليچي‎, also Romanized as Qelīchī; also known as Qal‘ehcheh) is a village in Balghelu Rural District, in the Central District of Ardabil County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2016 census, its population was 73, in 37 families.
