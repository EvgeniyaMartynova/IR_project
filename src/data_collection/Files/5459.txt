Title: I Am the Greatest: The Adventures of Muhammad Ali
Aspects: {'I Am the Greatest'}

I Am the Greatest: The Adventures of Muhammad Ali is an animated series featuring boxer Muhammad Ali, who starred as his own voice. The series was broadcast Saturday mornings on NBC in the fall of 1977, but was cancelled after just 13 episodes.
