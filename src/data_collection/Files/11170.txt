Title: Qabaq Tappeh, East Azerbaijan
Aspects: {'Qabaq Tappeh'}

Qabaq Tappeh (Persian: قباق تپه‎, also Romanized as Qābāq Tappeh and Qabāq Tappeh) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 509, in 95 families.
